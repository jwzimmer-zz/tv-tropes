Paquin is known as the "gypsy planet." Carnivals, sideshows and theatres abound on this planet. This also seems to be the home of every con-artist and swindler in the 'Verse. The unique atmosphere of Paquin produces some of the most beautiful sunsets around. The skies fill with colors ranging from purple to blue, to red to orange. These sunsets provide a backdrop for the grand opera house, which is set on a shore overlooking a large ocean. The opera house brought in many theater lovers from all around the 'Verse, which inspired others to take advantage of this newfound trade and more theatres were built. Paquin rivals Sihnon in terms of culture and it has become the artistic center of the Border Worlds. There is however, a darker side of Paquin. Despite the carnivals and sideshows that are there to provide honest entertainment for the folks of Paquin, there are those carnies who take advantage of their patrons or use their bright lights as a cover for darker activities.
