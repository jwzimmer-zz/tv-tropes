No Title Anime

This was an animated film, almost certainly Chinese, longer than a typical TV episode but shorter than feature length. I saw it on British TV, probably some time in the 1980s.

In the opening scene, a crop of cabbages is sprayed with pesticide. A caterpillar (her species is referred to as "cabbage worm" in the English dub) eats one of the cabbages and becomes ill. She travels to the "Hospital for Useful Insects", but she is considered a pest, so she has to shroud herself in leaves as a disguise in order to gain treatment. The doctor is a crow. She overhears an insect in a neighbouring hospital bed mentioning that he likes to eat cabbage worms, and she hides deeper in her disguise.

By the time Doctor Crow and the other insects discover what she really is, she has pupated. Doctor Crow tells the insects to watch the pupa closely. When she emerges as a butterfly, Doctor Crow and the other insects accuse here of being a cabbage worm, which she denies, claiming that she is indeed a useful insect. In the final scene, she attempts to fly away, but she becomes entangled in a spider web, and Doctor Crow and the other insects laugh at her. This is clearly intended as a happy ending.
