Allison Pregler (born December 6, 1989) is an female reviewer who takes on Z-grade movies, usually in the horror or martial arts genre; a roughly similar beat to The Cinema Snob, but tending towards dumb action flicks and a morbid enjoyment of them. She began reviewing in late 2009 and after gaining some notoriety on their forums (under her old handle, Obscurus Lupa), she became a member of the Channel Awesome family. She remained on the site until early 2015, when Obscurus Lupa Presents was retooled into Movie Nights.

  • Obscurus Lupa Presents: Z-movies from the early 70's to the modern day. A "Z" movie for Allison is something that doesn't have the capability or the budget to do something incredible. Not all the movies are bad. Some are terrible.
  • Commercial Dubs: Allison riffs on TV spots and film trailers from the 90's in her inimitable style.
  • Manic Episodes: Focuses on individual episodes from TV shows, usually of the Big Lipped Alligatorinvoked variety. As with her movie reviews, Allison points out inherent issues with the writing, acting and production. More recently, Allison has taken to reviewing the entire run of series that jumped the shark (She-Wolf of London), were cut down in their prime (Beauty and the Beast, Tales of the Gold Monkey), or didn't live up to their potential (Charmed).
      Advertisement:
    • Baywatching: A trek through America's gift to the world, Baywatch! Allison has vowed to review all 242 grueling episodes.
  • Radu Reviews: Christian and Edutainment films for kids reviewed by everybody's favorite vampire, Radu Vladislav! Radu seems to be under the impression that these films are macabre and edgy.
  • Movie Nights: Similar to Obscurus Lupa Presents but with the format changed to a discussion panel, either with Allison presenting solo or teaming up with a guest host (such as frequent collaborator Phelous). The movies chosen are still either Z-grade genre films or notorious mainstream flops that flew under the radar. Unlike OLP, Allison does not perform as a character.

She also currently runs two podcasts through her channel, both covering the Charmed franchise and both co-hosted with Phelous.

  • Charmed Hard with a Vengeance: Allison and Phelous recap and talk about each episode of the 2018 reboot of Charmed as it airs.
  • Charmed Rewind: When the reboot is on breaks, Allison and Phelous dip into episodes of the original show as selected by voters from her Patreon.

After a controversial departure from Channel Awesome, Allison officially announced on January 20th, 2015, that she'd be revamping OLP into an impromptu review show called Movie Nights, with an appropriately-shlocky elevator jazz theme.

Her videos can be seen here and here. Can also be found at her Twitter account and Tumblr account.

Can't get enough Allison? You can see her kicking ass as Interpol ninja Cheetah Lee in Ninja the Mission Force. She also appeared in Brad Jones' movie Jesus, Bro! and frequently guest-starred on his series Midnight Screenings, typically reviewing terrible childrens' films or RiffTrax live shows, as well as the occasional adult comedy (including, of course, Baywatch (2017)).
