Yikes! Oh! It's y-you! Sorry I f-f-freaked out like that! You just s-startled me! Well anyway, this t-trope is n-not to be confused with n-n-n-nightmare fuel, which is a popular page that describes various audience reactions, which also scares me! It's not tropes used in FEAR, though a few below are. It's not The Fear Index, even though this is one.

Compare Horror Tropes, Anxiety Tropes, and Weakness Tropes. Those really make me feel uneasy! Contrast Bravery Tropes. Oh, how I wish I were one of those! All these tropes are really scary! Let's hurry up and get this over with! I'm not sure how long I could sit through these!
