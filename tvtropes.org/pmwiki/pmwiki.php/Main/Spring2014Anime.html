Spring 2014 Anime

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

This chart is a list of TV anime series that aired between April and June of 2014, along with their Laconic descriptions.

See Winter 2014 Anime for the previous season. See Summer 2014 Anime for next season.

Here is this season's discussion thread.

TV Series:

  • Akuma no Riddle (Riddle Story of Devil)
    (PV)
    Airing from April 3 for 13 episodes

    A class at a girls' boarding school consists solely of a regular girl and assassins targeting her. One of the assassins starts to develop feelings for her. Not a comedy.
Advertisement:
  • Atelier Escha & Logy: Alchemists of the Dusk Sky
    (PV)
    Airing from April 10

    In a World... reaching the end of the current cycle, a country is using alchemical technology to prepare to survive. The story follows a city boy and rural girl as they help.
  • Baby Steps
    Airing from April 6 for 25 episodes

    A studious boy decides to take up tennis after meeting a girl who's devoted to it.
  • Black Bullet
    (PV)
    Airing from April 8

    In a World... where humanity has been largely defeated by viral parasites called 'Gastrea', we follow a young man who fights them with a mysterious power.
  • Blade & Soul
    (PV)
    Airing from April 3 for 12 episodes

    An adaptation of the MMORPG of the same name. When the Palum Empire murders her master and frames her for it, an assassin seeks revenge, fighting bounty hunters and agents of the Empire along the way. And yes, it does feature scantily clad women with weapons.
Advertisement:

  • The Kawai Complex Guide To Manors And Hostel Behavior (We Are All From Dormitory Kawainote Homophonous with "we are all pitiful")
    Airing from April 3

    A boy moves into a hall filled with weird perverts and a girl he likes.
  • Break Blade
    Airing from April 6

    Fantasy mecha series, previously adapted as a movie series.
  • Brynhildr in the Darkness (Gokukoku no Brynhildr)
    (PV)
    Airing from April 6 for 13 episodes

    A sci-fantasy series about a boy helping witches on the run from a research lab. Much Gorn ensues. Written and animated by the same staff who did Elfen Lied.
  • Captain Earth
    (PV)
    Airing from April 5

    A Super Robot anime from the folks who brought us Star Driver. Not to be confused with Captain Planet.
  • Cardfight!! Vanguard Legion Mate-Hen
    Airing from March 9

    The fourth season of Cardfight!! Vanguard anime. Aichi and Kai join forces against an enormous foe.
Advertisement:
  • Coffin Princess Chaika (Hitsugi No Chaika)
    (PV)
    Airing from April 9

    In a fantasy world, a young man is now retired after a war has ended. He ends up following a sorceress that carries a coffin in hopes of finding a new meaning to his life.
  • The Comic Artist and His Assistants
    (PV)
    Airing from April 7

    A gag comedy about a perverted mangaka and his female assistant.
  • Daimidaler the Sound Robot
    (PV)
    Airing from April 5

    A highschool boy fights against the penguin empire with the help of a giant robot.
  • Date A Live II
    Airing from April 11 for 10 episodes

    2nd season. Expect more harem hijinks.
  • Dragon Ball Kai Buu saga
    Airing from April 6

    The completion of the edited version of the Dragon Ball anime.
  • Dragon Collection
    Airing from April 6

    An adaptation of a TCG by Konami of the same name.
  • Duel Masters VS
    Airing from April 5

    Another season of the Duel Masters anime adaptation.
  • Fairy Tail
    Airing from April 5

    A continuation of the popular fighting series.
  • Fuuun Ishin Dai Shogun
    (PV)
    Airing from April 9

    An action romance set in a world where Japan remained secluded by driving off foreigners with ancient giant robots.
  • Gigant Shooter Tsukasa
    Airing from April 1

    A comedy featuring the eponymous fictional card game.
  • Haikyuu!!
    (PV)
    Airing from April 6

    A Sports Story featuring volleyball where two former rivals wind up on the same team.
  • Hero Bank
    Airing from April 7

    An elementary school boy finds himself suddenly saddled with a 10 billion yen debt, and tries to pay it off by playing a cyber-sport.
  • If Her Flag Breaks (Kanojo ga Flag o Oraretara)
    (PV)
    Airing from April 6

    Gaworare for short. A high school boy is able to see invisible flags(as in Dating Sim flags) and has tried to avoid getting close to others until he is forced not to.
  • Inugami-san to Nekoyama-san
    (PV)
    Airing from April 3 for 12 5-minute episodes

    A yuri 4koma about a catlike girl and a doglike girl.
  • The Irregular at Magic High School (Mahouka Koukou no Rettousei)
    (PV)
    Airing from April 5 for 26 episodes

    At a magic high school, a boy is at the bottom of his class and his sister is at the top.
  • Is the Order a Rabbit?? (Gochuumon wa Usagi Desu ka?)
    (PV)
    Airing from April 10

    GochiUsa for short. The requisite Slice of Life moe yonkoma adaptation, this time set at a bunny cafe.
  • Jo Jos Bizarre Adventure Stardust Crusaders
    (PV)
    Airing from April 4

    An adaptation of the third part of the celebrated manga, introducing stands and most of the best-known characters.
  • Kamigami no Asobi ~Ludere deorum~
    (PV)
    Airing from April 5 for 12 episodes

    At a school for gods, a human girl is ordered to teach (male) gods how to love.
  • The Kindaichi Case Files R
    (CM)
    Airing from April 5 for 26 episodes

    A new anime for the famous mystery series.
  • Kin'iro no Corda Blue Sky
    Airing from April 5

    An adaptation of the third game in the Otome Game series.
  • Knights of Sidonia
    (PV)
    Airing from April 10

    In the distant future, a colony ship fights for survival against strange, mutating aliens. By the author of Blame!
  • Lady Jewelpet
    Airing from April 5, for 52 episodes.

    The sixth season of Jewelpet focuses on a girl named Momona, who is brought to the Jewel Palace and partnered with Ruby in order to become a Petit Lady, a candidate for the title of Lady Jewel.
  • Love Live! Season 2
    (PV)
    Airing from April 6

    2nd season of the Winter 2013 Anime.
  • M3: The Dark Metal
    (PV)
    Airing from April 21

    In the near future, part of Tokyo has been swallowed by blackness and is now spawning monsters. The story follows eight youths on a mission to survey this area.
  • Mahou Shoujo Taisen Magica Wars (Magical Girl War)
    Airing from April 8 for 26 four-minute episode

    Part of an original project that presumably involves magical girls fighting. Nothing to do with Mahou Shoujo Madoka Magica.
  • Majin Bone
    Airing from April 1

    An new action anime/game project. Little plot details currently exist.
  • Mekaku City Actors
    Airing from April 12 for 12 episodes

    Part of Kagerou Project, a story about various people with special abilities related to their eyes. Animated by Studio Shaft.
  • Mushishi Zoku-shou
    Airing from April 4

    A sequel to the fall 2005 fantasy series. Running for 26 episodes split between the Spring and Fall seasons.
  • No Game No Life
    (PV)
    Airing from April 9

    A brother and sister pair of NEET gamers are summoned to a fantasy world run by games.
  • Abarenbou Rikishi!! Matsutarou
    Airing from April 6

    It's about sumo.
  • One Week Friends
    (PV)
    Airing from April 7

    A boy decides to become friends with a girl who can only retain memories for a week.
  • Oreca Battle
    Airing from April 6

    An adaptation of a TCG by Konami of the same name.
  • Ping Pong
    (CM)
    Airing from April 10 for 11 episodes

    It's about a pair of friends in a ping pong club.
  • Pretty Rhythm: All Star Selection
    Airing from April 5

    Fourth and final season of the Pretty Rhythm series. Laala, a newbie is under training from the protagonists of the previous three series.
  • Puchimas Petit Idolmaster
    Airing from April 1

    2nd season of the Winter 2013 Anime with super deformed version of the 765 Pro Idols.
  • Ryuugajou Nanana no Maizoukin
    (PV)
    Airing from April 10 for 11 episodes

    A boy moves into an apartment haunted by a ghost girl and ends up searching for her treasure.
  • Seikoku no Dragonar
    (PV)
    Airing from April 5

    A boy at a school for dragon riders ends up with a beautiful girl as his dragon.
  • selector infected WIXOSS
    (PV)
    Airing from April 3 for 12 episodes

    Girls fight a battle to have their wish granted, using special cards that have wills of their own.
  • Keroro
    (PV)
    Airing from March 22 as a series of shorts

    More alien frogs, this time animated in Flash.
  • Soul Eater Not!
    (PV)
    Airing from April 8

    A prequel to Soul Eater, focusing on a group of girls in Shibusen's non-fighting Not class.
  • The World is Still Beautiful (Soredemo Sekai Wa Utsukushii)
    (PV)
    Airing from April 5

    A princess of the Rain Dukedom is married off to the king of the Sun Country, who had previously taken over the world.
  • Yu-Gi-Oh! ARC-V
    Airing from April 6

    The fifth series of the trading card anime.
