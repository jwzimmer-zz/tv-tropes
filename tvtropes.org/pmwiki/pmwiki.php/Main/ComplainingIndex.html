Complaining Index

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

Ugh! I hate this index! It's full of tropes involving characters complaining about certain things or people! Why does it even exist anyway?!

Ugh! These tropes really get on my nerves!:

  • Accentuate the Negative: This index is terrible! It's just a list of pointless tropes about complaining! It sucks!
  • All of the Other Reindeer: All of the unassimilated need to be identical to us or die!
  • Appeal to Worse Problems: How dare you complain about your First World Problems, you ungrateful spoiled brat! Don't you know there are other people who have it worse than you?!
    • Advertisement:
  • Blamed for Being Railroaded: I hate that stupid character for what they do in the plot!
  • Bluenose Bowdlerizer: Oh no! I saw a bad word or that stupid porny thingamajiggy! Time to cut it all out!
  • Bratty Food Demand: It's been seven and a half minutes, I want my food NOW! HURRY UP!
  • Bratty Teenage Daughter: I'm thirteen years old and I hate how my parents are always treating me like a child and scolding me for being a brat!
  • Can't Take Criticism: Why does everyone criticize me?! My feelings are hurt!
  • City Mouse: Why am I out in the middle of this quiet and empty nowhere? Where's the nearest shop?
  • Commander Contrarian: I'm sick of people who always contradict me, even if I'm right!
  • The Complainer Is Always Wrong: I hate it when nobody believes anything I tell them! They always say I'm wrong! I'm never right!
    • Advertisement:
  • Complaining about Complaining: Would you people quit complaining?! It's getting annoying!
  • Complaining About People Not Liking The Show: You have absolutely no good taste in shows!
  • Complaining About Rescues They Don't Like: I didn't ask to be rescued!
  • Complaining About Shows You Don't Like: I watched the show, it sucks!
  • Complaining About Shows You Don't Watch: I never watched that show, but I still hate it!
  • Complaining About Things You Haven't Paid For: I hate those shoes! They look ugly! I'm glad I didn't buy them!
  • Country Mouse: What's with all that noise? I hate being crammed in with a bunch of people and buildings!
  • Cultural Posturing: Look at how superior my culture is, suck it up!
  • Die for Our Ship: You interfered with the OTP? Die now!
  • Does Not Like Magic: Ugh! Stupid magic, always taking the easy way out!
    • Advertisement:
  • Dreaded Kids' Table: I'm twelve, I should be allowed to sit with the adults during Thanksgiving!
  • Emo Teen: I'm a teen and my life is hell! I hate it! I want to die!
  • Enraged by Idiocy: Everyone around me is an idiot! I'm so pissed! Aaaauuuggh!
  • Entitled Bastard: I'm tired of that hero! I still expect benefits from him, though!
  • Fandom Heresy: How dare you like something out of our beliefs?!
  • Fan Hater: I hate those people who're fans of something I hate!
  • The Farmer and the Viper: You saved my life? You must die!
  • First World Problems: They took my favorite sandwich off the menu! I hate my life!
  • Fish out of Temporal Water: I hate that I've been sent too far in the future! I wanna go back to my timeline!
  • Fish out of Water: Ugh! It's very irritating how I'm in a place I don't fit in, especially where it's dry! Arrrgh!
  • Frivolous Lawsuit: You stepped on my foot! I'll sue you!
  • Grumpy Bear: I hate living in a world full of candy and cupcakes!
  • Grumpy Old Man: Grr! Stupid youngins!
  • "I Hate" Song: I hate this thing so much, I'm going to sing a song about it!
  • Jewish Complaining: I'm Jewish! Why does everything have to go wrong for me?!
  • Kids Hate Grownups' Conversations: I'm tired of these adults taking forever to end a conversation!
  • Klingon Scientists Get No Respect: I don't care how good you think that guy is doing, why can't he be just like us?
  • Knight in Sour Armor: I hate being the good guy! It sucks! But I'm still gonna do my job anyways. Ugh!
  • Minor Insult Meltdown: Someone threw a petty insult my way? How dare they?!
  • The Misophonic: Would someone stop making these irritating sounds?! I hate them!
  • Misplaced Nationalism: Your country sucks big time, they don't have all of those developments of mine!
  • Misery Poker: Your life sucks? Please! Mine's worse than yours!
  • Moral Guardians: We work together to ban all dirtiness in this media!
  • Nobody Ever Complained Before: How dare you object to our perfectly-reputed quirk!
  • Political Correctness Gone Mad: How dare you offend them! Take that down now!
  • Quit Your Whining: Good lord, your whining's getting on my nerves!
  • Rant-Inducing Slight: Aargh! I was just feeling calm about all the major predicaments happening to me ... until this minor incident! Now I'm really upset!
  • Real Women Don't Wear Dresses: Stop portraying women as GirlyGirls!
  • "The Reason You Suck" Speech: You want to know why I hate you? It's because you're a fat, lazy, jobless, weak, stupid, naive, immature, ugly, whiny, cowardly, pathetic loser who wastes their life on the internet and video games! And you never flush the toilet!
  • Refuse to Rescue the Disliked: I don't like saving anyone I hate!
  • The Resenter: Why does that guy always have to be better than me?!
  • Scrub: Your tactics are cheap and unfair! You're cheating!
  • Ship-to-Ship Combat: Our ship is the One True Pairing, and yours isn't!
  • Single-Issue Wonk: Even if others intersect, this one subject is still too important to stop ranting about!
  • Skewed Priorities: I don't care about my life being threatened by a hurricane! My breakfast is ruined!
  • Smug Straight Edge: Drugs Are Bad, and anyone who takes them is automatically bad!
  • Sore Loser: Agh! This isn't supposed to happen! I was supposed to win! You're a cheater! A freakin' cheater!
  • Sour Prudes: I hate people who express their sexual desires instead of being pure like us! It's insulting!
  • Spoiled Brat: No fair! I never get what I want!
    • Royal Brat: Even when I'm royalty, I still don't get what I want! No fair! I'm a royal child! I deserve everything!
  • Stop Being Stereotypical: Quit making our group look bad!
  • "Stop Having Fun" Guys: Playing is for winning and the competition, not fun!
  • Surrounded by Idiots: I live in a city full of morons!
  • There Should Be a Law: It should be illegal and disgraceful because it makes me and certain people uncomfortable and hurt our feelings! Waah!
  • Torches and Pitchforks: THEY TOOK OUR FOOD!!!!
  • Trauma Button: I had a terrible upbringing. Now stop laughing about it!
  • Ungrateful Bastard: I had my life saved. But I have nothing to say in return! So just get away from me already!
  • Ungrateful Townsfolk: We don't care about how much good you've done to us! We're still going to chase you out of town!
  • Unpleasable Fanbase: This new game sucks! I hate it! The makers of this awful series don't know how to please us fans!
  • Wangst: Everyone keeps ruining my life! I never get anything I want! I deserve better! You people don't understand me! Nobody understands me! WHAT??!!! WHAT DO YOU MEAN I'M TOO MELODRAMATIC AND ANNOYING YOU???!!!!! WAAAAHAAAAAAAAAHHHHHH???!!!
  • What Have You Done for Me Lately?: Absolutely nothing! Why don't you return the favor for what I've done for you already?
  • What the Hell, Hero?: Aren't you supposed to be the hero?! Why would you do something like that?!
  • When I Was Your Age...:...we never got to have those nice things as you! You youngins got it so easy!
  • While You Were in Diapers:...we were making lives of ourselves! So don't resent our generation!
  • Why Couldn't You Be Different?: I expected my newborn to be a girl! Why is it a boy?!
  • Yank the Dog's Chain: Ugh! I finally have something good happen to me for once, and it's ruined again!
Ugh! Why did someone waste their time making this index? It is freakin' pointless!
