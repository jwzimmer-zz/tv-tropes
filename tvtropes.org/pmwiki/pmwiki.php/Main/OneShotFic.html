One-Shot Fic

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

A one-shot (or oneshot) is defined as a fanfic that has only one chapter. It can be one very long chapter or one very short chapter (sometimes referred to as a "drabble", though other people will tell you that's a misnomernote According to the traditional definition, a drabble must be exactly 100 words), but as long as it's only one chapter then it counts. One-shots can also have sequel fics and still be considered one-shots.

Advertisement:

One-shots will also be examples of either Flash Fiction, Novelette, Novella, or Short Story.

Compare to Completed Fic for multi-chapter stories that have finished.
