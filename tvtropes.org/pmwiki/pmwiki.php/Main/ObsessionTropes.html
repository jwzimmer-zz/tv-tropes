An index of Obsession Tropes. These tropes describe the varying ways a character cannot leave something alone. Whether it be spending too much time, money, effort, or patience; the character will not stop until they are either dead, imprisoned, or possibly even finished. And maybe not even then.

Compare with Body Image Tropes, Prejudice Tropes, Selfishness Tropes & This Is Your Index on Drugs. Opposite of Apathy Index.
