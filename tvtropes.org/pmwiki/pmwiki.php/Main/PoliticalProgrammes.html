Political Programmes

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Quotes
  • Create New

Shows about politics, whether serious or humorous.

Examples

Sunday Morning Talk Shows

  • Meet the Press (debuted in 1945 on radio; 1947 on television) on NBC (and rebroadcasts on MSNBC)
  • Face the Nation (1954) on CBS
  • Issues and Answers (1960 - 1981) on ABC
  • This Week (1981) on ABC (replaced Issues And Answers)
  • Late Edition (1993) on CNN
  • Fox News Sunday (1996) on Fox (and rebroadcasts on Fox News)
  • The Andrew Marr Show on the BBC
    • Sunday Politics and the Monday-Friday Daily Politics on the BBC.

Pundit Shows (Television)

  • The 700 Club
  • Anderson Cooper 360° (also does current events)
  • ArmComedy (News Parody hosted by the Armenian equivalents of Jon Stewart)
  • Cenk Uygur
  • The Colbert Report (The Parody of the typical conservative pundit)
  • Countdown with Keith Olbermann
  • The Daily Show (Jon Stewart's not technically a pundit, per se, as he's a comedian, but he's still influential so he counts)
    • The Daily Show with Jon Stewart
    • The Daily Show with Trevor Noah
  • Democracy Now!
  • The Ed Show
  • Firing Line: ran from 1962 to 1999, hosted by National Review founder William F. Buckley Jr.. A reboot was created in 2018, hosted by Margaret Hoover.
  • Fox and Friends
  • Full Frontal with Samantha Bee
  • Glenn Beck
  • The Greg Gutfeld Show
  • Hannity (formerly Hannity and Colmes)
  • Hardball with Chris Matthews
  • The Jim Jefferies Show
  • Larry King Live
  • Last Week Tonight with John Oliver (in the same vein as Stewart)
  • Lou Dobbs Tonight
  • Morning Joe
  • Nancy Grace
  • The Nightly Show (panel show in the same vein as Stewart)
  • On the Record with Greta Van Susteren
  • The Opposition with Jordan Klepper (The Parody of "alternative" conservative media)
  • The O'Reilly Factor
  • Patriot Act with Hasan Minhaj
  • Piers Morgan Tonight
  • The Rachel Maddow Show
  • Real Time with Bill Maher
  • Red Eye with Greg Gutfeld
  • Special Report with Bret Baier
  • The View

  • Bryan Fischer
  • Neal Boortz
  • The Mark Levin Show
  • Rush Limbaugh
  • The Stephanie Miller Show

Political Satire Shows

  • Not the Nine O'Clock News
  • Not Necessarily the News
  • The Revolution Will Be Televised

Neutral Coverage

  • C-SPAN
  • BBC Parliament
  • CPAC (Canada)
  • Today In Parliament (BBC Radio 4)
  • Prime Time (RTE)
  • Nightly News With Vincent Brown (TV3 Ireland)
  • Shepard Smith Reporting
