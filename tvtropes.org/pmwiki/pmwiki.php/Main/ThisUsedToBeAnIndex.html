This Used to Be an Index

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

One fact of life is that many things change. Things in the present may be different from what they were in the past. Stories, the chief goal of them being "change", will also play with changes a lot.

Compare This Might Be an Index.

Not to be confused with Renamed Tropes or The Ex Index.

  • Became Their Own Antithesis
  • Becoming the Mask: What was originally the undercover agent's cover becomes their real personality.

  • Constantly Changing Name: A name is changed many times.

  • Defrosting Ice Queen

  • Former Bigot: A character that used to have bigoted views.

  • Former Friend of Alpha Bitch
  • Former Friends Photo
  • Formerly Friendly Family: Family members that were once close to each other, but now hate each other.
  • Former Teen Rebel: A mature adult who used to be a unruly, troublesome child.

  • Formerly Fat: A physically fit person was once obese.
  • Formerly Fit: An overweight person was once of a healthy physique.

  • From Zero to Hero: An insignificant person becomes a hero worthy of honor.

  • Took a Level in Kindness: A character becomes nicer than they were originally.
