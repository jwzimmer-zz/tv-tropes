A listing of the series published by Marvel Comics which may or may not take place in the broader Marvel Universe (and the Marvel multiverse as well).

For many series that are about a single character, there's no reason to describe the character separately from the series (you probably don't want to talk about only Iron Man's appearances in the series named "Iron Man", for instance), so see Marvel Comics Characters.
