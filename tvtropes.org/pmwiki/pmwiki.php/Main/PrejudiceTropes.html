Prejudice Tropes

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

"I don't think you're human if you don't acknowledge some prejudice."
— Orson Welles

Ugh, Tropers! I can't stand 'em.

Here are tropes that deal with biases, bigotry, discrimination, and prejudice involving different categories of people; including racism (realistic and fantastic), sexism, sexualism, sectarianism, etc. Or just any acts of (unfairly) judging others by some (arbitrary) physical attributes or mental behaviors.

Advertisement:

Note: this index is not for tropes that you think have Unfortunate Implications. This is for tropes that are explicitly about in-universe prejudice.

  • These Tropes Are Equal
  • Absolute Xenophobe: We will kill anyone who is not like one of us!
  • Absurdly Divided School: Our clique is so much better than yours, and there's no way we can get along!
  • Acceptable Targets: Specific individuals/groups that a culture considers OK to express prejudice towards, whether the reasons are legitimate or otherwise.
  • Aggressive Categorism: You're stupid because you're a troper. Therefore, tropers are stupid.
  • Albinos are Freaks: Their pale skin and red eyes creep me out!
  • All of the Other Reindeer: Ugh! I can't stand being near anyone who's much more different than us. They don't deserve to hang out with us.
    • Advertisement:
  • Angry Black Man: I constantly feel pissed off by all the oppression that my people have suffered because of white racism, both past and present.
  • Angry White Man: I constantly feel pissed off by all the complaints of homophobia/racism/sexism against straight white men such as myself, from all those politically correct social justice warriors.
  • Anti-Intellectualism: I don't trust the opinions of egghead nerds who seem or act smarter than myself, or who challenge my opinions with their "facts".
  • Ape Shall Never Kill Ape: You should treat the rest of our kind with proper respect... but you shouldn't be so generous to outsiders.
  • Armoured Closet Gay: I hate homosexuals, even though I'm secretly one of them.
  • Attractiveness Discrimination: I'll only go out with you if you're hot. I dislike ugly people.
  • Bastard Angst: I get a hard time from others because my parents never married each other.
    • Advertisement:
  • Bigot with a Crush: Even though I despise those people, I'll admit that one in particular looks so... beautiful and charming?
  • Bigotry Exception: I despise all tropers... except for that guy, he's the only good one.
  • Bigot vs. Bigot: I hate that bigot just as much as I hate those tropers!
  • Breaking the Glass Ceiling: At last, I am the first troper to rise past a limit imposed by prejudice!
  • Boomerang Bigot: I hate those people... despite the fact that I'm one of them.
  • The Bully: I just dislike certain people for whatever reason, so I should be cruel to them.
  • Bullying the Disabled: I dislike you because you're crippled.
  • But Not Too Black: Light skin is portrayed as superior to dark skin in dark-skinned ethnicities.
  • But Not Too White: Tan or bronze skin is portrayed as superior to pale or white skin in light-skinned ethnicities.
  • Categorism as a Phobia: These people actually make me feel scared.
  • Category Traitor: You're taking a liking to these folk? How dare you, you traitor!
  • Child Hater: I despise children. So yes, I'm an ageist and I don't care.
  • Child of Two Worlds: I'm not sure about which of these two groups I personally identify with more, because I feel like I would never really belong with both of them.
  • Color Me Black
  • Condescending Compassion: Poor tropers. Wasting their pitiful lives on this website. I pity their kind.
  • Contempt Crossfire: Even if you're a part of us, we hate you just as much as the other group! While the other group hates you for being associated with us, we hate you because you're not an ideal member of us!
  • Death by Racism: You tropers are worthless subhumans! You're all a bunch of— (Killed Midsentence)
  • Dehumanization: Tropers are not humans!
  • Dehumanizing Insult: They're nothing but vermin!
  • Deliberate Values Dissonance: Historical fiction should accurately depict such past attitudes, no matter how despicable they are today.
  • Discriminate and Switch: I can't stand that guy's kind... and I mean the fact that he didn't wipe his feet in my house. Did you think I was hating him for being black or something?
  • Does Not Like Men: I hate men.
  • Doesn't Trust Those Guys: I don't trust those tropers!
  • Double Standard: X did Z? Eh, whatever, good for them. Y did Z? How dare they!
  • Eurabia: Muslim people scare me. Those Arab bastards are trying to conquer the West!
  • Everything Is Racist: Are you insulting my race, you intolerant douchebag?!
  • Evil Colonialist: I serve a powerful colonial empire. Strongly believing in our innate superiority, I help them with conquering weaker nations and subjugating inferior races.
  • Equal-Opportunity Evil: I really don't give a damn about who I hire to do my nefarious work, or what they look or act like, just as long as they get the job done.
  • Feeling Oppressed by Their Existence: Those tropers have never done me any real harm, yet I hate their damned guts anyways.
  • Female Misogynist: I dislike women, despite having XX chromosomes as well.
  • Final Solution: We want the complete, bloody annihilation of certain races we have deemed as worthless. What our opponents would call "genocide", we instead call it "pest control".
  • Former Bigot: I used to really hate those people, but later I changed my mind about them. They're really not so bad after all.
  • The Fundamentalist: Society should adhere strictly to my religious principles, and any sinners or nonbelievers who disagree should just go to Hell for all I care.
  • Gayngst: It really sucks to be gay in this world.
  • Gayngst-Induced Suicide: Normal, healthy people hate homosexuals like me. God hates me. I'm worthless. So I might as well just kill myself.
  • Half-Breed Angst: It sucks to have mixed racial heritage, because I can't fully identify with either one of my parents' communities, and all those racist pricks will never accept me as being one of them.
  • Half-Breed Discrimination: We hate you because you're a filthy, genetically impure mixed-breed. Your parents should've never dared to conceive you!
  • Hates Everyone Equally: I'm not a bigot, I don't discriminate. I despise every group of people, including my own!
  • Have You Tried Not Being a Monster?
  • He-Man Woman Hater: I hate women.
  • Heteronormative Crusader: I can't stand those damn gay people, always plaguing our world with their disgusting lust for the same sex.
  • Homophobic Hate Crime: Gays deserve to be attacked and killed!
  • Innocent Bigot: Sorry, I honestly didn't mean to offend you.
  • Internalized Categorism: The fact that I'm one of those people, it really makes me feel insecure and hate myself.
  • Irrational Hatred: I can't stand those people, but I don't really know why.
  • Karmic Transformation: Dammit, karma's a bitch. I never dreamed of becoming one of those stupid tropers!
  • The Klan: We're the apex of white supremacy in America. We desire a return to the good old days of racial segregation, when non-white people lived in fear of us.
  • Loners Are Freaks: I don't like loners. They're nothing but freaks or social deviants.
  • Malcolm Xerox: I'm a black nationalist of the same vein as Malcolm X. I desire to achieve freedom for my people by any means necessary.
  • Maligned Mixed Marriage: X and Y should never try to mate together. How dare they taint their racial purity!
  • Master Race: We believe ourselves to not just be a superior race, but the best race in the world.
  • Misanthrope Supreme: I really hate all of humankind, even though I'm another stinkin' member of Homo sapiens.
  • Mistaken for Misogynist: No, seriously. I like and respect women.
  • Mistaken for Racist: How am I being racist?
  • Misunderstood Loner with a Heart of Gold: That guy doesn't have any friends or talk to anybody, he must be some kind of evil monster. I don't have to actually know him to know that he's bad news.
  • Misplaced Nationalism: My country is the best, and your country sucks!
  • Mistaken for Foreigner: I didn't realize you're from here.
  • Mistaken Nationality: I thought you were from Troperland.
  • Mistaken for Gay: Wait a minute, you're actually straight? I thought otherwise.
  • Mistaken for Terrorist: I'm suspicious of that foreign brown guy, I think he must be a jihadist!
  • N-Word Privileges: You called us what? Only we're allowed to call each other that!
  • A Nazi by Any Other Name: We're not exactly literal Nazis, but our xenophobia is very comparable.
  • No True Scotsman: You're being nice to those people? No true member of this group would ever do such an awful thing!
  • Noble Bigot: I may dislike tropers, but I do have a heart.
  • Noble Bigot with a Badge: I may not like tropers much, but I still have a duty to serve and protect people, even their kind.
  • Not Evil, Just Misunderstood
  • Not Like Other Girls: I'll admit miss, I think you're not stupid like other girls are.
  • Opinion Override: How dare you mock this group of people that I'm not apart of! What do you mean they actually liked that offensive joke?!
  • Oppressed Minority Veteran: Even though I fought for my country during the war, I still get treated like shit by my fellow citizens and the government.
  • Persecution Flip: AKA reverse discrimination; for when a (usually) privileged group of people find themselves getting treated like dirt by the (usually) oppressed underclasses. A very harshly ironic form of social role reversal.
  • Politically Correct History: Or we could just pretend it was never actually a big deal, because we really don't want to offend anyone.
  • Politically Correct Villain: Just because I'm a villain, it doesn't mean I'm some sort of bigot, and in fact I despise discrimination.
  • Political Correctness Gone Mad: Media Watchdogs and Moral Guardians are overly sensitive and get way too easily offended.
  • Politically Incorrect Hero: Just because I'm a hero, it doesn't mean I like certain groups of people.
  • Politically Incorrect Villain: I'm a villain, and that means I can be very hateful against certain groups of people!
  • Prejudice Aesop: I used to be prejudiced until I learned a valuable lesson on why it's bad.
  • Pretend Prejudice: Honestly, I was just kidding about whatever bad things I said about tropers! I didn't mean any of it!
  • Prom Is for Straight Kids: Only hetero couples can attend prom, not you fags!
  • Psychological Projection: I'm not the bad guy, it's those awful tropers who are the evil ones here!
  • Punished for Sympathy: You spared Alice, even though we have orders to take care of all of those people? You'd better be able to explain yourself to the Comrade Commissar, troper!
  • Racist Grandma: Because I'm really old, you shouldn't expect me to act PC toward other races.
  • Rainbow Lens: A character experiences prejudice for a queerness-unrelated trait in a very similar way to how a person would experience prejudice related to queerness.
  • The Social Darwinist: "Survival of the fittest" means that the strongest people deserve to dominate and/or eliminate anyone weaker than them.
  • Some of My Best Friends Are X, so thus I can't be biased or bigoted against them!
  • Straw Feminist: All men are nothing but dirty, lustful pigs!
  • Straw Misogynist: All women should just shut the hell up, and Stay in the Kitchen!
  • Suffers Newbies Poorly: You're stupid because your post count is lower than mine/you only joined yesterday.
  • Tall Poppy Syndrome: I feel intense envy against people who are better than myself.
  • Those Wacky Nazis: We're the apex of fascism in Europe. We desire Germanic racial supremacy, and the total extermination of Jews, Romani, Slavs, etc.
  • Too Qualified to Apply: Sorry, you exceed our expectations, so we won't hire you.
  • Tragic Bigot: A lousy past made me feel resentful against those people.
  • Trans Relationship Troubles: No one wants to date me because I'm Transgender.
  • Trans Tribulations: Trannies are freaks and belong at the funny farm... Or at least, far away from me and my friends!
  • Transgender Fetishization: I'm transgender and good-looking as my preferred gender, so I don't usually have that problem, but instead people sexualize me and don't take me seriously.
  • Troubled Sympathetic Bigot: I don't hate that nice person, Alice, or even really her kind of people, bad as they are. But it's gone too far, it's them or us now. I'll be doing my duty, may God forgive me. I just wish there could be another way! Alas...
  • Unsettling Gender Reveal: The pretty girl I was flirting with is really some kind of gay tranny freak?!
  • Urban Segregation: We just don't want to live next-door with a particular class or category of folks.
  • Values Dissonance: Unfortunately, some prejudices were considered far more acceptable in the past, and this can show up in older media.
  • Van Helsing Hate Crimes: To save humanity, all vampires must be destroyed. Even if you can point to some one-in-a-million exception that is not a murderous freak, why should I care? It's still a carrier of the disease that might yet doom us all.
  • Warring Natures: I'm the child of two parents from different races. And those two races also happen to violently hate each other. Do I really have to choose a side?
  • "Which Restroom" Dilemma: Bigots won't let me use the ladies' room, just because I'm physically male, even though I identify as a woman.
  • White Guilt: Past and present injustices against people of color make me feel ashamed of what people of my race have done to them.
  • Wishful Projection
  • Would Be Rude to Say "Genocide": What "genocide"? We're not trying to exterminate that pesky inferior race, we just want to put these subhumans in their proper place.
  • Yellow Peril: Asian people scare me. Those Chinese and Japanese bastards are trying to conquer the West!
  • You Are a Credit to Your Race: I'll admit it, you make other tropers proud. Even if the same can't be said for the rest of them.
  • You Are What You Hate: I hate tropers. What? I am a troper?
  • You Hate What You Are: I hate myself, and all the other tropers like me!
  • You Know I'm Black, Right?: You have a lot of nerve saying such bigoted things about tropers when we're standing right here!
  • You Know What They Say About X...: You know what they say about them tropers? They're ruining their lives.
  • Zombie Advocate: It's racist to assume all vampires are murderous! Well, okay, all the ones we've met so far have been, and all the ones we've heard about, but maybe the next one will be the first nice one! Everyone deserves to be treated as an individual!
