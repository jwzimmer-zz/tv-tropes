Apathy Index

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Fr
  • Create New

I have an intense, burning indifference
— Dr. Doofenshmirtz, Phineas and Ferb

Tropes about apathy, boredom, laziness, and other stuff. Eh, don't feel like explaining it. [Describe Topic Here]

Compare with Ignored Index, Despair Tropes, and Boredom Tropes. Contrast with Audience Reactions & Character Reaction Index. Opposite of Obsession Tropes index.

Don't feel like explaining these. Read it yourself.

Advertisement:
  • Apathetic Citizens: Someone's hurt and homeless...and we don't care.
  • Apathetic Clerk: I don't care about my job and don't you dare ask me to do it.
  • Apathetic Student: I don't give a crap about school. Just let us go home, man!
  • Apathetic Teacher: I have no desire to teach my students. Who cares about grades anyway?
  • Apathy Killed the Cat: How things are, you ask? I don't know.
  • Ass Pull: We just pull anything out of thin air just to advance the plot because we're too lazy to explain where it came from.
  • Atlantis Is Boring: It's boring living underwater.
  • Blah Blah Blah: Nothing but "blah". What were you expecting?
  • "Blind Idiot" Translation: How do you translate this? You know what? Forget it! Don't care anymore!
  • Boredom Montage: I get bored as time goes on.
  • Boring Insult: You're lame. That's all I have to tell you.
    • Advertisement:
  • Boring Return Journey: We're back home. Now what?
  • Borrowed Catchphrase: I'm gonna use someone else's catchphrase since I'm too lazy to come up with my own.
  • Brilliant, but Lazy: I have the skills, but don't bother applying myself with them.
  • Bystander Syndrome: Someone needs help? Not my problem.
  • Capcom Sequel Stagnation: So, we need to make a new game. Just take the old one and tweak it a bit. I don't care.
  • Cats Are Lazy: I feel like doing nothing but sleep on the cat tree.
  • Cliché Storm: Yeah, these tropes were used before, but we're too lazy to use anything that wasn't done before.
  • Complaining About Shows You Don't Watch: I don't like the show, and I won't waste time watching it to see why I don't.
  • Concepts Are Cheap: May as well rely on the concept and buzz words for an already thin script. We're too lazy to come up with a script with more effort into it.
    • Advertisement:
  • Conducting the Carnage: So what if the world is ending? I'm just going to stand here and move my hands like a conductor. Whatever.
  • Cowboy BeBop at His Computer: Uhh... I never watched that show, so I'm gonna throw information from my head to make that article more fitting.
  • Creator's Apathy: I really don't know or care what to do with my work.
  • Dan Browned: I know I lied when I said I did my research, but truth is researching is so boring!
  • Darkness-Induced Audience Apathy: There's nothing in this work that amuses us, much less anyone worth liking. So bored.
  • Describe Topic Here: Just do it. We're too lazy to describe it ourselves.
  • Didn't We Use This Joke Already?: Yes. Too bored to come up with any new jokes.
  • Dismotivation: I don't want to progress in life.
  • The Ditherer: I can't choose between two alternatives.
  • Eight Deadly Words: I don't care about any of these people.
  • The Elites Jump Ship: I'm upper class. I don't have to deal with your problems. I'm out of here!
  • Fantastically Indifferent: Wow. Magic. Whatever.
  • Fat Slob: I'm a fat guy who ain't cleaning up my mess.
  • Fleeting Demographic Rule: Let's just do a gimmick/storyline we've already done before. It's not like everyone's going to remember.
  • Four-Temperament Ensemble: Phlegmatic
  • Funny Character, Boring Actor: The character in the show/movie is hilarious! The guy who plays him.... eh, not so much.
  • Game-Breaking Bug: What? There's a glitch in the game that makes it impossible to play? Cool. Don't care.
  • Genius Slob: I'm too smart to clean up anything.
  • The Gods Must Be Lazy: I'm a god. I don't want to be bothered doing things for you mortals.
  • Good Bad Bugs: It's a good thing the bug in the game is fun, because we don't feel like fixing it.
  • Hand Wave: I'm far too bored to give out any complex detail to explain the plot points of my work.
  • Head-in-the-Sand Management: I'm in charge, but I won't address a threat till it's too late to do anything about it.
  • Laborious Laziness: I'll work on this thing here for 2 hours, even if it takes me an hour.
  • Lack of Empathy: Someone is sad, and I don't care.
  • Lazy Artist: I don't put any detail in my artwork.
  • Lazy Bum: I don't want to do anything at all.
  • Lazy Mexican: ¡Olvidalo, holmes! I ain't workin' for nothin', ese!
  • Lazy Husband: Meh, I'll let my wife clean up the mess.
  • Lazy Neutered Pet: Eh, they took out my gonads. Now I feel like doing nothing but eat and sleep.
  • Limited Animation: We're too lazy to make our animation more advanced.
  • Medieval Stasis: I know it's been, like, hundreds of years since our society has advanced in technology, but whatever. Let's just stay as it is.
  • Mellow Fellow: My main personality trait is that I don't care.
  • Misplaced-Names Poster: I know the names on the poster don't match up the actors but you couldn't pay me to fix it.
  • Museum of Boredom: I'm in a museum that's extremely boring.
  • Myopic Conqueror: I am The Conqueror, but the place I have conquered doesn't interest me in the slightest. Yawn......
  • Nature Is Boring: I am so bored by nature.
  • Never Recycle Your Schemes: Why bother revising the same scheme? It would be too tedious to reuse something that just failed.
  • No Plans, No Prototype, No Backup: Here, I made this thing. What do you mean, what if something happens to it? There's no "backup".
  • Non-Answer: [.....]
  • Not Distracted by the Sexy: She's hot. So what?
  • Obvious Beta: This game isn't finished but let's release it anyway.
  • Ode to Apathy: I don't even care about it but I'm singing about it anyway.
  • One-Product Planet: I have an idea—everyone on this planet should just make one thing.
  • Overused Running Gag: Too bored to come up with any other gags to fit the humor.
  • Perpetual Beta: Instead of just fixing the game once and for all, let's just keep patching it over and over.
  • Planet of Hats: Everyone on this planet has something in common, because who cares?
  • The Power of Apathy: Not caring about things makes me powerful. Whatever.
  • Professional Slacker: It's my job to sit around and do nothing all day.
  • Recycled Premise: We're just going to use a premise that has been done before because we're too lazy to write anything original.
  • Recycled Script: We're just going to use the same script from another show because we're lazy writers.
  • Rich Boredom: Man, opulence is so boring!
  • Ridiculous Procrastinator: I should probably do something...nah, I'm too lazy right now. I'll do it later.
  • Safety in Indifference: Not caring protects me from getting hurt.
  • Seen It All: We already saw it, and that makes it boring.
  • Same Story, Different Names: Okay, so I need to make a new story, but here's what I'm gonna do—I'll just do the same thing again and tweak the title. No one will notice.
  • Shaped Like Itself: Literally no one cares about analogies. It is what it is.
  • Shrug of God: I know you have a question about a story I wrote but...Eh. I don't know.
  • Single-Biome Planet: Isn't one biome on the planet enough?
  • The Slacker: I just sit around doing nothing all day.
  • Small Reference Pools: Look, if it's not popular, I'm not going to acknowledge it.
  • Small Town Boredom: This shabby place is so boring.
  • The Snack Is More Interesting: I know this is super important, but...Food.
  • So Okay, It's Average: It's just plain mediocre. Nothing interested about it.
  • Space Age Stasis: There's a Time Skip and technology hasn't changed at all. So what.
  • Stock Footage: Let's just use this clip over and over.
  • Stopped Caring: Ah, forget it.
  • Straw Nihilist: Life is meaningless anyway, so who cares what happens to everybody?
  • Stupid Neutral: Who cares about common sense? I'm simply uninterested in using my head anyway.
  • Surveillance Station Slacker: I don't have time to stare at surveillance cameras to keep an eye on anyone there. I am too busy slacking off.
  • Symbolic Serene Submersion: I'm floating limply underwater, symbolizing my apathy/helplessness, because I simply stopped caring.
  • Translation: "Yes"
  • Trapped by Mountain Lions: There's so many characters and subplots introduced yet none are relevant to the plot. Ergo they don't interest me.
  • True Love Is Boring: This OTP is snoozefest.
  • True Neutral (some examples): I don't care about the battle between good vs evil or order vs chaos.
  • Tyop on the Cover: Yeah, I know the cover of the work has a typo. What's your point?
  • Unfazed Everyman: I know this stuff is super weird but I couldn't care less.
  • Unfortunate Implications: (YMMV) Yeah, I'll just put this in the story. Whatev—Wait, what do you mean someone's offended by it?
  • The Unreveal: It takes time to detail exactly what you want to see. Not revealing it to you saves us time.
  • The Un-Twist: I don't care if it was super obvious. I'm still treating this like The Reveal.
  • Unusually Uninteresting Sight: Something awesome just happened. Meh.
  • Vanilla Edition: We don't care about adding "extra features" to a DVD release so you can get your so-called money's worth. Just buy it!
  • Victory Is Boring: I won! But I got nothing to do now.
  • While Rome Burns: Something horrible is happening...Time to chill.
  • White Void Room: I have decorated this room with literally nothing.
  • A Winner Is You: You've won the video game. Here's your reward: "Good job."
  • A Wizard Did It: What, you want me to explain something that doesn't make sense? Fine. The answer is "magic."
  • Wrongfully Attributed: Wait, I don't know who actually did this thing...Ah, whatever. It was this guy.
Eh, we don't care about making a more creative stinger.
