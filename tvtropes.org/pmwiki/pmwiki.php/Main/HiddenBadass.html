Hidden Badass

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

These characters are More Than Meets the Eye. They are badasses but are lacking in traits typically associated with your average everyday Hollywood Action Hero so their true strength may come as a surprise to both the viewer and whoever is unlucky enough to piss them off.

They're the badass you least expect.

This character is a badass, but you wouldn't expect it because s/he is...

  • Action Dad - a dad
  • Action Fashionista - too obsessed with the latest trends.

  • Action Girlfriend - someone's girlfriend
  • Action Politician - a politician
  • Action Mom - a mom
  • Action Survivor - someone who lacks the asskicking skills
  • Adaptational Badass - not a badass in the original

  • Almighty Janitor - too blue-collar
  • Ambadassador - an ambassador
  • Ambiguously Trained - is currently in a mundane profession

  • Badass Bystander - just some random passerby
  • Badass Normal - a normal around a bunch of superhumans
  • Badass on Paper - unimpressive in person
  • Badass Pacifist - totally not gonna fight
