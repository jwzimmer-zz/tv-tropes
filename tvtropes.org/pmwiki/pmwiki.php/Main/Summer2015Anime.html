Summer 2015 Anime

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

This chart is a list of the known TV anime series that aired between July and September of 2015, along with their Laconic descriptions.

For the previous season see Spring 2015 Anime. For the next season see Fall 2015 Anime.

The discussion thread is here.

TV Series

  • Aoharu × Machinegun (Aoharu × Kikanjū)
    (PV)
    Airing from July 2 for 12 episodes

    Through strange circumstances, a Bifauxnen finds herself drawn into the world of survival games. She teams up with a host and a ero-manga artist to become the best in Japan.
Advertisement:
  • Aquarion Logos
    (PV)
    Airing from July 2 for 27 episodes

    A third installment in the Genesis of Aquarion series, details are scarce.
  • Bikini Warriors
    (PV)
    Airing from July 7

    It is exactly what it sounds like.
  • Castle Town Dandelion (Joukamachi No Dandelion)
    (PV)
    Airing from July 2 for 12 episodes

    Nine siblings from a royal family and who have super powers, are under the constant watch of over 200 cameras.Their daily lives are broadcast nationwide in order to help the populace elect the next monarch. Adaptation of a Yonkoma.

  • Chaos Dragon
    (PV)
    Airing from July 2 for 12 episodes

    On an island country that's caught in a cold war between two major powers, the guardian deity has gone mad.
  • Charlotte
    (PV)
    Airing from July 4 for 13 episodes

    In a World... where a small percentage of the population gains powers during puberty, the plot starts when a boy meets a girl.
Advertisement:
  • Classroom Crisis
    (PV)
    Airing from July 3 for 1-cour

    Plot details are scarce, but it's described as a "school x work romantic comedy".
  • Daily Life with Monster Girl
    (PV)
    Airing from July 7

    Due to a cultural program between mankind and monster races, the protagonist must share his room with a Lamia. Soon more monster girls show up.
  • Danchigai
    (PV)
    Airing from July 2 for 12 5-minute shorts

    The story follows the everyday lives of five siblings—four sisters and one brother—who share a multi-unit apartment - the titular "danchi". Adaptation of a Yonkoma.
  • Dragon Ball Super
    (CM)
    Airing from July 5

    A new Dragon Ball series.
  • Durarara!! X2 Ten
    Airing from July 4 for 12 episodes

    The second cour (of three) of the second season.
  • Fate/kaleid liner PRISMA☆ILLYA 2wei! Herz!
    Airing from July 24 for 10 episodes

    Second season. Or third, depending on how you count
Advertisement:
  • Gangsta.
    (PV)
    Airing from July 1 for 12 episodes

    In a World... similar to our own, we follow a duo of Neighbourhood-Friendly Gangsters, who work as bodyguards-for-hire.
  • Gatchaman Crowds insight
    Airing from July 4 for 12 episodes

    Second season
  • Gate: Jieitai Kanochi Nite Kaku Tatakeri
    (PV)
    Airing from July 3 for 24 episodes

    A gate to a fantasy world opens in modern Japan.
  • God Eater
    (PV)
    Airing from July 12 for 13 episodes

    An adaptation of the action game. In a World... where humanity has been pushed to the brink of extinction by monsters called "aragami", they are fought with newly-developed symbiotic weapons.
  • Hetalia The World Twinkle
    Airing from July 3

    Sixth season
  • Himouto! Umaru-chan
    (PV)
    Airing from July 8

    A beautiful high-school girl, who's perfect at both sports and academics, turns into a complete slob once back at home, to the consternation of her brother.
  • THE iDOLM@STER: Cinderella Girls Second season
    Airing from July 17 for 1 cour

    The second season

  • Junjou Romantica 3
    Airing from July 8

    Third season
  • Sky Wizards Academy (Kuusen Madoushi Kouhosei no Kyoukan)
    (PV)
    Airing from July 8 for 12 episodes

    A former elite wizard, now loathed as a traitor, who must train a squad of three girls that suffered 10 consecutive losses.
  • Makura no Danshi (Pillow Boys)
    Airing from July 13 for 12 episodes

    A first-person anime that has various boys sleeping beside the viewer.
  • Million Doll
    Airing from July 6 for 12 episodes

    A shut-in with a talent for making female idols popular through her blog meets her match in an charismatic otaku.
  • Miss Monochrome
    Airing from July 3 for 13 10-minute shorts

    Second season
  • My Monster Secret
    (PV)
    Airing from July 6

    A boy finds out that the girl he likes it's actually a half-vampire. Now he must keep her true identity secret, or she will be forced to leave the school. The problem is, he can't tell a lie even if his life dependent on it. Then things get weird.
  • Non Non Biyori Repeat
    Airing from July 6 for 1-cour

    Second season
  • My Wife is the Student Council President (Okusama ga Seito Kaichō)
    (PV)
    Airing from July 1 for 12 10-minute shorts

    A Student Council Vice-President finds himself in an arranged marriage with his childhood friend, the Student Council President.
  • Over Lord 2012
    (PV)
    Airing from July 7 for 13 episodes

    A salaryman gets stuck in a defunct MMORPG and decides to take it over
  • Prison School
    (PV)
    Airing from July 10

    At a prestigious all-girls high school with a student body of 1,000, boys are finally allowed to attend. Five boys enter the school with the aspirations for a harem life, but end up instead forced to live almost as prisoners.
  • Ranpo Kitan: Game of Laplace
    (PV)
    Airing from July 2 for 1-cour

    An anime inspired by the works of mystery author Edogawa Ranpo. Boys solve murder mysteries at a middle school.
  • Rokka: Braves of the Six Flowers
    (PV)
    Airing from July 4 for 12 episodes

    6 heroes are chosen by the goddess to save the world. But when they meet, there are seven of them. One of them must be a fake sent to destroy the party.
  • School-Live!
    (CM)
    Airing from July 9

    A group of girls cope with living during a Zombie Apocalypse.
  • Senki Zesshou Symphogear GX
    Airing from July 3 for 1-cour

    Third season
  • Shimoneta
    (PV)
    Airing from July 4 for 12 episodes

    ShimoSeka for short. In a World... where all forms of obscenity are outlawed, we follow a group rebelling against this.
  • Snow White with the Red Hair (Akagami no Shirayukihime)
    (PV)
    Airing from July 6 for 1-cour, with another in 2016

    A beautiful doctor runs away after the prince of her country orders her to be his concubine. She then meets and befriends a boy in a forest. Very loosely based on Snow White.
  • Sore ga Seiyuu! (That is a Voice Actor!)
    (PV)
    Airing from July 7

    An adaptation of a Yonkoma by the author of Hayate the Combat Butler and voice actress Masami Asano, which details the life of three rookie voice-actresses.
  • SuzakiNishi
    Airing from July 8 for 12 episodes

    An adaption of a radio show where the titular voice actresses discuss business ideas.
  • To Love-Ru Trouble - Darkness 2nd
    Airing from July 6 for 1-cour

    Second season
  • Ushio and Tora
    (PV)
    Airing from July 3 for 26 episodes, with another 13 on Spring 2016

    The hero is forced to release an ancient demon sealed by his ancestor in order to protect his family and friends from wicked spirits.
  • Venus Project
    Airing from July 5 for 12 episodes

    Idols. Half the episodes is live-action
  • Wakaba Girl
    Airing from July 3 for 13 10-minute shorts

    The elegant heiress to a rich family is fascinated with the "gyaru" lifestyle. Adaptation of a Yonkoma.
  • Wooser No Sono Higurashi Mugen Hen
    Airing from July 3 for 13 10-minute shorts

    Third season
  • WORKING!!!!!
    Airing from July 4 for 13 episodes

    Third season, as is visible from the number of exclamation points.
