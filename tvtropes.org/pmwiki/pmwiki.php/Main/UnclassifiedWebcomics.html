These are Web Comics which don't seem to fit in any of the other categories, or maybe it's just that the person who made the page didn't bother to index it,note In which case it's also very likely that the author of the article also didn't bother to write the page properly. Please consider that all comics under this list are in desperate need of both wiki magic love and mirror on archive.org (you know, for long-term Wiki Magic) until proven otherwise. leaving that job to helpful wiki elves who don't know enough about the comic to classify it. (If you know one of them well enough to realize that it actually does fit in some other category, please move it there).
