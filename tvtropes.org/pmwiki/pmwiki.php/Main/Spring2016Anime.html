Spring 2016 Anime

This chart is a list of known Tv anime series that aired between April and June 2016, along with their Laconic descriptions.

For the previous season see Winter 2016 Anime. For the next season see Summer 2016 Anime.

Here is this season's discussion thread.

Tv Series

  • Age 12 ~Chiccha na Mune no Tokimeki~
    (PV)
    Airing from April 4

    A 12-year-old girl deals with her first love and other issues expected of someone entering puberty.
Advertisement:
  • Ace Attorney
    (CM)
    Airing from April 2

    An adaption of the popular lawyer series, covering the first two visual novels.
  • And You Thought There is Never a Girl Online?
    (PV)
    Airing from April 7

    A boy is confessed to by a girl in an online game, who turns out to have difficulty understanding reality.
  • Anne Happy
    (PV)
    Airing from April 7

    Schoolgirl series about unlucky girls.
  • The Asterisk War
    Airing from April 2

    Second season
  • Bakuon!!
    (PV)
    Airing from April 4

    Schoolgirls riding motorcycles
  • Battle Spirits Double Drive
    Airing from April 6

    A new anime adaptation of Battle Spirits. Shunta Mogami, a Battle Spirits player is summoned to Spirits World to prevent its destruction.
  • Beyblade Burst
    Airing from April 4

    A new Beyblade series.
  • Big Order
    Airing from April 15

    Urban Fantasy action series about taking over the world. From the author of Future Diary.
Advertisement:
  • Bonobono
    (PV)
    Airing from April 2 as a series of shorts

    Gag comedy about forest animals.
  • Bungo Stray Dogs
    (PV)
    Airing from April 6 for 12 episodes, with a second cour in October

    People named after famous authors kick butt and look cool doing it.
  • Concrete Revolutio: Choujin Gensou The Last Song
    Airing from April 3

    Second season of the Time Skip-happy superhuman series.
  • Endride
    (PV)
    Airing from April 2 for 24 episodes

    Shun, an ordinary boy from earth finds a strange crystal in his father's office and is then transported to another world. Emilio, a prince of a fantasy kingdom, attempts to take the throne from his uncle in an act of revenge. He fails, is captured by his uncle, and imprisoned. After being transported to Endra, Shun lands in his prison cell.
  • Flying Witch
    (PV)
    Airing from April 9

    A young witch moves in with her relatives in northern Japan. Slice of Life ensues.
Advertisement:
  • Hakuouki Otogisoushi
    Airing from April 5 as a series of shorts

    Hakuouki chibi comedy
  • Haven't You Heard? I'm Sakamoto
    (PV)
    Airing from April 7

    A comedy about the coolest boy in school.
  • High School Fleet
    (PV)
    Airing from April 9

    In a World... where most cities are on the seas, we follow girls learning to safeguard the seas.
  • Hundred
    (PV)
    Airing from April 4

    Earth is under attack by alien life forms that can only be defeated by a special type of weapon. Naturally, our story follows a boy at a school learning to use this weapon.
  • JoJo's Bizarre Adventure Part IV: Diamond Is Unbreakable
    (PV)
    Airing from April 1 for 39 episodes.

    The fourth part of the long-running series, this time focusing on Josuke Higashikata investigating murder in small-town Japan.
  • Joker Game
    (PV)
    Airing from April 5 for 12 episodes

    A spy thriller set in 1937 Japan.
  • Kabaneri of the Iron Fortress
    (PV)
    Airing from April 7

    In a World... overrun by literally iron-hearted zombies, a group of people onboard a weaponized locomotive must fight them in order to survive.
  • Kagewani (Shou)
    Airing from April 1 as a series of shorts

    Second season
  • Kaitou Joker 3rd Season
    Airing from April 4

    Note the title
  • Kiznaiver
    (CM)
    Airing from April 9

    It follows a group of students that are connected to a system that divides their wounds between them.
  • Kuma Miko: Girl Meets Bear
    (PV)
    Airing from April 3

    A Slice of Life series about a miko who lives with a (civilized, intelligent) talking bear.
  • Kuromukuro
    (PV)
    Airing from April 7

    A mecha series starring a samurai awakened in the "present day".
  • The Lost Village
    (PV)
    Airing from April 1

    30 people disappear into a mysterious, uninhabited village.
  • Macross Delta
    (PV)
    Airing from April 3

    A Hate Plague is spreading across the galaxy, and the only thing that can stop it is an idol group. We follow a girl trying to join this group.
  • Magi: The Adventures of Sinbad
    Airing from April 15

    Magi spinoff
  • My Hero Academia
    (PV)
    Airing from April 3

    In a World... where the majority of humanity has developed superpowers, there's been a need to train heroes to counter the villains. We follow a boy who acquires a unique power from one of the top heroes, and uses that to enter a prestigious school. Based on a popular shounen manga
  • Naruto Shippuden Itachi Shinden-hen ~Hikari to Yami~
    Airing from March 3

    Naruto spinoff covering Itachi's backstory.
  • Onigiri
    (CM)
    Airing from April 6 as a series of shorts

    MMORPG adaptation. Or rather, MMORPG parody.
  • Pan De Peace
    Airing from April 4

    Bread-themed School Girl Series
  • Re:Zero
    Airing from April 3

    A boy gets summoned into a fantasy world, and turns out to have the ability to rewind time to his summoning when killed.
  • RIN-NE second season
    Airing from April 9 for 25 episodes

    Second season
  • Sailor Moon Crystal
    Airing from April 4 for 13 episodes

    An adaptation of the Death Busters arc. The anime staff is new.
  • Seisen Cerberus Ryuukoku no Fatalite
    (PV)
    Airing from April 4

    In a fantasy world, a boy goes on a journey to kill an evil dragon.
  • Shounen Maid
    (PV)
    Airing from April 7

    A boy ends up working as a maid for a man that claims to be his uncle.
  • Space Patrol Luluco (Uchuu Patrol Luluco)
    (CM)
    Airing from April 1 as a series of shorts

    In an area where humans and aliens co-exist, an ordinary girl meets a mysterious boy.
  • Super Lovers
    (PV)
    Airing from April 6 for 10 episodes

    A BL series about a young man trying to civilize his unruly adoptive younger brother.
  • Tanaka-kun is Always Listless
    (PV)
    Airing from April 9

    A comedy about a listless boy.
  • Terra Formars: Revenge
    Airing from April 1

    Second season
  • Three Leaves, Three Colors
    (PV)
    Airing from April 10

    Schoolgirl series from the creator of Engaged to the Unidentified.
  • Tonkatsu DJ Agetarou
    Airing from April 10

    Gag comedy about a beginner DJ from a family that's run a tonkatsu restaurant for three generations.
  • Twin Star Exorcists
    (PV)
    Airing from April 6

    Two young onmyouji are put in an Arranged Marriage. Monster fighting and Belligerent Sexual Tension ensues.
  • Ushio and Tora
    Airing from April 1 for 13 episodes

    Finale
  • Wagamama High Spec
    Airing from April 11 as shorts

    A boy is blackmailed into joining the student council, as he draws a risque manga.
