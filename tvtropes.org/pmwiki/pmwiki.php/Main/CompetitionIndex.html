Competition Index

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

Yeah! Tropes about competition! It's time to play these tropes! You're gonna get smoked! I'm gonna wipe the floor with everyone here! Oh yeah! None of y'all can stand a chance against me! Y'all gonna lose a lot more than these games when I'm finished beating y'all!

I'm gonna beat y'all at all of these tropes!:

Categories:
  • Dueling Tropes
  • Sports Story Tropes
  • Victory and Defeat

  • And You Thought It Was a Game: Did you really think this was a game? What a loser! Ha ha!
    • Advertisement:
  • Baseball Episode: Let's play some baseball. There's no way you can beat me!
  • Beastly Bloodsports: My animal's gonna maul your animal in a fight!
  • Beat Them at Their Own Game: I'm gonna crush you so bad I'll make a fool out of you at your own game!
  • Beauty Contest: I'm far more beautiful than anyone in this contest! I'll win for sure!
  • Big Game: This is the big one where I'm gonna win it all!
  • Blood Sport: I'm gonna beat the blood out of you in this victory!
  • Body-Count Competition: Yeah! I'm catching bodies in this competition!
  • Burping Contest: Bet you can't do a better burp than me!
  • Caption Contest: A funny picture, huh? I bet I can make a wittier remark about it than you can!
  • Challenging the Bully: Hey, Bully McJerkface, I challenge you to a fight!
  • Challenge Gamer: Who wants to accept my challenge in gaming?! Ready to get crushed by me?!
    • Advertisement:
  • Competition Freak: It's what the index is about! So enough questions and more competing!
  • Contest Winner Cameo: I'm just a background character whose purpose is to make an appearance to show that I beat the main characters in a competition! Yeah it's embarrassing to lose against the guy who barely appeared at all! Ha!
  • Cooking Duel: Your meals are so insipid! I can cook better and faster meals than you! You don't have the skills of a chef the way I do!
  • Crippling the Competition: I must hurt that guy in some way to keep him from competing in a contest I want to win!
  • Deadly Game: I'm gonna kill you in this game!
  • Disqualification-Induced Victory: HA! They got disqualified! That means I automatically win!
  • Doom as Test Prize: Seriously?! I have to be murdered just because I won?! What kind of shit is that?!
    • Advertisement:
  • Drench Celebration: Oh yeah! I'm all wet with victory!
  • Drinking Contest: Bet you can't get drunk before me!
  • Dueling Works: No way your work is better than mine! I'm the innovator! You copied the concept of my work! Without my work, your wouldn't even exist! So I deserve to win with my work!
  • Duel to the Death: It's time to duel! Seeing as who the winner will be, it's obvious this will be your last duel in your life!
  • Eating Contest: I can eat more than you! We'll see who wins!
  • Endurance Duel: Face it! You're too fatigue to compete against me in who can withstand the most pain!
  • Friendship-Straining Competition: Screw friendship! Winning is more important to me!
  • Fictional Mystery, Real Prize: That board game I played really helped me get my hands on the real prize I want! It's all mine now! I guess fiction can turn into a reality after all!
  • Forced Prize Fight: I can't believe I'm forced to compete in a fight! I guess I have to be lucky to win then!
  • Friendly Rivalry: Let's compete! May the best man win!
  • Girls vs. Boys Plot: My gender is better than your gender, and I can prove it!
  • Graceful Loser: Good game, man! I can take a loss!
  • I'm Not Here to Make Friends: This Is a Competition, not some place to make friends!
  • Inanimate Competitor: I'm actually competing against an inanimate object?! Ha! This'll be a piece of cake!
  • Inept Talent Show Contestant: This talent show didn't go as I expected it, but I won anyways, so who cares?!
  • "Jar of Jellybeans" Contest: I'm totally going to guess the right amount of jellybeans in the jar before you do! 1, 2, 3, 4...
  • Kill Tally: I've shot down more enemies than you have!
  • Mega Meal Challenge: Give me the biggest meal you got! I'll scarf them all down in seconds! Nobody can top my appetite!
  • Mini-Golf Episode: We'll see which one of us can win in a game of golf, which is me!
  • Muggle Sports, Super Athletes: I'm gonna beat you in sports with my superpowers!
  • No-Holds-Barred Contest: Let's fight! I'll knock you out in ten seconds!
  • Nobody Calls Me "Chicken"!: Who are you calling "chicken?!" I'll take your challenge right now!
  • Office Sports: The office is so boring! Let's play a game to liven it up a bit! Bet you can't beat me!
  • Opposing Sports Team: That sports team is going down! Come on, people! Let's show 'em what we made of!
  • Paintball Episode: I'm gonna shoot you down with so many paintballs with my trusty paintball gun! I'm a master at this, and you can't take me!
  • Radio Contest: There's no way anyone else will win those tickets! I'm getting my hands on those tickets, and I'm gonna call and be the lucky number!
  • Relieved Failure: I lost, and I'm somehow okay with it!
  • Removing the Rival: That rival has to go! I need to think of a way to get rid of him/her in order for me to have a chance at this competition!
  • Rigged Contest: No fair! This contest was rigged! They don't deserve that victory!
  • The Rival: My old nemesis! Looks like we're gonna be competing regularly!
  • Rival Science Teams: Let's form a science team to beat that opposing team at the science fair!
  • Robot Athlete: I'm a robot who can beat anyone at any game!
  • Sore Loser: No fair! I should be the winner! Not you! I hate losing!
  • Spelling Bee: W-I-N-N-E-R spells me! L-O-S-E-R spells you!
  • Sports Stories: I would love to tell my wonderful stories on how I mercilessly crushed my opponents in the sports I've played!
  • Staring Contest: I can stare at you longer than you can at me!
  • "Stop Having Fun" Guys: Competition is supposed to be about winning! Not fun!
  • Talent Contest: I'm gonna win this contest, because I got more talent than anyone around here!
  • This Is a Competition: ...So stop treating it like a silly game of tag and start competing to win! That's what it's all about!
  • Too Qualified to Apply: Stupid people didn't make me qualify for a competition because they just hating on me for being a competitor!
  • Town Contest Episode: We're gonna have a town-wide competition! You're gonna be hitting the streets once I'm finished beating you to the ground!
  • Unsportsmanlike Gloating: Yes! I won! You lost! In your face, bitch!
  • Victory by First Blood: Ha! You bled first! That means I win!

Yeah! What'd I tell you?! I win again! Come back when you losers have the talent to crush the champion like me!
