Rule of Cute

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Laconic
  • Quotes
  • Create New

No other words can describe this.
Advertisement:

The limit of the Willing Suspension of Disbelief for a given element is directly proportional to its adorableness.

For example, anatomically improbable creatures can be perfectly justified in the right setting.

Subtrope of Rule of Cool. Compare Rule of Glamorous, Tiny Tropes.

Contrast Gonk, Hollywood Homely (where someone isn't supposed to be cute or beautiful, but is), Informed Attractiveness (where someone is supposed to be cute or beautiful, but isn't more than anyone else), and Subverted Innocence.

Advertisement:

Tropes running on this rule include:

  • Abandoned Mascot: A series mascot gets replaced or phased out.
  • Adorable Abomination: An incomprehensible nightmare in a broadly adorable form.
  • Adorable Evil Minions: Cute Mooks.
  • Adorably Precocious Child: A child who is seen as cute because they try to act mature beyond their years.
  • Animal-Eared Headband: A headband consisting of animal ears.
  • "Aww"-choo: A character's sneeze is cute.
  • Badass Adorable: Cute and can kick ass.
  • Big Anime Eyes: Large, sparkly eyes emphasize the cuteness of a character.
  • Bunnies for Cuteness: Bunnies are used to symbolize cuteness.
  • Cat Smile: A character smiles like a cat (:3).
  • Cheerful Child: Sweet and innocent little kid.
  • Childish Tooth Gap: A gap in between one's teeth to signify their innocence.
  • Crapsaccharine World: A Crapsack World disguised as a Sugar Bowl.
    • Advertisement:
  • Creepy Cute: A character who is cute and disturbing at the same time.
  • Cry Cute: A character who looks cute when crying.
  • Cuddle Bug: Someone who loves cuddling and hugs.
  • Cute Approaches Camera: An animal approaches the camera to enhance their cuteness.
  • Cute and Psycho, which applies to cute characters that can notably go Ax-Crazy at the same time.
  • Cute Bookworm: A nerd or intellect who's very sweet and endearing.
  • Cute Bruiser: The Cutie has brutal physical defense skills.
  • Cute, but Cacophonic: A cute character with an annoying voice.
  • Cute Clumsy Girl: The Klutz is an adorable lady.
  • Cute Critters Act Childlike: Cute anthropomorphic animals behave in an immature manner.
  • Cute 'em Up: A Shoot 'em Up game consisting of cute creatures.
  • Cute Giant: Big or strong person is incredibly adorable.
  • Cute Ghost Girl: A cute girl who's a ghost of a dead girl.
  • Cute Indignant Girl Stance
  • Cute Is Evil is when a cute character turns out to be evil.
  • Cute Kitten: An adorable cat.
  • Cute Little Fangs: Vampire fangs are depicted as adorable.
  • Cute Machines: Adorable robots.
  • Cute Monster: A monster who is also adorable.
  • Cute Monster Girl: Female monsters are depicted as adorable.
  • Cute Mute: Cute silent person.
  • Cuteness Equals Forgiveness: Forgot what they did, they're cute enough to be forgave.
  • Cuteness Overload: Something so cute either causes you to freeze or explode.
  • Cuteness Proximity: Cute things provoke a distracted 'aww' reaction.
  • Cute Owl: An adorable owl.
  • Cute Sports Club Manager
  • Cute Witch: A witch who is also cute.
  • The Cutie: A characters whose default status is sweet, innocent, and endearing.
  • Deliberately Cute Child: A manipulative child uses their endearing nature to get their way.
  • Dojikko: Clumsy Japanese girl is cute and attractive.
  • Dork Knight: A kindhearted, but capable, hero.
  • Eggshell Clothing
  • Endearingly Dorky: Socially awkward person who is cute because of their dorkiness.
  • Everything's Better with Penguins: Penguins symbolize cuteness.
  • Everything's Better with Plushies: Plush toys symbolize cuteness.
  • The Fake Cutie: Turns out The Cutie faked their ego this whole time.
  • Fun Size: Smaller means cuter.
  • Generic Cuteness
  • Grotesque Cute
  • Hates Being Called Cute: Being called 'cute' provokes a disdainful reaction from them.
  • I'm Taking Her Home with Me!
  • The Internet Is for Cats: The Internet is full of cute cat photos/videos.
  • Kawaiiko: A cutesy Japanese person.
  • Killer Rabbit: A cute animal with murderous intent.
  • Little Miss Badass: Little girl knows how to knock you down.
  • Lovable Lizard: Even the scaly creatures can be cute, and lizards are some of the best in the biz when it comes to reptiles.
  • Meet Cute: When two characters' romance begins incidentally.
  • Meganekko: A cute nerdy female wears glasses.
  • Mega Twintails
  • Meido (but not French Maid)
  • Moe: An extremely adorable character.
  • Moe Couplet
  • Moe Stare
  • Nice Mice
  • Not Allowed to Grow Up: Character retains their childlike appearance well into adulthood.
  • Piggyback Cute
  • Precious Puppy
  • Precocious Crush: A child's innocent crush on an adult or adolescent.
  • Puni Plush
  • Puppy-Dog Eyes: Using your cute eyes to get your way.
  • Puppy Love: Little kids who fall in love.
  • Ridiculously Cute Critter: Animal who's very cute.
  • Series Mascot
  • Shoulder-Sized Dragon
  • Sleep Cute
  • Sprouting Ears
  • Squish the Cheeks: A character's cheeks are squished to tease them over their cuteness.
  • Strawberry Shorthand
  • Sugar Bowl: A beautiful and colorful world.
  • Super-Cute Superpowers
  • Super-Deformed
  • Sweet Seal
  • Sweet Sheep
  • Tastes Like Diabetes: So adorable it's unhealthy.
  • Token Mini-Moe: The only cute member of The Team.
  • Ugly Cute is when this is pulled off against unlikely odds.
  • Ultra Super Happy Cute Baby Fest Farmer 3000: A video game that Tastes Like Diabetes.
  • What Measure Is a Non-Cute?: If it's not cute, then it's not good.
