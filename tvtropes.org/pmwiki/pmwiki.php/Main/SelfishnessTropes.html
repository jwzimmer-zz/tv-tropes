Selfishness Tropes

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

"There is a very fine line between loving life and being greedy for it."
— Maya Angelou

The only person I care about is me! My wants and needs matter more than yours and everyone else! I want everything to myself and I won't share it with others! This is one of the reasons why Humans Are Bastards, but whatever. Here is MY index dealing with selfish and greedy characters.

Compare with Ego Tropes, Obsession Tropes, and The Jerk Index, three of my defining traits. Money Tropes are high on my list of favorite priorities. Contrast with those sharing twinks at Selflessness Tropes.

Advertisement:

These are my Tropes which you can't have!:

  • Above Good and Evil: Screw right and wrong, I'm doing what I want!!
  • All Take and No Give: Taking is better than giving!
  • Always Save the Girl: MY relationship with the girl is more important than others' suffering!
  • Ambition Is Evil: "Ambitious" in fiction means "Will screw over everything and everyone for prestige".
  • Apathetic Citizens: We got better things to do than to help save some homeless people.
  • Apathetic Clerk: I don't care if it's my job, I'm not going to work.
  • Artifact of Attraction: That thing is mine. Move aside!
  • Ate It All: *Burp* I'm sorry, I was supposed to share all of the food with others.
  • Attention Whore: PAY ATTENTION TO ME AND ME ONLY, GODDAMMIT!!
  • Bitch in Sheep's Clothing: I only ever pretend to be a nice, friendly person if doing so can benefit me somehow.
    • Advertisement:
  • Bratty Food Demand: I don't CARE how much time and hard work food preparation takes for the chef, I WANT TO EAT NOW!
  • Bystander Syndrome: Let the cops get the robber. I've got better things to do than to do their job.
  • Corrupt Corporate Executive: I'm the boss around here, and the only thing that's important is increasing my own profits!
  • Con Man: I play gullible people for fools to get rich off their stupidity.
  • Crazy Survivalist: I'm preparing myself for this catastrophe. I don't care about you!
  • Crocodile Tears: Waaaaah! Pay attention to meee!
  • Deal with the Devil: If the Big Bad has your best desires, then you better accept his offer you can't refuse.
  • Death by Materialism: This gold! It's all mine! Wait, what the hell is happening? HOLY CR—
  • Despotism Justifies the Means: I don't care about making the world a better or terrible place. I just want to be a ruler.
    • Advertisement:
  • Dirty Coward: Sorry, but my safety is more important than yours. See ya!
  • The Ditherer: So apparently, it's selfish of me to not make a decision for others. Whatever!
  • Egocentrically Religious: God, give me everything I want! Amen!
  • Enlightened Self-Interest: I'm doing good things just so I can get a lot of rewards and recognition.
  • Entitled Bastard: These idiots should only be helping ME with my problems. Who cares how I treated them!
  • Entitled to Have You: You're going to be mine, regardless of what YOU want! You have to be with me, I WANT you!
  • Executive Excess: Who cares about working a full day anymore? I'm head of the company, so I get to party whenever I want!
  • False Friend: The only reason I ever hang out with you or tolerate your company is because you're a just a useful idiot to me.
  • Gold Digger: I'm not dating or marrying you for "true love". I just want all your money, you rich fool!
  • Gold Fever: I want gold!
  • Greed: Self-explanatory. It's all mine and I'm not going to share!
  • Greedy Jew: The old and harmful stereotype about Jewish people being greedy and malicious.
  • The Hedonist: I only care about my own pleasures, and mine alone!
  • Heroic Team Revolt: I don't have to save that guy. What? You guys are gonna save him? Whatever.
  • If I Can't Have You...: I want you all to myself and if you won't be with me, I'll make sure no one else can have you either.
  • It Amused Me: I do it because I'm bored.
  • It's All About Me: It sure is! And anyone who thinks I should care about others can piss off!
  • Jerkass: Screw politeness or respect, because you're just an idiot who's wasting my precious time!
  • Jerk With A Heart Of Jerk: When I do nice things, only for me to yank it away, fet a cheap laugh, or some other selfish reason behind it.
  • Lack of Empathy: I couldn't care less about other people's stupid feelings or problems, because what I want is far more important!
  • Lovable Rogue: I break the rules for my own benefit, but I'm still a likable guy.
  • Loves Only Gold: I'm greedy, but for a very specific form of wealth, like gold!
  • Manipulative Bastard: I actively fool and mislead other people in order to trick them into serving my own interests.
  • Marrying the Mark: I'm not dating or marrying this person for "true love". I'm actually just trying to rob them blind, or worse.
  • Miser Advisor: I only wanted to be your friend so I can get your money.
  • Money, Dear Boy: I know this film is a load of crap but hey, money!
  • Moral Myopia: I did horrible things to you? Who cares, you deserved all of it. But you did the same things to me? How dare you even try to mess with me!
  • Narcissist: The universe revolves around me, because I'm the greatest person to ever live! Other people only exist to admire my greatness and serve my desires.
  • Never My Fault: It's your fault, not mine!
  • Nominal Hero: I'm joining you guys not because I really care about doing the right thing, but because I just want to kick the villain's ass for messing with me personally.
  • Not in This for Your Revolution: I'm not here for your little war against the bad guys.
  • Not-So-Well-Intentioned Extremist: The end justifies the means... What I meant is my own ends.
  • Only in It for the Money: ...I'm in it for the money.
  • Opportunistic Bastard: In order to get to the top, I take advantage of the plans of others.
  • Original Position Fallacy: I'm all for it! Surely I will benefit from this! What do you mean I'm gonna pay the price? Can I back down?
  • Parental Savings Splurge: Son, I know we were saving up money for college, but this completely arbitrary sale is way too good to pass up!
  • Penny-Pinching Crab: I'm a crab, and I want more material wealth!
  • Personal Gain Hurts: Sadly, there's consequences in getting what I want. Fuck this!
  • Pragmatic Villainy: Even Evil Has Standards. True, but I'm always in it for myself.
  • Protagonist-Centered Morality: If you're good to me regardless of your actions, you're good. If you're a nice person but don't agree with me, you're bad.
  • Proud Peacock: I'm a peacock! Everyone look at me and adore me!
  • Pushed at the Monster: Ahhh! A monster! Don't eat me! Eat my friends!
  • Psychological Projection: It's not me who's being a selfish prick, it's everyone else!
  • Reluctant Gift: I want to give away something, but I can't help clinging to it.
  • The Scrooge: My money matters more than you!
  • Secretly Selfish: An altruistic character is forced to question if their motives are really selfless.
  • Self-Serving Memory: This is how I remember these events... so here's a personal account that shows my own actions in the best light possible.
  • Signed Up for the Dental: I work here so I can get healthcare (or other specific benefits).
  • Slimeball: I'm selfish and I'm not afraid to show it!
  • Smug Snake: I use and crush foolish people so I can get what I want!
  • The Social Darwinist: It's survival of the fittest! Working together is so lame!
  • The Sociopath: I absolutely do not care about any other people around me, because they're all just disposable tools for me to exploit in order to get whatever the hell I want.
  • Spoiled Brat: It's all about what I want and I want it now!
    • Royal Brat: I'm royalty, therefore, you give me what I want now!
  • Stringing The Hopeless Suitor Along: Someone likes having a Hopeless Suitor and prevents them from getting into other relationships, but doesn't actually want to be with the suitor.
  • Taking Advantage of Generosity: What better way to achieve my goals than to exploit others' kindness.
  • The Unapologetic: I don't care about anyone who says I hurt them. I won't apologize to make them feel better, because I regret nothing.
  • The Unfettered: I'm gonna get what I want, and I don't care who I have to step on or who suffers to get it!
  • Wasteful Wishing: If wishing for something that's unimportant seems selfish, then so be it!
  • What Have You Done for Me Lately?: You're only valuable if you do things for me and me only!
  • Wild Card: I'm on nobody's side. I'm on my side!
The hell are you standing there for? These are all mine! I'm not letting you see these!

Alternative Title(s): Selfish Evil
