Fall 2016 Anime

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

This chart is a list of known TV anime series that aired somewhere between October and December 2016, along with their Laconic descriptions.

For the previous season see Summer 2016 Anime. For the next season see Winter 2017 Anime

The discussion thread is here.

TV Series

  • Age 12 ~Chiccha na Mune no Tokimeki~
    Beginning on October 3

    Second season.
  • Ajin 2nd Season

    Second season.
  • ALL OUT!!
    Beginning on October 6

    A sports series about high school rugby.
Advertisement:
  • Ameiro Cocoa 3rd Season

    Third season.
  • Aooni the Blue Demon
    Beginning on October 2

    An Animated Adaptation of a freeware Survival Horror game.
  • Bloodivores

    An adaptation of a Chinese manhua called Space-Time Prisoners. An epidemic of insomnia leads to the creation of a medicine with the side effect of turning anyone who takes it into vampires. The series follows four of these vampires.
  • Brave Witches
    Beginning on October 5

    A Spin-Off of the Strike Witches focusing on the 502nd Joint Fighter Wing that takes place between the first two seasons.
  • Bubuki Buranki The Gentle Giants of the Galaxy

    Second season of BBK/BRNK.
  • Bungou Stray Dogs 2nd Season
    Beginning on October 5

    The second season of the supernatural mystery series.
  • Chi's Sweet Home 2016
    PV.
    Beginning on October 2

    A second adaptation of the adventures of an adorable cat, this time animated in CG.
Advertisement:
  • Classicaloid
    Beginning on October 8

    An original anime featuring bizarro versions of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart, and Beethoven with the mysterious power of "mujik".
  • Cyborg 009: Call of Justice
Beginning on November 25 // An All CGI adaptation of Cyborg 009 featuring an original plot involving the 00 Cyborgs going up against a mysterious organisation of superhumans with nefarious goals named the "Blessed".
  • Digimon Universe: Applimonsters
    PV.
    Beginning on October 1

    The latest installment of the Digimon franchise, which will focus on monsters based on various apps.
  • Dream Festival!
    Beginning on September 23

    Rookie Idols compete for the chance to win Dream Festival! and secure their CD debut.
  • Drifters
    Beginning on October 7

    Famous people are summoned at death's door in order to fight against each other in a fantasy war.
  • Flip Flappers
    Beginning on October 6

    Two girls from different time periods meet by means of the "Pure Illusion", an overlapping dimension, and together they search for a wish-granting gem.
Advertisement:
  • Gakuen Handsome

    A deliberately poorly-drawn short anime about a boy who attends a school filled with handsome guys. Yaoi teasing abounds.
  • Girlish Number

    A cynical look at the world of voice acting, starring an up-and-coming voice actress who lands a role as an idol.
  • The Great Passage

    The story revolves around a serious man and his friendship with his sociable companion as they try to create a dictionary.
  • Haikyuu!! Karasuno Koukou vs Shiratorizawa Gakuen Koukou
    Beginning on October 7

    Third season of the popular Volleyball manga.
  • Heybot!
    Beginning on September 17

    On a screw-shaped island inhabited by sentient robots called Bokyabots, two of them scramble to find the "Bokya Neiji" screws which, when used, allows one to partake in "Bokya Battles" for who can think up and tell the best jokes.
  • Izetta: The Last Witch
    PV.
    Beginning on October 1

    A story about a budding relationship between a witch and a princess during alternate World War II.
  • Kaiju Girls
    PV.
    Beginning on September 27

    Based on Ultra Monsters Anthropomorphic Project, a Moe Anthropomorphism project of Ultra Series.
  • Keijo!!!!!!!!
    PV.
    Beginning on October 6

    A Ecchi series about keijo - a sport where two women try to push each other of a board into the water, but are only allowed to use breasts or butts to touch the opponent.
  • Kitaros Youkai Picture Diary
    Beginning on October 5

    The daily life of a boy who naturally attracts Youkai.
  • Kiss Him, Not Me
    Beginning on October 1

    A Formerly Fat Otaku loses weight after her favorite anime character dies. She becomes much more attractive and becomes a Dude Magnet much to the dismay of her inner Yaoi Fangirl.
  • Long Riders!

    A story of a girl and her new found love for bikes as she delves into the world of cycling.
  • Lostorage Incited WIXOSS

    A new installment of the WIXOSS series.
  • Luger Code 1951

    A young genius and university professor try to break the enemy werewolves' code.
  • Magical Girl Raising Project
    Beginning on October 1

    Adaptation of a Light Novel. In a World... where one person out of ten thousands can gain the power of a "magical girl", 16 Magical Girls fight each other to the death to retain their powers.
  • Magic-kyun! Renaissance

    A young girl transfers into a prestigious highschool and is placed on the Hoshinomori Summer Festival with six other boys.
  • Magic of Stella

    A story about girls and their doujin game development circle.
  • March Comes in Like a Lion
    Beginning on October 8

    A story featuring a young professional Shogi player and his struggles with life.
  • Matoi the Sacred Slayer
    Beginning on October 4

    An Anime First series about a middle school girl who becomes a Magical Girl exorcist.
  • Miss Bernard Said
    Beginning on October 6

    A young girl tries to present herself as well-read, even though she doesn't read at all.
  • Mobile Suit Gundam: Iron-Blooded Orphans 2nd Season
    (PV)
    Beginning on October 2

    The second season to the 2015 Gundam series.
  • Monster Hunter Stories Ride On
    Beginning on October 1

    An Animated Adaptation of the spin-off game in the Monster Hunter. It features Monster riders who befriend the monsters rather than hunt them.
  • Mysterious Joker 4th season

    The fourth season of the series about the elusive Gentleman Thief.
  • Nanbaka

    A series that focuses on four young prisoners and their desire to escape.
  • Natsume's Book of Friends Go

    The fifth season revolving the youkai-seeing Natsume and his bodyguard as they continues their quest to release youkai from their contracts in the Book of Friends.
  • Ninja Girl & Samurai Master

    A gag manga revolving around a young female ninja and her desire to help her lord Oda Nobunaga.
  • Nyanbo!

    A anime short featuring a cat made of cardboard boxes inspired by Kiyohiko Azuma's Danbo.
  • Occultic;Nine

    Adaptation of a sci-fi light novel from the creator of Steins;Gate. It's about nine people brought together by a NEET's blog specialized in the occult.
  • My Wife is the Student Council President + (Okusama ga Seito Kaichō +)

    Second season.
  • Poco's Udon World
    Beginning on October 8

    A former web designer adopts a strange young boy during a visit to his hometown.
  • Pocket Monsters: Sun and Moon
    Beginning on November 17

    The 6th Pokémon anime series, based on Pokémon Sun and Moon.
  • Scorching Ping Pong Girls

    In a school club dedicated to ping pong, two girls become rivals.
  • Soul Busters

    An adaptation of a novel based on the iconic Romance of the Three Kingdoms.
  • Sound! Euphonium 2
    Beginning on October 5

    The second season of the high school band drama.
  • Tiger Mask W
    PV.
    Beginning on October 1

    A sequel to a wrestling anime from the late 60s.
  • Time Bokan 24
    PV.
    Beginning on October 1

    The latest entry in the Time Bokan meta-series. Two agents travel through time to find the hilarious True History.
  • Touken Ranbu - Hanamaru
    Beginning on October 2

    An Animated Adaptation of the game based when the player character can turn swords into attractive males.
  • Trickster

    It is the 2030s. Kogoro Akechi, the mysterious detective and leader of the Boy Detectives Club, meets Yoshio Kobayashi who was granted immortality from "Unindentifiable Mist" much to his dismay.
  • Uta No Prince Sama Maji LOVE Legend Star

    Fourth season.
  • ViVid Strike!

    Fifth season/spin-off of the main Nanoha continuity taking place 1 year after the events of ViVid.
  • WWW.WORKING!!

    More of WORKING!!, with a different cast.
  • Yuri!!! on Ice

    A sports series about men ice skating.
