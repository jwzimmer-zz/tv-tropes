Summer 2014 Anime

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

This chart is a list of TV anime series that aired between July and September of 2014, along with their Laconic descriptions.

For the previous season see Spring 2014 Anime. For the next season see Fall 2014 Anime.

Here is this season's discussion thread.

TV Series

  • Ai Mai Mi ~Mousou Catastrophe
    PV
    Airing from July 1

    Second season of the Winter 2013 short series.
  • Akame ga Kill!
    Airing from July 6 for 24 episodes.

    In a fantasy world, a group of assassins fight a corrupt empire.
Advertisement:
  • Aldnoah.Zero
    (PV)
    Airing from July 5 for 12 episodes

    A mecha series taking place in an Alternate History where humanity discovered a hypergate to the moon and got in a war with Mars. Gen Urobuchi is at least partially responsible for the concept, and wrote the first three episodes.

  • Bakumatsu Rock
    (PV)
    Airing from July 2

    In an alternate version of the Bakumatsu revolution, the battles are fought through music.
  • Barakamon
    Airing from July 5 for 12 episodes

    A Slice of Life comedy about a city-raised calligrapher that moved out to a rural town.
  • Black Butler Book of Circus
    Airing from July 10 for 10 episodes

    Third season
  • Bladedance of Elementalers
    Airing from July 14

    In a World... where pure maidens are able to make contracts with spirit, our main character becomes the only male elementalist.

  • Blue Spring Ride
    (PV)
    Airing from July 7 for 12 episodes

    A girl meets her first love for the first time in two years, and tries to restart their relationship.
Advertisement:

  • DRAMAtical Murder
    (PV)
    Airing from July 6

    An adaption of the BL mystery visual novel.
  • Fate/kaleid liner PRISMA☆ILLYA 2wei!
    (PV)
    Airing from July 9

    Second season.
  • Francesca Girls Be Ambitious
    (PV)

Hokkaido is being attacked by the shinsengumi and protected by an idol and an exorcist. Also, nearly everyone is a zombie.

Free! Eternal Summer
(PV)
Airing from July 2

2nd season of the popular swimming anime
  • Futsū no Joshikōsei ga Locodol Yattemita (Normal School Girls Tried Being Local Idols)
    (PV)
    Airing from July 3 for 12 episodes

    'Locodol' for short. A pair of highschool girls become idols promoting their town. Based on a 4koma.
  • Monthly Girls' Nozaki-kun
    (PV)
    Airing from July 6 for 12 episodes

    Girl confesses to her crush, but he mistakes her as a fan of his shoujo manga. Hoping to get closer to him, she becomes his assistant.
  • Glasslip
    (PV)
    Airing from July 3

    Original anime by P.A. Works. A 17-year-old girl dreams of becoming a glass artisan.
Advertisement:
  • Hanamonogatari
    (PV)
    Airing on August 16 (4 episodes back-to-back)

    The previously unaired arc of the Monogatari Series' "second season".
  • Hanayamata
    Airing from July 7

    A dancing-themed Schoolgirl Series.
  • Himegoto
    Airing from July 7, as 5-minute episodes

    It's about a boy being forced to go to school dressed like a girl.
  • Jinsei
    (PV)
    Airing from July 6 for 13 episodes

    The main characters writes the life (jinsei) column for the school newspaper, with the help of three girls.
  • Love Stage!!
    (PV)
    Airing from July 9

    A Boys' Love series, about the relationship between an otaku and a famous actor.
  • Magimoji Rurumo
    Airing from July 9 for one cour

    A perverted boy gets tickets that allow an apprentice witch to grant wishes
  • Minarai Diva (Diva-in-training)
    Airing from July 14

    A comedy series about idols. Will be performed live. Yes, this is possible for an anime.
  • Momo Kyun Sword
    Airing from July 8

    The story of Momotarou, re-imagined with busty girls.
  • Nobunaga Concerto
    Airing from July 11

    A boy travels back in time, and ends up taking Oda Nobunaga's place.
  • Persona 4 Golden: The Animation
    Airing from July 10

    Like the previous P4 anime, but with a new side character.
  • PriPara
    Airing from July 5

    Successor to Pretty Rhythm series.
  • Rail Wars!
    (PV)
    Airing from July 3

    The story follows an oddball group that works on a train In a World... where Japan's railways were never privatized.
  • Re:_Hamatora
    Airing from July 7 for one cour

    Second season
  • Rokujyouma no Shinryakusha!?
    (PV)
    Airing from July 11

    Harem comedy. A boy ends up sharing his tiny apartment with a variety of supernatural girls.
  • Sabagebu!
    (PV)
    Airing from July 6.

    A gag comedy starring the members of a girls' airsoft club.
  • Sailor Moon Crystal
    (PV)
    Airing from July 5, with two episodes per month, for 26 episodes

    A new adaptation of the famous Magical Girl manga.
  • Sengoku Basara Judge End
    Airing from July 5 for 12 episodes

    Third season
  • Shirogane no Ishi Argevollen (Silver Will Argevollen)
    (PV)
    Airing from July 3

    An original Real Robot series. That's really all there is to know.
  • Shounen Hollywood -Holly Stage for 49-
    (PV)
    Airing from July 5

    It's about a male idol group. Takes place 15 years after the novel Shounen Hollywood.
  • Sin Strange+
    Airing from June 28

    Second season.
  • Space Dandy Season 2
    Airing from July 5 (in America) for 13 episodes

    Second half.
  • Sword Art Online II
    (PV)
    Airing from July 5 for 24 episodes

    Second season.
  • Terror in Resonance
    (PV)
    Airing from July 10 for 11 episodes

    A bomb exploded in the heart of Tokyo. The terrorists are two teenagers that call themselves "Sphinx". Now they launch a huge game that involves the entire Japanese population. Soundtrack to be composed by Yoko Kanno.
  • Tokyo ESP
    Airing from July 11

    After certain bizarre events, a number of people gain psychic powers.
  • Tokyo Ghoul
    (PV)
    Airing from July 3 for 12 episodes

    A boy is turned into a man-eating monster.
  • Yama no Susume 2
    Airing from July 9, for 24 15-minute episodes

    Second season
  • Yami Shibai 2

    Second season
