Tear Tropes

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Laconic
  • Create New

The most beautiful of princesses will cry tears as sparkling as diamonds, and as soft as ermine.

"To weep is to make less the depth of grief."
— William Shakespeare, Henry VI Part 3
Advertisement:

Tropes about wet stuff in eyes.

Compare Sadness Tropes, Gray Rain of Depression.

Contrast Smile Tropes, Happiness Tropes.

Tropes:

  • Berserker Tears: I am so angry that I start crying!
  • Biting the Handkerchief: I am so sad that I start biting my tissues!
  • Bleed 'em and Weep: I just killed someone... *begins crying*
  • Broken Tears: I am losing my mind and I just... start crying!
  • Crocodile Tears: WAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHH! I am crying! This is just for attention, and sometimes I'm sad already while I do this!
  • Cry Cute: You look so cute when you are crying!
  • Cry into Chest: Let me cry into your chest please!
  • Cry Laughing: HAHAHA that's so funny! *breaks down into tears*
  • Crying After Sex: The sex was so good it made me cry!
  • Crying a River: Wow, my tears could fill a bathtub at least!
  • Crying Critters: Why is this cat crying?
    • Advertisement:
  • Crying Indian: I am an Indian and I am crying in reference to a commercial.
  • Everybody Cries: You aren't crying alone. You are crying with all of us
  • Fell Asleep Crying: I cried myself to sleep last night because I was sad
  • Heartbreak and Ice Cream: The only thing that will cure my sadness is a tub of ice cream.
  • He Will Not Cry, So I Cry for Him: He won't cry nor express grief? I will do it for him.
  • I'm Crying, but I Don't Know Why: Why am I crying? There is no reason to cry yet there is one!
  • Incriminating Indifference: You did this crime because you aren't showing any kind of emotions right now!
  • Inelegant Blubbering: I am so sad I can't even speak english anymore and there is snot running down my nose!
  • Manly Tears: I am a man and yet I shed tears sometimes
  • Men Don't Cry: Real men don't cry ever because it shows weakness! Men cry too
    • Advertisement:
  • My Eyes Are Leaking: Is this what you humans call sadness?
  • Ocular Gushers: I have so many tears it creates a waterfall!
  • Onion Tears: Cutting up onions make you cry.
  • Prone to Tears: It is so incredibly easy to make me cry!
  • Puppy-Dog Eyes: Pleaaasssssee do something for me because my eyes are huge and I'm shedding tears.
  • Running Away to Cry: I can't believe this... I must run away and cry!
  • Sand In My Eyes: I am not crying... it's just something is in my eyes.
  • Single Tear: It's so beautiful I can only shed one tear
  • Sparkling Stream of Tears: Instead of crying tears, I cry bright sparkles instead.
  • Swiss Army Tears: Crying can solve a lot of problems.
  • Tear Jerker: This material may make you cry because of how gloomy it is
  • Tearful Smile: *sniffling* I can't stop smiling! *begins crying*
  • Tears from a Stone: Why is this statue crying? It shouldn't be able to express emotions let alone cry!
  • Tears of Blood: Instead of crying tears... I cry blood or something that isn't tears
  • Tears of Fear: Please help me... I am so scared I could cry.
  • Tears of Joy: I am so happy I could cry!
  • Tears of Remorse: I did a bad thing and as a result, I cry.
  • Tender Tears: You are so kind and thoughtful to me that... that... I can't help but cry!
  • Trying Not to Cry: I am not gonna cry... I am not gonna cry... I am not gonna cry
  • Unable to Cry: This event is so grim that I am unable to process it and grieve.
  • Your Makeup Is Running: My tears are ruining my makeup!
