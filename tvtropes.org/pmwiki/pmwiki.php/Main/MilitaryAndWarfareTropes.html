"It is well that war is so terrible — lest we should grow too fond of it."
—Robert E. Lee

War stories are not quite as convention-laden as The Western or commedia dell'arte, but it is a near thing. And The Army is frequently featured in such stories for some reason... Here are some things you may rely on.
