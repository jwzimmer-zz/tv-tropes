Prideful Tropes

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

Welcome, you inferior troper! Feast your little eyes on this awesome index which deals with pride and prideful people!

See also Dignity Tropes, which I have. Go to These Tropes Love to Brag which is my personal favorite. Contrast Humility Tropes, which are for losers by the way.

Here are my superior tropes!

  • Acquired Situational Narcissism: I'm popular now! Therefore, I can look down on you losers all I want!
    • Advertisement:
  • Arrogant Kung-Fu Guy: I'm more skilled than you! I can knock you out with my eyes closed!
  • At Least I Admit It: Admitting my many flaws and shortcomings, no matter how bad, makes me better than all of you!!
  • Awesome Ego: Yeah, I'm a braggart, but the fans love me for it!
  • Big Breast Pride: Check out my big sexy boobs, guys!
  • Big Ego, Hidden Depths: Just because I'm an egomaniac doesn't mean there isn't more than meets the eye. And that makes me twice as awesome!
  • Blasphemous Boast: I'm so awesome, you should call me God!
  • Break the Haughty: NOOOOOOO! How could this happen to me?! My pride...ruined!
  • Can't Take Criticism: How dare you criticize me! There's absolutely nothing wrong with me!
  • Carload of Cool Kids: Hey there, nerd! Check out the cool shiny car we're on and you aren't!
  • Cats Are Superior: Meow. I am by far the best animal in the world and every other species is worthless!
    • Advertisement:
  • Dancing Royalty: I'm so great at dancing, I'm popular enough to be treated like royalty!
  • Determinator: I'm too proud to admit when it's too hard!
  • Devil Complex: I am Satan! Bow before me!
  • Don't You Dare Pity Me!: Screw you! I don't need pity from someone inferior like you!
  • Experience Entitlement: I have more experience than you rookies, and thus, I have every right to show it!
  • Fat and Proud: I'm proud of being fat and I'm not afraid to flaunt my fatness!
  • The Fighting Narcissist: I'll kill you if you tarnish my amazing beauty!
  • Friendship Denial: Oh, please! That low-class geek isn't my buddy! (friendship version of She Is Not My Girlfriend)
  • Gagging on Your Words: I...I...
  • A God Am I: I'm superior to you because I'm a god! Bow down to me!
  • Godhood Seeker: I should be a god! I should have absolute power!
    • Advertisement:
  • Grudging "Thank You": There's one thing I want to say to you... um... thanks?
  • Haughty Help: My job is to serve you, but I'm still better than you.
  • Haughty "Hmph": Hmph! How inferior you are.
  • Hidden Heart of Gold: Yeah, I do some nice things, but don't tell anyone about it, 'cause I have a reputation to keep.
  • Holier Than Thou: I am the most righteous person on the face of this earth!
  • How the Mighty Have Fallen: I was once great, but now I'm nothing!
  • "I Am Great!" Song: I'll sing a song dedicated to how awesome I really am!
  • Inferiority Superiority Complex: I'm better than you! ... I'm pathetic!
  • Insecure Protagonist, Arrogant Antagonist: Pffft, I'm so much better than that other guy!
  • Insufferable Genius: I'm smarter than you!
  • It's All About Me: Me, my greatness, and my interests are more valuable than you!
  • Know-Nothing Know-It-All: You take that back! I know a lot more than you do!
  • Leave Me Alone!: I don't need your help! I'm too great for that.
  • Look on My Works, Ye Mighty, and Despair!: A proud civilization builds spectacular things to prove their superiority, setting them up for an eventual karmic undoing.
  • Narcissist: I love myself. I'm perfect! You all must love me, too.
  • Never My Fault: It's your fault, not mine!
  • Petite Pride: Who needs big breasts? Flat-chested girls are awesome!
  • Pompous Political Pundit: A fictional, aggressive, political talking head in a satire.
  • Pride: It's what this perfect index is all about!
  • Pride Before a Fall: The story opens with my pride broken.
  • Pride Parade: I'm proud of being queer!
  • Proud Beauty: I'm proud of my good looks! I know you want me.
  • The Proud Elite: I'm handsome, fit, intelligent, sophisticated, and rich! I also don't like low-life losers such as yourself!
  • Proud Peacock: I'm the most attractive bird of them all! Everyone should adore me now!
  • Proud to Be a Geek: I'm proud of being a geek!
  • Rightly Self-Righteous: I'm morally superior than you, and the best part about it is I'm actually right! Sucks to be you, does it?
  • Small Name, Big Ego: What do you mean you've never heard of me? I'm a big deal!
  • So Proud of You: You make me proud.
  • Social Climber: I'm more determined than anyone to climb up the social ladder.
  • Smug Snake: You can never surpass me, you fool!
  • Smug Super: I know I'm more powerful than you and I let you know it!
  • Too Clever by Half: How could my incomparable intelligence be my downfall?!
  • The Unapologetic: I don't have to apologize. I'm too great for that kind of stuff.
  • Ungrateful Bastard: Don't press your luck. A proud person like me doesn't have to thank anyone.
  • Wrote the Book: Don't tell me about that, I wrote the book on it!
Have you had a wonderful time reading these great tropes of mine? I'm sure you'll praise me and tell me how great all of them are.
