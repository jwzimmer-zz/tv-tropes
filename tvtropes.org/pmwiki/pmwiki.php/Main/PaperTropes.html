Paper Tropes

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

Index for paper stuff: letters, documents, contracts, reports, homework, notes, messages, notebooks, posters, newspapers, toilet paper and so on, and various related plots.

Compare Bookish Tropes, Box Tropes, Mail and Delivery Tropes and Photography and Illustration.

Badass on Paper, Paper Cutting, Paper People and Paper Tiger are unrelated.

Art, crafting, creating and stuff made of paper

  • Dunce Cap: A student who misbehaves or does poorly in school is punished by being made to sit in the corner and wear a pointy cap made of paper.
    • Advertisement:
  • Improvised Umbrella: Can be improvised with a large piece of paper, usually a newspaper.
  • Love Doodles
  • Nightmare Fuel Coloring Book
  • Significant Sketch Book
  • 1000 Origami Cranes

Contracts and documents

  • Conveniently Interrupted Document
  • Destroy the Evidence: If the evidence is a document, e.g. a letter, a contract or something similar.
  • Eat the Evidence: If the evidence in question is something made of paper.
  • Fictional Document
  • Magically Binding Contract
  • Read the Fine Print
  • Unreadable Disclaimer

Letters and messages

  • Blood-Stained Letter
  • Chain Letter
  • Dead Man Writing
  • "Dear John" Letter
  • Epistolary Novel: Consists of letters.
  • Love Letter
  • Love Letter Lunacy
  • Message in a Bottle
  • Obsessive Love Letter
  • Passing Notes in Class
  • Strongly Worded Letter
  • Voiceover Letter

Newspapers

  • Authentication by Newspaper
  • Extra! Extra! Read All About It!
  • Newspaper Dating
  • Spinning Paper

Paper bags

  • Brown Bag Mask
  • Hyperventilation Bag: Breathing into a paper bag to calm down.
  • Paper-Bag Popping

Advertisement:

Posters

  • Fauxtivational Poster
  • Just Take the Poster
  • Poster-Gallery Bedroom
  • "Wanted!" Poster

Toilet paper

  • Only Useful as Toilet Paper: A piece of writing is considered so poor that its only good use is for wiping your ass or some other purpose that involves defiling the paper.
  • Toilet Paper Prank: A prank where you wrap rolls of toilet paper around everything.
  • Toilet Paper Trail: Someone leaves the bathroom with a piece of toilet paper stuck to their foot.
  • There Will Be Toilet Paper: People who cut themselves shaving always use bits of toilet paper to stop the bleeding.

Writing

  • Diary
  • Rewriting Reality
  • Secret Diary
  • Scrapbook Story

Written assignments and homework

  • Completely Off-Topic Report: A student is asked to write a paper, but somehow has it written on a subject that has nothing to do with the topic that was assigned.
  • A Dog Ate My Homework: A student who neglected to do their homework (frequently something written on paper) avoids getting in trouble by claiming their dog ate the homework.
  • Writing Lines: The lines can be assigned to be written into a notebook/piece of paper.

Other

  • Break-Up Bonfire
  • Burn Baby Burn
  • Cardboard Box of Unemployment: When a person gets fired, they have to carry their possessions in a cardboard box.
  • Kids Prefer Boxes: Children prefer playing with the box rather than the toy the box contained.
  • Newspaper-Thin Disguise: Holding up a newspaper or other form of reading material to avoid being seen.
  • No-Paper Future
  • Padding The Paper: Someone is assigned to write a document and pads it out by adding unnecessary words, larger fonts, pictures, etc.
  • Paper Destruction of Anger
  • Paper Fan of Doom
  • Paper Master
  • Paper Talisman
  • Pen Pals: Friends who exchange letters.
  • Press Hat: A piece of paper that says PRESS tucked in a reporter's hat.
  • Ripped from the Phone Book: Tearing out a phone book page instead of writing the number down.
  • Scantron Picture: Someone taking a Scantron test fools around by filling in the dots to make a picture.
  • Stalker Shrine
  • Wastebasket Ball
  • Writer's Block Montage
