Summer 2016 Anime

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

This chart is a list of known TV anime series that aired between July and September 2016, along with their Laconic descriptions.

For the previous season see Spring 2016 Anime. For the next season see Fall 2016 Anime.

The discussion thread is here.

TV Series

  • 91 Days
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 8, run of 12 episodes.

    In a prohibition era mob town, a young man befriends the son of a mafia don while plotting revenge against them for murdering his family seven years ago.
    • Advertisement:
  • Active Raid 2nd
    Beginning on July 10

    A continuation of a futuristic cop show that aired in Winter 2016 Anime.
  • Alderamin on the Sky
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 8

    A lazy, war-hating lecher turns out to be a tactical genius.
  • Amanchu!
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 8 and running for 12 episodes

    A girl who loves the ocean and diving discovers the joy of scuba diving. From the author of Aria.
  • Ange Vierge
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 9

    A portal opens and fuses three worlds, while giving teenage girls mysterious powers.
  • B-Project Kodou Ambitious
    Beginning on July 2, 13 episodes

    An anime about a girl managing male idols.
  • Bananya
    Beginning on July 4, 13 episodes.

    A look into the secret lives of cute cat characters who live inside bananas. Episodes are three minute shorts.
  • Battery
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 14

    A pitcher who has lost faith in his own talent is approached by a catcher that wants to form a partnership with him.
    • Advertisement:
  • Berserk (2016)
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 1; 12 episodes in Summer 2016, second season airing Spring 2017.

    A BFS-wielding swordsman suspends his Roaring Rampage of Revenge against demonkind to rescue his Amnesiac Lover from a Knight Templar priest. Based on the Dark Fantasy manga Berserk.
  • Cheer Danshi
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 5

    Male cheerleading.
  • Cute High Earth Defense Club LOVE! LOVE!
    Beginning on July 7

    Second season
  • Danganronpa 3 The End of Hope's Peak Academy
    2 episodes per week, beginning on July 11 and 14

    Part of the Danganronpa franchise, tying up the Hope's Peak Academy storyline, split into two series airing simultaneously. Not an adaptation of the third game, but rather a new plot revolving around the previous two seasons.
  • DAYS
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 2

    Soccer story about a passionate but average player, and an isolated genius.
    • Advertisement:
  • D.Gray-Man Hallow
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 4, 13 episodes

    Second season.
  • The Disastrous Life of Saiki K.
    Weekday morning broadcasts beginning July 4, weekly late-night broadcasts beginning July 10

    A gag manga about an incredibly powerful psychic.
  • Fate/kaleid liner PRISMA☆ILLYA 3rei
    Beginning on July 6

    Fourth season
  • Food Wars!: Ni No Sara
    Beginning on July 2, 13 episodes

    Second season
  • The High School Life of a Fudanshi
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 5

    A comedy about a boy that's a fan of Boys' Love and his friends.
  • Handa-kun
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 7

    A prequel to Barakamon , focusing on Handa's highschool years.
  • Hatsukoi Monster
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 2

    A highschool student is saved by a mysterious stranger. After she successfully asks him out she is shocked to learn that her new boyfriend is a fifth grader.
  • The Heroic Legend of Arslan: Fuujin Ranbu
    Beginning on July 3, 8 episodes long

    Second season of the Spring 2015 anime.
  • Hitori no Shita: The Outcast
    Beginning on July 9, 12 episodes

    A boy is attacked by zombies and saved by a sword-wielding girl.
  • Hybrid × Heart Magias Academy Ataraxia
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 5

    A boy visits a military school and finds out that he has the ability to power girls up through indecent acts.
  • Kono Bijutsubu Niwa Mondai Ga Aru
    (CM)
    Beginning on July 7

    A series focusing on the quirky members of a middle-school Art Club.
  • Love Live! Sunshine!!
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 2

    A Love Live! spinoff/sequel, following a new group of girls living in a beach town.
  • Mob Psycho 100
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 11

    A boy with psychic powers has to keep his emotional capacity from reaching 100% or he will explode. So he tries to limit his powers in order to have a normal life. From the author of One-Punch Man.
  • New Game!
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 4

    Cute girls working at a game studio.
  • Orange
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 3

    A girl gets a letter from her future self cryptically tells her to keep an eye out on the New Transfer Student.
  • Ozmafia!!
    Beginning on July 6, running as shorts

    Reverse harem with an amnesiac girl rescued by a mafia family.
  • Puzzle & Dragons Cross
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 4

    An adaptation of the popular game Puzzle & Dragons.
  • Qualidea Code
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 9

    In a World... invaded by strange enemies, children have awoken from cold sleep with supernatural powers. They've now set up defensive cities.
  • Regalia: The Three Sacred Stars
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 7

    The young empress of Enastoria fights robots with her "older sister", a little girl who transforms into a Humongous Mecha called a Regalia.
  • ReLIFE
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 1, 13 episodes

    An unemployed man takes a pill that turns him back into a high school student so he can redo his life.
  • Rewrite
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 2, 13 episodes

    An adaptation of a Key/Visual Arts VN about a boy in an occult research club. Said to cover a previously untold "sixth route".
  • Scared Rider Xechs
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 5

    A group of young men were assembled to fight alien invaders, but the invasion suddenly stopped, so they now have to adjust to ordinary life and their new female instructor and commander.
  • Servamp
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 5

    One day a black cat crosses a path of a Ordinary Highschool Student, said cat turns out to be a vampire. The student later finds himself dragged into a conflict between Vampires and humans.
  • Show by Rock!! Short!!
    Beginning on July 4

    A series of shorts of the 2015 anime.
  • Sweetness & Lightning
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 4, 12 episodes

    A recently widowed math teacher tries to raise his young daughter, with some help from one of his students. Emphasis on cooking.
  • Taboo-Tattoo
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 4

    A martial artist one day receives a tattoo from a random homeless man he defended, which grant him supernatural powers. This leads to him being dragged into a cross-national conspiracy.
  • Tales of Zestiria The X
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 3

    An anime adaptation of the latest game from the Tales Series.
  • The Morose Mononokean
    (PV)
    Beginning on June 28

    A highschool student ends up helping a surly classmate of his with exorcism.
  • Time Travel Shoujo ~Mari Waka to 8-nin no Kagakusha-tachi~
    (PV)
    Beginning on July 9, 12 episodes

    Educational anime about girls meeting famous scientists.
  • Tsukiuta
    (CM)
    Beginning on July 6

    An anime adapted from a series of music and drama CDs about the months personified as attractive men.
