Attraction Tropes

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

He's a bad boy Hunk, and she's a Fiery Redhead. Why shouldn't they have the hots for each other?
Advertisement:

Characters are drawn to someone for some reason. The person has sex appeal and is called beautiful, sexy, hot, handsome, desirable, or plain attractive. Might be Love Interests or just lust interests.

Can lead to Kissing Tropes and Sex Tropes or Dating Tropes and Flirting and Courtship. More realistic than Rule of Sexy and more for the character than Fanservice, Fanservice Costumes or Nudity Tropes.

Advertisement:

Tropes:

Sub-categories:
  • Attraction to Outliers
    A character is attractive because they aren't socially ideal.
  • Delicate Is Beautiful
    A character who's delicate and fragile is considered attractive.
  • Agents Dating: Secret agents are most attracted to other secret agents.
  • All Amazons Want Hercules: Tough women tend to be attracted to men who are just as strong as, if not stronger than them.
  • All Girls Want Bad Boys: Moody, anti-social bad boys attract a lot of attention from girls.
  • All Guys Want Cheerleaders: Cheerleaders tend to be considered desirable to guys.
  • All Guys Want Sorority Women: A woman is likely to be considered desirable if she's in a sorority.
  • Amazon Chaser: This guy is attracted to girls who kick ass.
  • Amazonian Beauty: A girl with an athletic build who is also quite beautiful, often sought after by an Amazon Chaser.
    • Advertisement:
  • Aroused by Their Voice: The sound of a character's voice is a turn on.
  • Artists Are Attractive: A character is attractive due to their artistic talents
  • Attractive Bent-Gender: A character looks attractive when disguised as a member of the opposite sex.
  • Attractive Bent Species: A character changes into a highly attractive member of another species.
  • Attractive Zombie: Undead corpses can even be found attractive.
  • Attractiveness Discrimination: Attractive people tend to be treated better than unattractive people.
  • Attractiveness Isolation: They're very attractive to the point that people are too intimidated to ask them out.
  • Big Beautiful Man: A heavyset man who is considered attractive.
  • Big Beautiful Woman: A heavyset woman who is considered attractive.
  • Big Brother Attraction: Character feels attracted to their older brother.
  • Bigot with a Crush: A character has romantic and/or sexual feelings for a member of a group whom they are prejudiced against.
  • Celeb Crush: Celebrities have many fans, some of which who are attracted to them.
  • Chick Magnet: A guy who attracts a lot of ladies.
  • Chubby Chaser: A character who is attracted to more heavyset people.
  • Crush Filter: A person appears ethereally attractive through the viewpoint of someone who's crushing on them.
  • Cupid's Arrow: Attraction to people is caused by the god of love, Cupid.
  • Devoted to You: Being committed to the one you're attracted to.
  • Distracted by My Own Sexy: You find yourself attractive.
  • Distracted by the Sexy: Someone is so beautiful it can be used to distract others.
  • Elegant Classical Musician: Characters who play music are very desirable.
  • Endearingly Dorky: A character's dorkiness makes them highly desirable.
  • Even the Girls Want Her: A girl is so attractive that other girls love her.
  • Even the Guys Want Him: A guy who's so attractive that other guys love him.
  • Everyone Loves Blondes: Blondes tend to get the most attention from people.
  • Everybody Wants the Hermaphrodite: Since they have both genitals, intersex characters are found attractive by everyone regardless of gender.
  • Evil Is Sexy: Villainous characters are very attractive.
  • Fair Cop: An attractive police officer.
  • Fairy Sexy: Attractive fairies.
  • Firemen Are Hot: Male firefighters tend to be quite handsome.
  • Gay Guy Seeks Popular Jock: The star quarterback attracts the attention of a gay guy.
  • Geeky Turn-On: Being turned on by how smart someone is.
  • Girls Like Musicians: Girls are attracted to boys who can play music.
  • Give Geeks a Chance: Someone is attracted to a nerd because Nerds Are Sexy.
  • Good-Looking Privates: Men in uniform tend to be studs.
  • Has a Type: Characters have specific kinds of people they're attracted to.
  • Head-Turning Beauty: A girl is so hot, guys drool and throw themselves at her.
  • Hello, Attorney!: Attractive lawyers.
  • Heroes Want Redheads: The Hero is attracted to redheads.
  • Hospital Hottie: An attractive doctor/nurse.
  • Hot as Hell: Attractive infernal beings.
  • Hot Consort: The attractive spouse of a royal.
  • Hot God: A divine being who is very beautiful.
  • Hot Librarian: Not all librarians are stuffy old hags.
  • Hot Men at Work: When attractive men go to work, expect lots of Fanservice out of it
  • Hot Scientist: Attractive scientists.
  • Hot Teacher: Attractive school teachers.
  • Hot Witch: A witch who is a bombshell rather than an ugly, old hag.
  • Hunk: A tall muscular man who is very handsome, often comes with a Lantern Jaw of Justice.
  • If It's You, It's Okay: A character finds themself attracted to someone outside of their preferred gender.
  • Inconvenient Attraction: A character is attracted to somebody and they hate it.
  • Informed Attractiveness: They are considered attractive in-universe despite what the audience might think.
  • Inherently Attractive Profession: When a job itself is part of someone's appeal.
  • Lady in Red: A sexy seductress wearing red.
  • Like Goes with Like: Characters are only attracted to those of the same ethnicity as them.
  • Likes Older Men: A female character is attracted to guys who are much older than her.
  • Likes Older Women: A male character is attracted to girls who are much older than him.
  • Living Aphrodisiac: A character that can manipulate pheromones to make people be attracted to them.
  • Love Is in the Air: A Love Potion causes a bunch of people to feel attraction towards each other.
  • Love Potion: A magical potion that causes a person to feel romantic attraction for another.
  • Lustful Melt: Characters are so attracted to somebody they literally melt.
  • Moe: They're cute and huggable, so naturally they get lots of attention.
  • Mrs. Robinson: An older woman who is attracted to much younger men.
  • Mixtape of Love: Mixtapes are a way to show your affection for someone.
  • Nerds Are Sexy: Nerds are considered attractive rather than repulsive.
  • Only Has Same-Sex Admirers: This character attracts people of the same sex but never anyone of the opposite sex.
  • Power Is Sexy: Having loads of power makes a character desirable.
  • Pretty Boy: A guy who is attractive in a delicate and feminine way.
  • Race Fetish: A character is attracted to people of a different ethnicity than them.
  • Raven Hair, Ivory Skin: Jet black hair and pale skin make a character very attractive.
  • Right-Hand Hottie: A character's attractive assistant.
  • Rivalry as Courtship: Attraction that starts with two people competing against each other.
  • Romantic Fusion: Attraction that results in the lovers performing a Fusion Dance.
  • Seductive Mummy: An attractive, undead mummy.
  • Sexy Priest: A man of God who's considered attractive, often has a Vow of Celibacy for an extra Forbidden Fruit angle.
  • Sexy Secretary: Attractive female assistants.
  • Sexy Stewardess: Attractive flight attendants.
  • Silver Fox: An attractive and visibly old character.
  • Single Girl Seeks Most Popular Guy: The shy, unpopular girl is attracted to the most handsome and most popular guy in school.
  • Single Woman Seeks Good Man: A woman is attracted to sweet and compassionate men.
  • Smells Sexy: Attraction based on how someone smells.
  • Speech-Impeded Love Interest: Attraction towards a character with a speech disability.
  • Stacy's Mom: A middle-aged man/woman that is crushed on by someone much younger than them.
  • Stupid Sexy Flanders: Being heterosexual doesn't stop you from admiring an attractive person of the same sex.
  • Stupid Sexy Friend: Feeling attraction towards someone who's supposed to be only a good friend.
  • Sweet on Polly Oliver: A guy is attracted to a girl who is disguised as a guy.
  • Tall, Dark, and Handsome: Tall stature + dark hair and dark eyes = very desirable.
  • Teamwork Seduction: Two characters team up to attract someone's attention.
  • A Threesome Is Hot: Being turned on at the thought of having sex with two people at the same time.
  • Troubled, but Cute: Despite being anti-social and possibly a criminal, they're considered desirable.
  • Unwanted Harem: A bunch of people are attracted to this character, much to their chagrin.
  • Villainesses Want Heroes: Evil women tend to be attracted to heroic men.
  • Weakness Turns Her On: Guys with softer, weaker (by typical masculine standards) dispositions attract this girl's attention.
  • Where Da White Women At?: Black men tend to be most attracted to white women.
  • World's Most Beautiful Woman: This woman is truly the fairest in all the land and has many admirers likewise.
