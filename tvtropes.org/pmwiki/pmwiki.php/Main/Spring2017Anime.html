Spring 2017 Anime

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

This chart is a list of known TV anime series that aired between April and June 2017, along with their Laconic descriptions.A note on The King's Avatar The Animated Adaptation for The King's Avatar (also known as Quan Zhi Gao Shou or Full-Time Expert) is listed on some sites such as MyAnimeList as a part of the Spring 2017 Anime lineup; however, since it is a Chinese-production without any Japanese inputs whatsoever, for indexing purposes, it will be left off of this page.

Advertisement:

For the previous season see Winter 2017 Anime.

The discussion thread is here.

TV Series

  • Akashic Records of Bastard Magic Instructor (Roku de Nashi Majutsu Koshi to Akashic Records)
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 4

    A lazy bum becomes a part-time teacher at a prestigious magic academy, even though his sorcery is really weak.
  • Alice & Zoroku
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 2

    A young girl with the power to materialize anything that she imagines meets with a cynical old man.
  • Ani Ni Tsukeru Kusuri Wa Nai
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 7

    A comedy series about a pair of siblings that constantly annoy each other, but will stand by each other when the need arises. Based on Chinese webcomic Please Take My Brother Away.
  • The Anonymous Noise (Fukumenkei Noise)
    PV1, PV2
    Premiere Date: April 11

    A girl with an amazing singing talent reunites with her two childhood friends, but during their time apart a lot has changed.
Advertisement:
  • Atom: The Beginning
    PV1, PV2
    Premiere Date: April 15

    A Prequel to the classic Astro Boy manga, which will detail the events that lead up to the birth of Anime's icon.
  • Attack on Titan 2nd Season
    [1]
    Premiere Date: April 1

    The sequel season to the worldwide phenomena. Humanity continues its struggles against the Titan horde.
  • Armed Girl's Machiavellism (Buso Shojo Machiavellism)
    PV1, PV2
    Premiere Date: April 5

    In a particular high school, female students hold dominance over male students by equipping themselves with large weapons. A boy who is a master of martial arts transfers there and almost immediately stands up against the status quo.
  • Berserk (2016) 2nd season
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 7

    Second season.
  • Beyblade Burst God
    Premiere Date: April 3

    Second season.
  • BORUTO: Naruto Next Generations
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 1

    A Spin-Offspring Sequel to the long-running Naruto series.
Advertisement:
  • THE iDOLM@STER: Cinderella Girls Theater (Cinderella Girls Gekijo)
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 4

    A comedic Spin-Off of the THE iDOLM@STER: Cinderella Girls series. Based on a 4-koma.
  • Clockwork Planet
    PV1, PV2
    Premiere Date: April 7

    Earth has been completely destroyed and then rebuilt with nothing but gears. 1000 years later, a boy obsessed with machines encounters an automaton girl.
  • Duel Masters 2017
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 2

    The son of the Duel Masters Victory protagonist must save an alternate dimension by summoning monsters via a trading card game.
  • The Eccentric Family 2
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 9

    Second season about a dysfunctional shape-shifting tanuki family living in modern day Kyoto.
  • Eromanga Sensei
    CM
    Premiere Date: April 9

    A young light novel writer discovers that his Hikikomori little sister is the artist behind the perverted art in his books.
  • Frame Arms Girls
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 4

    A high school girl receives a parcel that contain a small AI-controlled doll with armaments for it. Based on a model kit line.
  • Future Card Buddyfight Battsu
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 1

    4th season about teens summoning and forming contracts with monsters from another worlds via a card game.
  • Granblue Fantasy the Animation
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 2

    In a World... in the sky, a boy and a girl must escape the clutches of an evil empire while searching for a legendary island. Based on a browser/smartphone game.
  • Grimoire of Zero (Zero kara Hajimeru Maho no Sho)
    PV1, PV2,PV3
    Premiere Date: April 10

    A half-human, half-beast mercenary forms a pact with a witch called Zero to find a spellbook of immense power, in exchange of being turned into a human.
  • Hinako Note
    PV1, PV2
    Premiere Date: April 7

    A shy girl that naturally attracts animals moves to Tokyo and meets a cavalcade of eccentric characters, first and foremost her book-eating roommate.
  • ID-0
    Announcement, PV
    Premiere Date: April 9

    After being rescued from space pirates, a girl pilots a robot for a excavating team.
  • Idol Time PriPara
    CM
    Premiere Date: April 4

    A sequel to the PriPara, dealing with a new character becoming an idol via a magic ticket.
  • Kabukibu!
    Announcement, PV
    Premiere Date: April 6

    A boy with deep passion for the kabuki theater wants to start a club centering around it in his high school.
  • Kado: The Right Answer (Seikaisuru Kado)
    Teaser, Trailer
    Premiere Date: April 7

    A young politician must act as the intermediary between the human race and aliens that appeared in a giant cube in Japan.
  • Kenka Bancho Otome Girl Beats Boys
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 12

    A teenage girl must pose as her long-lost twin brother at a school full of bloodthirsty (and handsome) delinquents. Based on an Otome Visual Novel spinoff of the video game series Kenka Bancho.
  • The Laughing Salesman (Warau Salesman NEW)
    Trailer
    Premiere Date: April 3

    An ever-smiling salesman goes around fulfilling people's desires, but always in a way that will ruin their lives.
  • Love Rice (Love Kome: We Love Rice)
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 5

    Five boys representing different types of rice form a club in order to show to their school the appeal of rice.
  • Love Tyrant (Renai Boukun)
    CM, PV
    Premiere Date: April 6

    A high-schooler is forced by a cupid to kiss someone within 24 hours or he will die. Matters are complicated when he opts to kiss the cupid, which does not sit well with the Yandere that's crushing on him.
  • My Hero Academia 2nd Season
    PV1, PV2, PV3
    Premiere Date: April 1

    Second season about the school for aspiring superheroes.
  • Natsume's Book Of Friends 6 (Natsume Yuujin-Chou Roku)
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 11

    Sixth season about a boy that tries to free the Youkai bounded by his grandmother's notebook.
  • Ninja Girl & Samurai Master 2nd (Nobunaga no Shinobi: Ise Kanegasaki-hen)
    Premiere Date: April 7

    Second season about an air headed but skilled shinobi under Oda Nobunaga's servitude.
  • Rage Of Bahamut Virgin Soul (Shingeki no Bahamut Virgin Soul)
    PV1, PV2, PV3
    Premiere Date: April 8

    A sequel to Rage of Bahamut: Genesis set 10 years later and focusing on a new female protagonist with super strength and a weakness for pretty boys.
  • Red Ash: Gearworld [2]
    Premiere Date: March

    An adaptation of Keiju Inafune's Red Ash: The Indelible Legend following Beck and Tyger meeting Call and their first adventure together.
  • Re:CREATORS
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 8

    Characters from various form of media start to pop up in the "real world" and must come to terms that their entire lives are fictional.
  • RIN-NE Season 3
    Premiere Date: April 8

    3rd season about an impoverished god of death.
  • The Royal Tutor (Ōshitsu Kyōshi Heine; Königliche Familie Lehrer)
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 5

    A teacher with a child-like stature is tasked by the king to tutor his four sons.
  • Saekano: How to Raise a Boring Girlfriend Flat (Saenai Heroine no Sodatekata ♭)
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 14

    Second season about a group of high school students trying to make their own visual novel.
  • Sagrada Reset
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 5

    In a seaside town where over a half of the citizens have some sort of superpower, a boy with photographic memory teams up with a girl that can reverse time.
  • Sakura Quest
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 5

    Five girls are hired as tourism ambassadors with hopes that it will bring business to a small town out in the boonies.
  • Seven Mortal Sins (Sin: Nanatsu no Taizai)
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 15

    After being cast down from Heaven, Lucifer decides to make herself the ruler of Hell by facing against demons embodying the seven deadly sins. Yes, she.
  • The Silver Guardian (Gin no Guardian)
    PV1, PV2
    Premiere Date: April 1

    A skilled gamer must venture inside a tomb raiding game in order to save his classmate. Based on a Chinese webcomic.
  • The Snack World
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 13

    In a fantasy world with convenience stores and smartphones, a young adventurer sets out to avenge the destruction of his village.
  • Starmyu 2nd season
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 4

    Second season about 5 music students perfecting their craft of dancing.
  • Sword Oratoria: Is It Wrong to Try to Pick Up Girls in a Dungeon? On the Side (Dungeon ni Deai wo Motomeru no wa Machigatteiru Darouka Gaiden: Sword Oratoria)
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 15

    A Spin-Off of the Is It Wrong to Try to Pick Up Girls in a Dungeon? series, focusing on the exploits of the Loki Familia - especially its "sword princess", Aiz Wallenstein.
  • Tomica Hyper Rescue Drive Head
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 15

    In the near future, four grade-schoolers are enlisted to join the Hyper Police, where they pilot Transforming Mecha.
  • Tsugumomo
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 2

    A boy finds out that his passed down sash can take form of a beautiful girl.
  • Tsukigakirei (Tsuki ga Kirei)
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 7

    A Slice of Life series following four middle schoolers and the relationships between them.
  • Twin Angel BREAK
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 7

    A Spin-Off to the Kaitou Tenshi Twin Angel series, where a new pair of Magical Girls fight evil. Based on a line of pachinko machines.
  • What do you do at the end of the world? Are you busy? Will you save us? (Shuumatsu Nani Shitemasu ka? Isogashii desu ka? Sukutte Moratte Ii desu ka?)
    PV
    Premiere Date: April 11

    A man reawakens after half of the millennium to find that humanity lost the war against powerful invading beasts and now must survive on floating islands. Now he helps raising the only form of offense against the threat - small girls with incredible magical powers.
  • The World YAMIZUKAN
    Premiere Date: April 2

    A horror series told in a omnibus format and presented in a picture card story-style.
  • Yu-Gi-Oh! VRAINS
    Premiere Date: May 10

    6th entry in the Yu-Gi-Oh! franchise. A student that never played Duel Monsters takes up the game in order to solve a mystery in the cyberverse.
