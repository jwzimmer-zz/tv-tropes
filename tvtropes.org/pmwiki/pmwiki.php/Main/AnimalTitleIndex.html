An index made specifically to list every work title that contains an animal species (real or fantastical) in it. This includes the word "animal" itself plus its derivatives and synonyms, like "pet" or "beast".

Needless to say, humans aren't included here, unless they share title recognition with another animal species (ex. Of Mice & Men).

Those works usually feature one of the following:

  • A story about heroic animals.
  • A story about a hero who has an animal-based name.
  • A monster movie about an animal that eats or kills humans in the film.
  • Just plain symbolism.
