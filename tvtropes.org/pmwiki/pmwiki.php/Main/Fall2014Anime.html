Fall 2014 Anime

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

This chart is a list of TV anime series that aired between October and December of 2014, along with their Laconic descriptions.

For the previous season, see Summer 2014 Anime. For the next season, see Winter 2015 Anime.

Here is this season's discussion thread.

TV Series

  • Ai Tenchi Muyo
    Airing from October 6

    The newest show in the Tenchi Muyo! verse, where Tenchi must go undercover as a student teacher at an all-girls school in order to save the world (which is in danger thanks to Washu).
Advertisement:
  • Amagi Brilliant Park
    (PV)
    Airing from October 6 for 13 episodes

    A narcissistic genius is recruited to be the manager of a failing amusement park, to gather feelings of happiness for a magical kingdom.
  • Bonjour Koiaji Patisserie (Bonjour Love Flavor Pâtisserie)
    Airing from October 11

    A reverse harem set in a confectionary school.
  • Buddy Complex Kanketsu-hen -Ano Sora ni Kaeru Mirai de- (Buddy Complex Finale -In the Future When We Return to Those Skies-)
    Airing as a 2-part special on September 29 and 30

    The conclusion of the mecha anime
  • Celestial Method (Sora no Method)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 5

    An original anime. It's set in a town that has a mysterious disk floating over it as the result of a few girls' wish.
  • Coffin Princess Chaika AVENGING BATTLE (Hitsugi no Chaika AVENGING BATTLE)
    Airing from October 8 for 10 episodes

    A continuation of the spring 2014 fantasy anime.
Advertisement:
  • Cross Ange Tenshi to Ryuu no Rondo (Cross Ange Rondo of Angels and Dragons)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 4 for 25 episodes

    In a World... where magic has created a utopia, the imperial princess is exiled after it's discovered that she can't use magic. She joins with a group of similar girls, who use Humongous Mecha to fight interdimensional dragons.
  • Daitoshokan No Hitsujikai (The Great Library's Shepherd)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 8 for 12 episodes

    A harem series set in a school's (quite large) library.
  • Denki-gai no Honya-san (The Electronics District's Bookshop Worker)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 2 for 12 episodes

    A comedy set in a manga store.
  • Fate/stay night [Unlimited Blade Works]
    Airing from October 4 for 12 episodes

    A new anime for the famous Visual Novel, covering Unlimited Blade Works.
  • GARO: The Animation (Garo -The Carved Seal of Flames-)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 3

    An animated entry in the dark tokusatsu franchise.
Advertisement:
  • Girl Friend BETA
    Airing from October 12

    An adaptation of the mobile dating game.
  • Gonna Be the Twin-Tail!! (Ore, Twintail ni Narimasu!, or I Will Become a Twin Tail)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 9 for 12 episodes

    "Ore Twi" for short. A boy becomes a genderbending warrior to fight against aliens that come to Earth to steal twintails. Yes, you read that right.
  • Grisaia no Kajitsu (The Fruit of the Grisaia)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 5 for 13 episodes

    The main character is the only boy of the six students at a school for students who find themselves at odds with the world.
  • Gugure! Kokkuri-san
    (PV)
    Airing from October 5 for 12 episodes

    A reverse-harem comedy with youkai boys.
  • Gundam Build Fighters Try
    Airing from October 8 for 2 cours

    Second season, with a new cast.
  • Gundam: Reconguista in G
    (PV)
    Airing from October 2 for 2 cours

    The latest Gundam series, taking place after the classic UC era. The story follows a young pilot who works for an organization that protects a Space Elevator.
  • Hi-sCoool! SeHa Girls
    (PV)
    Airing from October 8

    A group of girls based on Sega hardware have to go into Sega games if they want to graduate school.
  • I Can't Understand What My Husband Is Saying
    (PV)
    Airing from October 2 for 13 five-minute episodes

    A comedy series following an OL and her otaku husband.
  • Kaitou Joker
    Airing from October 6

    A story about Joker who steals treasures one after another.
  • Laughing Under the Clouds (Donten ni Warau)
    Airing from October 3 for 12 episodes

    During the year Meiji 11, the government of Japan creates an inescapable lake prison to deal with the rising crime rates. The story follows three brothers who are assigned to escort prisoners to it.
  • Log Horizon 2
    Airing from October 4 for 25 episodes

    Second season.
  • Lord Marksman and Vanadis (Madan no Ou to Vanadis)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 4 for 13 episodes

    In a World... where special female warriors are given weapons by dragons, our hero gets captured by one.
  • Magic Kaito 1412
    Airing from October 4 for 24 episodes

    A young magician becomes a master thief. In continuity with Detective Conan.
  • Merman In My Tub (The Circumstances in My Home's Bathtub)
    Airing from October 6 for 13 five-minute episodes

    A high school boy that's been living on his own has a handsome merman move into his bathtub.
  • Mushishi
    Airing from October 18

    Final season
  • Ookami Shoujo To Kuro Ouji (Wolf Girl and Black Prince)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 5 for 12 episodes

    A girl ends up in a fake relationship with a sadistic boy.
  • Parasyte -the maxim-
    (PV)
    Airing from October 8 for 24 episodes

    A Ordinary Highschool Student gets an man-eating parasitic alien embedded in his hand, together they fight against parasites that were more successful at taking over their hosts.
  • Psycho-Pass 2
    Airing from October 9 for 11 episodes

    Second season to the hit Cyberpunk police anime.
  • Rage of Bahamut: Genesis
    (PV)
    Airing from October 6 for 12 episodes

    An adaptation of the social card game. During an age of peace, half of the seal to a powerful dragon is stolen.
  • selector infected WIXOSS
    Airing from October 3 for 12 episodes

    The second half of the dark card game anime.
  • The Seven Deadly Sins (Nanatsu No Taizai)
    Airing from October 5 for 24 episodes

    A princess seeks to find a group of disbanded knights called the Seven Deadly Sins in order to defeat the Holy Knights, who have taken over the country.
  • Shirobako (White Box)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 9 for 24 episodes

    An original comedy about five girls working in the anime industry.
  • Terra Formars
    (PV)
    Airing from September 26 for 13 episodes

    An action sci-fi series set on a terraformed Mars.
  • Tribe Cool Crew
    Airing from September 28

    It is about kids doing street dancing.
  • Trinity Seven
    (PV)
    Airing from October 7 for 12 episodes

    A boy attends a Wizarding School to find a way to save his cousin. The titular trinity seven are a group of powerful mages at this school, and all beautiful girls.
  • Ushinawareta Mirai O Motomete (In Search of a Lost Future)
    (CM)
    Airing from October 4

    As a school prepares for a culture festival, the astronomy club, which contains a number of the school's bigger names, is asked to deal with certain mysterious incidents that have been occuring.
  • When Supernatural Battles Became Commonplace (Inō-Batoru wa Nichijō-kei no Naka de)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 6 for 12 episodes

    InoBato for short. The five members of a literature club get superpowers but have their ordinary lives continue despite their best efforts.
  • World Trigger
    (PV)
    Airing from October 5 for 50 episodes

    A series following an alien who transfers into an Earth high school. A task force named Border has been set up to eliminate alien threats.
  • Yona of the Dawn (Akatsuki no Yona)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 7 for 2 cours

    A princess is forced to flee after her cousin usurps the throne.
  • Yowamushi Pedal GRANDE ROAD
    Airing from October 6 for 24 episodes

    Second season
  • Your Lie in April (Shigatsu wa Kimi no Uso)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 9 for 22 episodes

    A depressed former child prodigy meets a Manic Pixie Dream Girl.
  • Yuki Yuna is a Hero (Yuuki Yuuna wa Yuusha de aru)
    (PV)
    Airing from October 16 for 12 episodes

    YuYuYu for short. In an alternate world, our main character is an ordinary middle-school girl. The story focuses on her activities in the Hero Club, dealing with strange being called "vertex".
