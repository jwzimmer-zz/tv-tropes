Hello! My name is Misuki. It's a username that I use in many different websites.

I'm 31 years old, and I have a Bachelor's Degree in Early Childhood - 6th Grade Education. I currently serve a local school district as a substitute teacher. (As of March 8, 2020, I have been furloughed due to the Covid-19 pandemic.)

Throughout my life, I have indulged in many different forms of media. I've come to realize how awesome and powerful the act of storytelling really is. It can change us and the way we see our world. Storytelling is one of the fundamental parts of being human, so I am honored to both experience stories as well as make my own.

Advertisement:

My writing is a hobby which I have no plans to make a career of, and that is fine with me. While I don’t touch on subjects which could be considered popular, my stories come from the heart and there is a little piece of me in each one of them.

I have participated in Na No Wri Mo 2009, 2010, and 2011. Recently, I also jumped into the arena of Na No 2019 as well as Camp Na No April and July 2020. I am proud of each work I’ve created in these crazy writing sprints, even if I have only technically finished some of my entries. The important parts were to practice, and have fun.

My most active/favorite fandoms at the moment are Red Dwarf, Discworld, Fire Emblem: Three Houses and Avatar: The Last Airbender. I have met many amazing, creative people in my fan circles, and my hope is to connect to even more through TV Tropes' community.

Advertisement:

I am a firm believer in the writing process: Prewriting, drafting, revising, editing and publishing. It should be done in that order, and no other way.

Tropes Present in My Life:

  • Not So Stoic: I can be calm and aloof, until something bad happens, and then I just crack.
  • Crapsaccharine World: I used to think that the world was a happy place, but the research I've done in college about all the world's Real Life problems has nearly made me Go Mad from the Revelation.
  • Gosh Dang It to Heck!: I use substitutes for curse words around my parents and at work. I find them more amusing anyway.
  • Dysfunctional Family: To some extent.
  • Blind Without 'Em: If I take my glasses off, I can't see an inch in front of me without blurriness.
  • Layman's Terms: I often have to put anime and manga into this format when explaining to non-fans.
  • Creator Breakdown: I have experienced a series of these throughout my time as a writer. I'm currently in recovery from a massive writers block.
  • Old Shame: Most of my fanfics I wrote in High School.
  • Most Tropers Are Young Nerds: Self-explanatory.
  • Wiki Walk
  • TV Tropes Will Enhance Your Life
  • Gen Fic: This is usually my flavor of fanfic I read and write.
  • Extremely Short Timespan: My fanfics Road to Recovery, We Wonder Why, and Never get Caught all take place in the course of one day and/or night.
  • Nightmare Fuel: Ugh. Don't show me corpses of animal or human, or realistic blood. Just don't.
    • Nausea Fuel: Related to the above.
  • Dog Walks You: Despite my pups being small, they sure pull!
  • Embarrassing Old Photo: My childhood pictures and some of my teen/tween pictures.
  • Earn Your Happy Ending: My favorite ending to any story I read, but also my belief about life.
  • Archive Binge: My addictions...er, hobbies have me doing this.
  • From Bad to Worse: Story of my life from the years 2015-2017, with a series of family problems, financial worries, and a world that appears to be changing for the worse.
  • Adult Fear: Oh so many.
