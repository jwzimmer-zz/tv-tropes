I.... am Arimo!

Here's a little somethin'-somethin' of an introduction'.

'Round and 'round these nets I go by the little name of Arimo, or Arimo the Reindeer, if you will. Or heck, even アリモ will do, too. I am only a mere 19 years of age.

I've been reading TV Tropes for quite a while, and one day, I decided to sign up with the sole purpose of adding a page for Tensai Bakabon. However, I am enjoying the massive community a lot, so I am here to stay! You can see me around the forums in the Forum Games and Yack Fest sections, but if you're lucky, you might find me someplace else.

You may know me as that weird weeb who likes old children's anime and the Showa era way too much.

Oh, and bolded things indicate they are my super duper favorites!

Last updated: September 28, 2020

Tropes that apply to my OCs (Saviors of Sound) (WIP)

    The Saviors 
As a whole:
  • AM/FM Characterization: X is hyper and cheery and likes happy hardcore. Dieudonné is chill and relaxed and likes French house. Vladislav is a hoodlum and likes hardbass. Falterbienekatzen is childish and likes children's music.
  • Multinational Team: While it's never stated where X is from, Dieudonné is French, Vladislav is Russian, and Falterbienekatze is German.
X

  • Does Not Like Shoes: He prefers being barefoot, but if he must wear shoes, he has to wear sandals. However, he does like wearing rollerblades.
  • Mask of Confidence: He feels more confident and secure wearing his smiley face mask and never takes it off.
  • Oral Fixation: He often chews on his kandi and has a collection of pacifiers. He also likes chewing gum.
Dieudonné (and François)

  • Companion Cube: He talks to François a lot. While François is technically alive, Dieudonné doesn't know because he doesn't move around him, and can't speak anyway.
  • Cuteness Proximity: He doesn't really have a weakness for cute things...except François, who he always coos about how cute he is.
  • French Jerk: Specifically a Parisian jerk, one of the "rude to tourists" variety. X met him on a visit to Paris and was treated rudely by him until he returned François to him.
  • Living Toy: On the Sliding Scale of Living Toys, François is Schrodinger's Toy around Dieudonné and I've Got No Strings around everyone else.
  • Security Blanket: His doll François, who he is almost never without. He becomes distressed if François is taken away from him or seemingly in danger.
Vladislav

  • Ambiguous Criminal History: He is a wanted man in Russia but refuses to open up on what for when asked. He often vaguely alludes to it in a Noodle Incident type way.
  • Bland-Name Product: His tracksuit is clearly meant to resemble that of a certain real life brand, but the logo is upside down. (However, he could just be wearing a knockoff...)
  • Gang Banger: While the gang activity he participated in back in Russia is pretty unclear, he alludes to it often, and a lot of his behavior and appearance are based on a stereotypical gopnik.
  • Knife Nut: He loves his pocket knife and it's his Weapon of Choice.
  • Trademark Favorite Food: Ah, Vladislav and his sunflower seeds. He seems to almost leave a trail of them behind him wherever he goes.
Falterbienekatze

  • The Cutie: He is the sweet and childlike voice of optimism of the group.
  • Epic Hail: After escaping from Kleinstein's clutches after his army occupied Germany, he went around warning other nations that he was coming for them next.
  • Mix-and-Match Critters: He's part butterfly, bee, and cat.
  • Small, Annoying Creature: He was a ringtone mascot character (think like Crazy Frog and other Jamster characters) that gained a Misaimed Hatedom and as a result, was one of the first to be captured by Kleinstein. The other Saviors find him annoying too, but still keep him around.
    The Reverses 
As a whole:
  • Artificial Human: They were made in Kleinstein's lab and look and feel human.
  • Modified Clone: They are clones of the Saviors, but were made wth different personalities amd appearances.
Lucy

  • Opposite-Sex Clone: To X.
  • True Blue Femininity: She has a blue motif which represents her perpetual sadness as well as her gentleness.

Bleudonné

  • Absurd Phobia: He has many phobias, but his most absurd is an irrational fear of pineapples.
  • Canada, Eh?: Bleudonné claims to be Canadian, even though that is very obviously not the case. He exhibits many stereotypes because of it such as being overly polite and loving syrup.
  • Lovable Coward

Avlida

  • Gun Nut: She loves her BFGs.
  • Opposite-Sex Clone: Of Vladislav.

Teufelflederkatze

  • Cute Little Fangs
  • Dark Is Not Evil: Despite his evil looking appearance, he is not a Token Evil Teammate. His "evil" consists of childlike misbehavior.
  • Mix-and-Match Critters: Part devil, bat, and cat.
     The Schalldämpflotte 
As a whole:
  • Brainwashed and Crazy: Most of Kleinstein's army are people he is puppeteering with his baton.

Glock and Spiel

  • Funetik Aksent: Ja, ja, zey talk like zis.
  • Identical Twin ID Tag: Glock has a scar under his eye.
  • Polar Opposite Twins: Glock is smart and serious while Spiel is clumsy and dimwitted.
  • Punny Name: Their names are a pun on "glockenspiel".
  • Those Two Bad Guys

Kleinstein

  • Cartoon Conductor: Taken to the extreme as his baton controls people, not music.
  • Magic Wand: Specifically a magic baton with brainwashing and comtrolling abilities.
