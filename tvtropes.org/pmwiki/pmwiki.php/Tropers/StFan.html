St Fan is a French troper and editor. Long-time fan of anime in general and Rumiko Takahashi in particular. Also a Fan Fiction writer specialized in lemons.

Biggest contribution to the cause of Fandom, this Web page (Not Safe for Work):
The Ranma ½ Lemon Stories Archive

St Fan's main contributions to TV Tropes had been editing and entry pimping for the Code Lyoko, Lone Wolf, The Order of the Stick and Wakfu pages, among many others.

Advertisement:

Also intent on bringing increased awareness of various French media to this Anglophone-centric Wiki.

Proudly involved in the Wick Namespace Migration and Hyphen Migration efforts.
