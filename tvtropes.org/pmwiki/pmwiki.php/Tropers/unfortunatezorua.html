Yo, I'm Zorua. Pretty much just your average troper slash chuunibyou teenager passing through—that is, someone whose raison d'etre doesn't include much but anime, video games, music, and fictional characters. I've had an account since May 5th, 2018, though like many others, I've been browsing this site for much longer.

On here I hang out on the Forum Games sections the most, but still make a fair amount of mainspace edits; if you're lucky, you can catch me expanding and cleaning up work pages, or asking around on the Trope Finder for TLP drafts. My pastimes can sometimes include art, writing, and original characters. Well, when I'm not on TVT, that is.

I go by a wide variety of handles, so it might be a little hard to find my accounts on other platforms, but overall, my Discord (Kurogiri#2160)'s a pretty reliable place to hit me up if you ever want to talk.
