     Non Ideaverse O Cs 

The Awes

turns out that awe921 isn't a human being, but rather a specimen of the Awe species- physical manifestations of awesomeness, embodying a singular talent that they're incredibly talented with.

Timeline Master Awe

the Awe of time and space. he governs over the violence of GET THAT PIZZA!, ensuring that its participants aren't turned off by troublesome players.

  • Do Not Taunt Cthulhu: his response to Avery's Tempting Fate is to pull out a glock, shoot him then revert time- without removing the pain.
  • Humanoid Abomination: he's an Awe, and a particularly monstrous one at that. his constantly wreathed in shadow presence, his always shining glasses and inexplicable origins of their powers definitely solidify him as such.
  • Scary Shiny Glasses: one of his defining character traits, and the most present one. his also shiny teeth shows up when he's bothering to be scary, as if his 'normal' look wasn't enough.
