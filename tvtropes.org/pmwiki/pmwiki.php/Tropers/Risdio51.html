Our savior, and the only man I’m gay for, Kaworu
Hello there! I am Risdio, but you can call me Ed if you’d like. I’m 17, a boy, and I have ADHD/Asperger’s. I live in Ohio, so literally nothing interesting happens to me or anyone I physically know. My bday is July 24. Not that you’d care, but my Twitter is @justarandomweeb. I’m not crazy active on it (read: I’m crazy active for only an hour or so every day and then stop), but I should tweet and retweet stuff a couple times every day. But I should be on here virtually everyday, so feel free to message me! I might add some art here, but I don’t want to treat this site like DeviantArt. If you want to see it then I’ll add a page for art or...something, we’ll see.

    Tropes that apply to me I guess 

  • Adorkable: My friends have referred to me as such.

  • Accentuate the Negative: As much as I hate it when people do it, I’ve fallen guilty of doing it my self.

  • All of the Other Reindeer: In elementary school, I was bullied and picked on for being weird. It’s gotten better since middle school though.

  • Berserk Button: Whatever you do, do not make a mocking nickname of me.

  • Big Fun: I’m pretty chubby, and I am also a pretty fun guy according to my friends.

  • Cloudcuckoolander: I can be weird most of the time.

  • Deadpan Snarker: I’m not mean with my sarcasm, but I can get snarky.

  • Genius Bruiser: While it’s arguable how much of either I am, it’s worth mentioning that not only is my IQ some 113, (it’s really just above average and I don’t believe it’s a merit of intelligence, but it’s still worth mentioning.) also, in 1st grade, I picked fights against kids way bigger than me and won

  • Hot-Blooded: If it’s something I really like, I will get intense about it.
  • Eagleland: I am firm believer in Type 3, it ain’t perfect, but there are worse countries to be in.
  • Not So Stoic: When you first meet me in person, I’m very cold, blunt and emotionless. When you get to know me, I am the exact opposite of that. Unless I don’t like you.

  • Large Ham: When I get worked up, most definitely.

  • Real Men Wear Pink: One of my favorite musicians is Ariana Grande, but I wouldn’t call myself manly.
