Hello everyone on this here wiki! No matter what people have told you, I am not an alien. I am a 18 year old American male.

Everytime I think, "oo! This is an example of that trope!" it turns out to already be listed. As a result, I don't edit pages (other than this one) very often, and when I do, it's usually to fix grammar or mark up.

General Stuff

  • Real name: I'm not saying it here, but it fallows the Law of Alien Names. Not that I'm an alien or something!
  • Gender: Male
  • Orientation: About 90 degrees with the ground. Bi
  • Age: 18
  • Country: United States of America
  • Favorite color: Red, blue, and black. Yes, I know that's three colors.
  • Favorite music genre: Heavy Metal, though I try not to limit myself.
  • Favorite extant animal: Tardigrade
  • Favorite extinct animal: Hard to say. I'll go with Opabinia regalis (or vacuum cleaner bug, as my grandfather and I called it).
    • Advertisement:
  • Favorite planetoid: Eris
  • Astrological sign (even though I don't believe that stuff): Capricorn.
  • Religion: Atheism (not really a religion, but enough people use the word like it is one).

I show examples of…

  • Anti-Nihilist: This is my philosophy (or at least, I try to keep it like this).
  • Asperger's Syndrome: I have this. As do a lot of you, appearently.
  • Barbarian Longhair: YMMV on the barabarian part, but I don't like haircuts. Downplayed, as I actually take care of it.
  • Berserk Button: Calling Autism/Asperger's a disease and/or calling for a cure. This makes things hard because my uncle thinks this way.
  • Conlang: Yeah, I have a few.
  • Cuckoo Snarker: Describes me well in real life. My internet self doesn't have as much snarking to do.
  • Why Did It Have to Be Snakes?: How to tell if I'm scared of an animal:
    • Is it bigger than my fingernail?
    • Can it puncture skin?
    • Is it highly active?
      • Advertisement:
    • If you answered yes to two or more of the above questions, I'm probably terrified of it.
      • Unless it's a reptile.
      • I've recently been able to stay calm around bees. Let's hope I can make more progress!
