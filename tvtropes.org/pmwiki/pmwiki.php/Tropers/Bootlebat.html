     Tropes that apply to me 
  • The Anti-Nihilist: Very much so. For one, I don't find the universe being so vast compared to us Puny Earthlings scary or depressing. Indeed, I've always thought it was actually pretty cool.
  • Apologizes a Lot: To the point people tell me to stop
  • Arch-Enemy: People like ISIS, neo-nazis etc
  • Asperger's Syndrome: I have it.
  • Berserk Button: When people use solar system and galaxy interchangeably. There's a HUGE difference!
    • Also when people write my name with a lower case letter since It's like saying I'm not important enough to have my name capitalized
  • Beware the Nice Ones: I can get pretty mad sometimes. Go ahead and defend the perpetrators after a terrorist attack or mass murder to me, just try it.
  • Broken Bird: A rare male version.
  • Celibate Hero: Much to my dismay.
  • Cloud Cuckoo Lander: Would be an understatement. I've often wondered if I am the strangest person who ever lived..
  • Death Glare: I'm really good at these.
  • Earn Your Happy Ending: How I hope my life will turn out.
  • For Happiness: My general philosophy of morality. With a bit of For Great Justice mixed in.
  • Gentle Giant: I'm 6'6
  • Hair of Gold, Heart of Gold
  • Heartbreak and Ice Cream: Make that "depression and white chocolate."
  • Hollywood Nerd: While I am very nerdy I certainly don't look the part.
  • Hot-Blooded: I am very passionate.
  • Intergenerational Friendship: Multiple examples.
  • Likes Older Women: For sure
  • Manchild: More often than not unfortunately. Mentally I'm about 15
  • Manipulative Bastard: A mild example. I am very good at finding people's soft spots.
  • Mr. Vice Guy: Wrath is definitely my defining sin, although often times the things I get mad about really are reprehensible
  • Neutral Good: According to most alignment tests I've taken.
  • Opinion Myopia: One of my main flaws unfortunately. See under Principles Zealot.
  • Outgrown Such Silly Superstitions: How I feel about religion
  • Perverse Sexual Lust: So many examples..
  • Principles Zealot: I tend to believe things very strongly and have trouble seeing why anyone would think differently.
  • The Quiet One: I'm a man of few words.
  • Weaksauce Weakness: I am afraid of ceiling fans, but not for the reason you think
  • What Happened to the Mouse?: Has happened with a few people I knew online. One of them was in her 60's so she may be dead by now
  • Who Wants to Live Forever?: Frankly, the whole concept of living forever, even if you went to Heaven or the like, terrifies me to the bone.
  • The Woobie: If you only knew
  • World Half Full: How I see the world in general.

     Tropes I like 
  • Battle Couple: Just seems cool, and kinda sweet if they are the good guys.
  • The Determinator: Who doesn't like a brave and committed person? sometimes even if they are your enemy.
  • Fire-Forged Friends: The concept of enemies becoming friends/lovers always interested me.
  • Good Bad Girl: I kinda hope to meet a girl like this as a partner honestly
  • Great Escape: I've always liked stories about prison escapes and the like for some reason. Someone suggested that it was because I feel like a prisoner in my own life.
  • Kindhearted Cat Lover: I know several people like this.
  • I Resemble That Remark!: Just because I think it's funny, especially to call people out on it.
  • Iron Woobie: I knew someone who personified this trope, unfortunately, she died recently.
  • Lovable Alpha Bitch: Knew a girl kinda like this in high school (she was always nice to me but also claimed she was rather mean sometimes.)
  • Time Travel: Pretty much anything involving it has interested me all my life.

     Pet Peeve Tropes 
  • Culture Justifies Anything : Anyone who believes this is scum in my eyes.
  • Horrible Judge of Character: Seems to be America's hat.
  • Knight Templar: I usually find such people more reprehensible than those who are outright evil. If you ask me, knowing what you're doing is bad is less fucked up than believing it's a noble cause.
  • Moving the Goalposts: I can't stand it when people do this. It's cheating, plain and simple
  • Opinion Override: Always comes across as obnoxiously self-righteous and patronizing to me.
  • The Right of a Superior Species: Pretty much for the same reason as Culture Justifies Everything.
  • The Social Darwinist: Particularly considering there are a fair amount of people who are like this in real life.
  • Troll: Self explanatory
  • Virtue Is Weakness: Namely, American culture seems to think greed and selfishness are good qualities to have, and that compassion and self-sacrifice are for losers. And while not everyone believes this, any number above zero is too many.
