Hey there, names KingOfStickers, but you call me Kay. I use she pronouns.

I'm a fan of anime. If I was born in a World of Badass and had an "Awesome McCool" Name, it would probably be Lucky Diamonds, Valentine Hunter, or Mist Wanderlust. My favourite genres are basically Surrealism including its subgenres, and Action.

I proposed and helped launch Love Overrides the Law.

If I had to describe myself, I would be a combination of Knives Chau, Pinkie Pie and Homura.

Apparently, I look like Tomioka from Demon Slayer.

    My Moves 

  • The Nose Knows: I can smell things most people can't from a further distance.
  • Special Attack: a Hadouken that doubles as an Attack Reflector.
  • Implausible Fencing Powers: Because the only "weapon" I've been trained in was in a fencing class, this is the natural next step.

     Tropes that apply to me 

  • Ahoge: I tend to have a single or multiple, long hair curls. They come and go when they please. If I let them grow long enough, they end up in the shape of a question mark.
  • Air Guitar: I enjoy "practicing" at random times, although it usually isn't guitars but keyboards...
  • Agent Mulder: Oh boy.
  • Amateur Sleuth: If you've seen some of my previous posts, then you might have already put the pieces together. But anyways, my main goal is to [REDACTED].
  • Author Appeal: Girly boys, Foe Yay, dreams and Everyone Is Bi.
  • Author Avatar: I have 3 of them, all of which are in my avatar gallery. Minus Isabelle, obviously.
  • Badass Bookworm: I practiced Judo for a while and I enjoy reading books, so I guess this applies to me.
  • Berserker Tears: Haha yup.
  • Bi the Way
  • Black and Nerdy
  • Brilliant, but Lazy: My parents call me out for being this a lot.
  • Catchphrase:
    • "Yea."
    • "Indeed!"
    • On a scale of yes to no? Yes.
  • Collector of the Strange: I have a necklace collection.
  • Covert Pervert: Well it's not covert anymore if you're reading this. >:C
  • Creepy Good: I'm a fan of the occult and I enjoy randomly scaring people. Sometimes I don't even scare them on purpose!
  • Daydream Believer: I believe in the concept of parallel universes and I also believe that there are ways to drastically travel between them. Although it's easier to accomplish with the more "occulty" methods.
  • Determinator: Downplayed. I'm known for being a stubborn smartass, but I know when to fold 'em.
  • Does Not Like Spam: I hate anything with onion bits. It's the crunchy texture!
  • Dude Looks Like a Lady: I've been mistaken for a girl a lot. I don't mind.
  • Energy Beings: Dreamed I was one, once. I was a made of a white type of electrical-like energy, although I was a Humanoid Alien. I traveled to different universes and planets with my family by infinitely shrinking down. Although neither of us saw atoms or anything. There were literally just more planets within universes as we shrunk down, almost as if we were flying down. But we weren't, really.
  • Evil Laugh: Extremely rarely, you might catch me doing a "Nyohoho" under certain circumstances.
  • Extreme Omnisexual: monster gang monster gang. I play Monster Prom for a reason.
  • Fanon Discontinuity: As a general rule for myself, I believe that all Breaking the Fourth Wall moments are automatically non-canon for any work, unless it's actually a part of and important to the story.
  • The Fool: Most people believe I am this... They're not really wrong.
  • Forgets to Eat: Add to the fact I have no appetite, I often don't feel hunger as well as others do.
  • First-Person Smartass: If I get annoyed.
  • Have I Mentioned I Am Bi?: Somewhat downplayed, since I never mention it to my parents.
  • Hidden in Plain Sight: I have a serious problem with finding objects, especially if they're right in front of me. This is infuriating. I can invoke this trope pretty well myself, however.
  • Hollywood Nerd
  • Headphones Equal Isolation
  • I Have Many Names: Some people don't like to refer me by King, so they sometimes simply go by Stickers or rarely abbreviate my username to KOS. Nowadays I sometimes ask people to call me by "Kay", which is a nickname given to me by a friend.
  • I Know Mortal Kombat: A few years ago I wanted to see if I could do a hadouken in real life, so I tested it on my cousin. The Large Ham Announcer didn't announce a Non-Lethal K.O., but my cousin did clutch his stomach and fell slowly to the ground.
  • Keet: What I am on the inside.
  • Killer Rabbit: I seem to dream about these from time to time. I have literally no idea why. I don't even really care for them that much.
  • Laser-Guided Amnesia: I have some memories that are locked away, apparently.
  • Longing for Fictionland: Haha, yup.
  • Make Me Wanna Shout: My common go-to power in dreams for some reason. In my dreams I can usually shout loud enough to distort the entire area, or to reverse things back to normal.
  • Mathematician's Answer: My favourite type of answer.
  • Memetic Hand Gesture: I was the source of this for one of my classes. I arbitrarily did the hand gesture that OVER JUSTICE pulled and my classmates caught on and, well...
  • Memory Palace: Thanks to my hypno-therapist, I have gained one.
  • Mr. Imagination: Overlaps with Daydream Believer.
  • Named After Somebody Famous: My second name was named after Djavan Caetano Viana.
  • Nightmare Fetishist
  • No Sense of Direction: Please don't ask me to use a map of any kind I beg you.
  • No Social Skills: Definitely didn't have any before. I'm working on them though, and sometimes I still have trouble with my skills.
  • Neo-Paganism: I'm a Chaote.
  • Picky Eater: Very picky.
  • Pre-Asskicking One-Liner: I love using these whenever the situation calls for one. Of course, I never actually get into fights that often, so I use them in common conversations, as if I'm about to kick someones ass. Like the person reading this.
  • Sidetracked by the Gold Saucer: I'm an absolute sucker for this. If I encounter an Invisible Wall I will die on the inside.
  • Taught by Television: Attack On Titan taught me how to judo flip before I even knew it was thing.
  • The Quiet One: I don't talk much.
  • The Stoic: I used to present myself as one for personal reasons, but I'm trying to come out of the habit now.
  • Things That Go "Bump" in the Night: I usually begin to hallucinate if I stay up for too long. Therefore, time itself is pretty much my own Sanity Meter.
  • Trademark Favorite Food: Biscuits, mainly jam biscuits.
  • Training Montage: I like to pretend I'm in one whether I go to the gym. "Beat It" by Michael Jackson is my go-to song.
  • Tetris Effect: The media I tend to consume usually weaves its ways into my dreams somehow. I've had multiple Worm based dreams, at least one Paranatural dream, and so on so fourth.
  • The Unfettered: Type 3. I have an interest in following a broad range of my dreams and absolutely no one can convince me to stop believing in them. Many have failed to convince me otherwise, and I am practically at my goal already.
  • Used to Be a Sweet Kid: Downplayed. I was an Extreme Doormat known for being a general Nice Guy, but since recently I've decided to stop being a doormat and become just a bit harsher in general.
  • What the Hell Is That Accent?: Due to travelling the world and learning a couple of other languages (French and Japanese), my accent tends to make my words hard to understand unless I put a lot of effort into sounding remotely discernible. It has been described as a mixture of British and French. Unintentional Elmuh Fudd Syndwome sometimes results because of this.
  • Wholesome Crossdresser: No, I am not trans. I'm literally just a crossdresser.
  • Why Did It Have to Be Snakes?: I absolutely hate flies.

     Reverie Diamondah Cups 
Just me if I was a pony, so there isn't much on here that I'll list as I've already listed tropes above. As Troper No. 9001 said, while these are sona's they are still OCs. Also, it's just fun!

Headcanon voice: Well, myself. But if I had to choose it would be Dirk Gently.
  • A Storm Is Coming: One almost always appears to follow him. He has limited Weather Manipulation, able to disperse the current weather or summon the closest storm. After reading a book on magic he discovered his innate ability for weather manipulation and the paranormal after some ponies helped him realized that it tended to storm whether he goes, and attempted to dissipate it out of pure annoyance. That was the moment when his Cutie Mark appeared.
  • Badass Bookworm: Instead of learning how to physically fight, he decided to become adept in the art of magic. To give an example, he used his powers to kick the butt of a scientist. Overlaps with Cute Bookworm.
  • Bi the Way: Yup, still applies.
  • Camera Fiend: His talent is also in photography.
  • Celebrity Crush: On Octavia Melody.
  • Cloudcuckoolander: Reverie loves being a weirdo around other ponies and enjoys just running about. He's basically just me but more hyperactive and *anime sparkles* hard to control.
  • Color-Coded Wizardry: His magical aura is cyan.
  • Creator Provincialism: Lived in Great Bridle. He moved to Ponyville, though.
  • Deadly Game: Once participated in one unknowingly with the mafia. He was the first to dis and was wrongfully blamed for trying to kill someone by placing scorpions into her mouth.
  • Dude Looks Like a Lady: Plays this trope as well.
  • Double Meaning: His Cutie Mark represents his love and talent for the occult side of things and photography. He acquired it after discovering that he was incredibly good at taking photos, then a storm happened and a magical burst of energy surged from his horn, allowing him to control it at will.
  • Early Installment Character Design Difference: Used to have a much brighter colour scheme and his Cutie Mark went through several changes. He had a saddle on him and his hooves were trimmed. His first Cutie Mark used to be a sextant.
  • Esoteric Motifs: His Cutie Mark is a photograph of my Tuareg-pendant, which represents his affinity for the occult and paranormal. Basicslly, he's a parapsychogist.
  • Friendless Background: On top of being a Blank Flank for most of his current life and only discovering his talent recently, he had a hard time making friends which led him to become mostly withdrawn. He left Great Bridle to travel to Ponyville to find friendship and to get rid of the storm that always followed him, almost always causing flooding places. The magic storm plus his friendship problem was what set the Cutie Map off and the Elements of Harmony went off to solve it.
  • Gay Bar: Works in one. The place is also known for it also being a strip club, and Reverie works as one in the same bar.
  • It Is Pronounced "Tro-PAY": "Diamondah" is pronounced as Dee-ya-mon-dah.
  • Loners Are Freaks: Has real trouble making friends, so he tries to avoid becoming friends with other ponies.
  • Magic Librarian: Oveals with Cute Bookworm and Hot Librarian. He works in the Canterlot Archives an assistant.
  • Meaningful Name: Reverie is a term for when one daydreams, which is a thing that I frequently do. As for Cups, well... I'm usually seen holding one and i'm known for keeping a bunch of them around since I was a kid. I just think they're neat! His middle name, "Diamondah", comes from my diamond-shaped birthmark, and also because I have a middle name as well.
  • Messy Hair: Actually has this plus a few random curls like I do, but it's not shown due to the limitations of pony.town.
  • Nice Hat: Wears a stocking hat that he got for Christmas. He never really took it off since then.
  • Not So Stoic: His stoic expression is just what he normally looks like on the outside.
  • Occult Blue Eyes: Although he isn't evil, his eyes are symbolic of this.
  • Old Shame, In-Universe: He stayed as a Blank Flank for quite a long while up until his late teens.
  • Shonen Hair: Had a spiky dark-sea green and silver mane.
  • Speaks Fluent Animal: Downplayed and Justified. When he doesn't feel like talking, he resorts to neighing instead.
  • Time Travel: Once traveled back in time by using a spell to kick a scientists butt for a Love Potion... Or did he? Wink wonk.
  • Too Kinky to Torture
  • Villainous Crush: Inverted. He tends to crush on other villains and the ones he faces.
  • What Could Have Been: Went through several changes in the past. He also used to go by the name Scope Doze and his talent was in Astronomy.
  • Wholesome Crossdresser: Unlike me (unfortunately), Reverie regularly wears kneesocks while outdoors.
