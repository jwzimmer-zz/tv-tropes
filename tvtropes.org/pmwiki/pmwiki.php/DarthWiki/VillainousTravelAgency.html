Hello there, welcome to Honest Johns Travel Agency. Maybe you've had a bit of a hard time from all that endless wiki walking?. Perhaps you need to get away from it all for a while? Look no further!

Our exhilarating range of exciting locales and exotic locations includes everything you could imagine, and a few things you can't, including beautiful old buildings, nature walks, and mysterious old temples. For our wealthy interworld travellers, please choose from our excellent selection of relaxing destinations, ranging from hot and fiery scorchers to cool, crisp shelters from the relentless summer sun.

Marvel at the gigantic statue overlooking the east side, said to be the city's protector! Gaze at how impossibly high the state building is (surely that can't only be 100 floors)! Choose from our 12 cabins, each one with it's own luxuries. Ladies, our manager suggests Cabin One! Bates Motel, Psycho

A relaxing stay here in the Rockies of Colorado is just what you need to refresh yourself! Hundreds of suites are available, so come visit and explore our illustrious past and nature. An exciting array of attractions are featured within our property, such as a intricately designed Hedge Maze and a vast ballroom. Our staff is amazing at their jobs, from preparing fine dining, to making sure this place doesn't burn down! And don't even worry about our lodging during closed season. Our lovely caretaker will look after this place until we open once more! The Overlook Hotel, The Shining

Our friendly employees will work their hardest to make sure you get the experience that you deserve! Our warm climate makes for a fun getaway, with a clean environment for a variety of fun activities. This exotic vacation spot has a rich past which you will love to learn about it! We promise to personalize every experience for you, so that no one's trip is the same. So, come on down; you may never want to leave! Hell, Various Mythologies
