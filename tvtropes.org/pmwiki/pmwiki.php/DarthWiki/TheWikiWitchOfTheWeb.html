AKA Gigatrope-tan.

The Anthropomorphic Personification of all the evils of the TV Tropes. She may or may not be Trope-tan's Super-Powered Evil Side; all those who have come across her have lost their minds and are now unable to recall anything confirming or denying the rumor. It probably doesn't help that she looks a lot like Trope-tan, save for a Slasher Smile of Cute Little Fangs.

Some say the witch first appeared when too many Entry Pimps tried to make Unjustified Edits, causing an imbalance in the system, thus allowing the Wiki Witch to appear, and that say she was created when an opportunistic troper decided to make an entry without going through YKTTW. Anyway, others will say that she doesn't exist at all. And yet, her presence can be felt, working in the background, making all sorts of mischief.

Advertisement:

To keep the Wiki Witch under control, some of the pages where she may lurk have been locked. This is a cautionary measure so don't be alarmed if you can't edit a page. It's probably better this way.

The Wiki Witch:

  • Can use the Wall of Text as both an offensivenote extremely offensive and a defensive weapon.
  • Runs on Nightmare Fuel.note Usual Mode of Operation involves infiltrating the dreams of directors and authors by causing them to write Downer Endings. Is probably responsible for the deaths of every dead hero in all directions.
  • Is Nigh Unkillable, to the extent of sometimes being a Boring Invincible Villain.
  • Odds are good the form that we see is not what she really is, and under our perceptions lies a reality-breaking Eldritch Abomination.
  • Uses the underground art of Trolling.
  • Is Grammar Nazi.
    • ...but her corrections nearly wrong always.
  • Probably suffers from Wiki Schizophrenia.
      Advertisement:
    • No she doesn't.
      • Might suffer from Wiki Schizophrenia.
  • Sows confusion among her foes by inciting Natter.
  • Justifies all her edits.
    • Actually, she frequently makes Justifying Edits by defending the use of tropes in shows she enjoys no matter how badly it messes up the flow of the article or how illogical and useless her edits turn out to be. Her own edits are never justified.
      • She also has no problem with using third level bullet points so she can add her say to a conversation.
  • Is friends with both Adam Smith and Karl Marx.
  • Her quill can become a wing, and she has implied that it is only the first step in some kind of monstrous transformation.
  • Her high school photo was Just a Face and a Caption.
  • Uses Snark Bait to catch her favorite meal, the Snark.
  • May be a member of the Data Vampires, but this is an unconfirmed rumor regarding this unconfirmed rumor.
  • Is said to be in the Browser Narcotics Industry.
    • She's probably high on Proximal Errors.
  • Lives and breathes Fridge Horror.
  • Likes to wear Draco's Leather Pants on occasions.
  • Has a license to whine.
  • Murdered Fred Durst.
  • Lures in Drive By Editors using her evil charms.
  • Is the mother of That Troper.
  • May or may not be Trope-tan’s witch form.
