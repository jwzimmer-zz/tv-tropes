Stories and series that only exist on the author's hard drive, or worse, in their head. If the author publishes them eventually, they can get proper work pages. Pretty much the only evidence of their existence is the pages listed below.

See also Troper Works, which lists works that are available to the public in some form.

Do not make subpages for unpublished works. Do not link to unpublished works except from DarthWiki pages. Either action can imply that the work can be independently verified, and until it does, the work is not allowed on the main wiki.
