    Mega Destruction Dark Ball of Darkness and Destruction (4) 
All of these events are references to YouTuber Just a Pancake's "Kingdom Hearts in a Nutshell" videos. For further explanation: in the videos, Ansem, Seeker of Darkness and Riku would often send out a sphere-like projectile that's often blocked by their opponents. Riku would actually express amazement that he actually managed to hit someone with the move, though said person ended up being Saïx who tanked the hit anyway. Ansem would eventually succeed in getting somebody to be hit by this projectile when Aqua is slammed by the ball. Riku even points out how dumb she was for getting hit by it.
