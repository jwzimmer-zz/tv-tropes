The acclaimed game has returned...

32 champions shall face off in a great battle of faith, selflessness, and popular votes.

Every nominator shall bring two of their mightiest heroes to battle in the great fight, nobody will die, but the winner shall run all of the JFF forums...for two days.

Win the popular vote and move on, fail to do so and go to the loser’s bracket. Fail to do that’s and you shall fail to win the competition (albeit with some cool cutscenes)

Do whatever you can to convince people to join your side, music, art, poetry...just no bribery.

I will interview many of the nominees, making sure they are ok after their battles, and watching people deal with their loss.

We will even throw some things in to spice things up! What things? Join the thread to find out!

Rules:

1. The work the character comes from must be non-pornographic in nature and abide by the usual wiki standards.

2. You must include an avatar of the character. If you have to upload one, that's fine.

3. If you participate in this thread, you must wear the character in question for the two days.

4. Two submissions per person.

5. I have the power to accept and reject any submission at any time if it violates a rule or does something that warrants me adding a rule against it.

6. I can also add rules at any time if I need to. However, the community is entitled to call me out for an unreasonable addition and I will listen.

7. No Bribing, Raiding, or Loophole Abuse. If caught doing any of these, your competitors will automatically lose.

8. Teams are allowed.

9. Make sure your characters are at least kind of notable. OCs are fine as long as they are well known across the forums. Author Avatars are straight out.

10. Please state the work that the character is from (unless it is something like a box or a normal dog, that is not from a certain source)

11. Roleplaying is allowed, but not mandatory.
