The last thing your avatar remembered was falling asleep.

... and then, they woke up in their bed not.

   GREETINGS.    a voice boomed. Your avatar looks up. While in the darkness you only see shiny glasses. But once your eyesight adjusts you can make out their figure.

   LETS JUST GET THIS ASIDES- IF YOU ARE READING MY GRANDIOSE SPEECH IN MOBILE, I ALSO HAVE PREPARED A SECOND PICTURE WITHOUT THE QUOTERIGHT FORMATTING. THIS IS NOT AN EXCUSE TO PLASTER MY IMAGE A SECOND TIME, WHAT ARE YOU TALKING ABOUT?   

     FOR THE USERS OF THE MOBILE DEVICE. 

   SO. YOU MAY HAVE NOTICED YOU ARE NOT IN YOUR BEDROOM. THAT'S A BUMMER, RIGHT? IS THAT WHAT THE KIDS SAY NOWADAYS?   

   YOU HAVE BEEN RELOCATED TO MY FINEST MANSION, BUILT BY THE FINEST OF THE AWES. THIS IS WHERE MY LATEST GAME WILL TAKE PLACE, AND YOU ARE ONE OF ITS PARTICIPANTS.   

   WE'RE PLAYING A GAME OF MURDER. OF DECEPTION. DOESN'T THAT EXCITE YOU?   

   BEFORE I UNLOCK THE DOORS AND LET THE GAME BEGIN, I WILL ELABORATE ON THE BASICS OF THIS GAME.   

   HAVE YOU EVER HEARD OF MAFIA? BECAUSE THAT'S HOW THIS WORKS. OR TOWN OF SALEM. OR AMONG US, BUT WITHOUT TASKS AND LACKING IN A ROOM OF DEATH AND PAIN. I'M LOOKING AT YOU, ELECTRICAL.   

   EACH OF YOU WILL BE DM'D IN SECRET, ASSIGNING YOU A ROLE. PLEASE DO NOT SHARE THE DMs. THAT IS A DIRTY PLAY LOW FOR EVEN MY STANDARDS. ALL ROLES ARE ASSIGNED RANDOMLY, AND THEY'RE THE KILLER, THE INVESTIGATOR (double votes) AND THE INNOCENTS. IT MAY BE BASIC, BUT THIS IS THE FIRST ONE OF THESE EXPERIMENTS I'M RUNNING, SO I AM NOT TAKING TOO MUCH OF A RISKY MOVE.   

   LET US ELABORATE ON THE ROLES. AS AN INNOCENT/INVESTIGATOR, YOUR OBJECTIVE AS A GROUP IS TO FIND OUT THE KILLER AND THEN VOTE THEM OUT. SINCE YOU CANNOT COMMUNICATE OUTSIDE OF THIS THREAD, I SUGGEST YOU WORK TOGETHER WITH WHO IS LYING AND WHO ISN'T. EVEN IF YOU DIE AS AN INNOCENT, YOU WILL WIN IF THE KILLER IS DEAD. AS AN INVESTIGATOR, YOU HAVE THE ABILITY TO MAKE 2 VOTES.   

   NOW, FOR THE KILLER. THIS IS THE EXCITING PART. AFTER THE VOTING AKA NIGHTTIME, YOU CAN CHOOSE TO MURDER SOMEONE. YOUR OBJECTIVE IS TO KILL ENOUGH INNOCENTS UNTIL THERE IS ONLY ONE, AT WHICH POINT YOU WIN!   

   THE GAME STARTS OUT IN SOMETHING CALLED A DAY PHASE. IN THIS PHASE, ALL PLAYERS WHO ARE NOT DEAD MAY PARTICIPATE IN DISCUSSION AS WELL AS VOTE FOR WHO GETS LYNCHED (NOTE THAT YOU CAN ALSO VOTE FOR NO ONE GETTING LYNCHED). THE DAY PHASE WILL CONTINUE UNTIL A SET DEADLINE (3 DAYS) HAS PASSED OR WHEN A DECIDING VOTE IS REACHED.   

   ONCE THE DAY PHASE IS FINISHED, THE NIGHT PHASE BEGINS. NO ONE MAY TALK IN THE THREAD DURING THIS PHASE. THE SCUM'S KILL WILL BE SENT TO ME VIA DM(UNLESS OTHERWISE STATED). ONCE THE NIGHT PHASE DEADLINE IS REACHED OR WHEN ALL ACTIONS ARE SENT IN, I WILL PM EVERYONE INVOLVED WITH THEIR RESULTS AND THEN A NEW DAY PHASE WILL BEGIN. IF ANYONE DIES, THEN THAT PERSON AND THEIR ROLE WILL BE REVEALED.   

   WE HAVE THE GAME STRUCTURE COVERED... NOW FOR THE RULES.   

   RULE NUMBER 1- VOTING. IDEALLY YOU WANT TO MAKE YOUR VOTES LOOK LIKE THIS, SO IT WOULD BE EASIER FOR ME TO COUNT. I WILL STILL TOLERATE VOTES THAT DO NOT FOLLOW THIS PATTERN, BUT PLEASE DO SO.   

/lynch NotSuspicious

   RULE NUMBER 2- PARTICIPATION. I HAVE SET UP THIS GAME FOR ENTERTAINMENT, AND IT IS NOT TOO ENTERTAINING WHEN NOBODY INTERACTS WITH EACH OTHER, WHEN HALF THE PLAYERS ARE MISSING AND THEY ONLY HAVE A FEW POSTS. TRY TO POST A FEW TIMES DURING EACH DAY PHASE AND CONTRIBUTE TO THE DISCUSSION- IT WILL BE VERY IMPORTANT FOR YOUR SURVIVAL, KILLER OR OTHERWISE. IF YOU DON'T PARTICIPATE, YOU RUN A RISK OF BEING REPLACED OR BE KILLED BY MY OWN HAND.   

   RULE NUMBER 3. DO NOT CHEAT. IT RUINS THE FUN OF THE GAME. And if I DO catch you...   

   RULE NUMBER 4. IF YOU ARE DEAD, STAY DEAD. IF YOU DIE, THEN DON'T POST ANYMORE- YOU ARE STILL ALLOWED TO SPECTATE HOWEVER.   

   RULE NUMBER 5. DON'T EDIT YOUR POSTS. IF YOUR POST HAS AWFUL GRAMMAR, JUST MAKE A NEW ONE TO CORRECT IT. I WONT JUDGE UNLIKE IN THE PIZZA EXPERIMENT. THIS IS TO PREVENT CONSPICIOUS THINGS FROM BEING QUICKLY CORRECTED.   

   RULE NUMBER 6. DO NOT QUOTE MY DMS. CREATING A FALSE DM IS STRICTLY PROHIBITED AND FALLS UNDER VIOLATION OF NUMBER 3. YOU ARE ALLOWED TO PARAPHRASE THE INFORMATION WITHIN, BUT NEVER LITERALLY QUOTE THEM.   

   RULE NUMBER 7. WE CANNOT HAVE STALLING. IF NOBODY DIES WITHIN TWO DAY AND NIGHT PHASES IN A ROW DUE TO ANY FACTION WITHHOLDING KILLS, THE GAME ENDS IN FAVOUR OF WHICHEVER FACTION(S) DID NOT WITHHOLD THEIR KILL IN THOSE TWO DAY AND NIGHT PHASES, OR WAS THE LAST TO DO SO IF THE TWO FACTIONS DID.   

   IF YOU WOULD LIKE TO PARTICIPATE, SIGN RIGHT UP WITH AN AVATAR ALONGSIDE INFORMATION.   

   YOU CAN ADD AS MUCH INFO AS YOU WANT, BUT HERE IS A TEMPLATE AS A GOOD STARTING OFF POINT.   

THE TEMPLATE:

NAME:

AGE:

GENDER:

SPECIES:

ABILITIES:

   REMEMBER THAT YOU CAN ADD IN MORE INFO IF YOU WANT.   

   I HAVE ALSO PROVIDED YOU WITH A MAP PARTIALLY INTO THE GAME. YOU CAN ACCESS IT HERE.   

   NOW, FOR THE GAME SPECIFIC RULES.   

   1 - IF YOU DO IT, DO IT. AKA NO 'I CHOOSE TO KILL X', YOU MUST DESCRIBE THE ACTION THAT KILLS THE PERSON. EXAMPLE: 'I TAKE A CANDLE, MEET UP WITH MY VICTIM IN THE KITCHEN, BURN THEIR EYES OUT AND THEN BEAT THEM TO DEATH, CARRY THE BODY OUT AT ROUGHLY 3 AM AND HIDE IT IN THE CABINETS, THEN THROW THE CANDLE INTO THE TRASH'.   

   2 - FEEL FREE TO TALK TO EACH OTHER. THE 'FREELY PLAN BETWEEN EACH OTHER DURING NIGHT PHASE' STILL IS PRESENT THOUGH. IF YOU WANT TO BE COVERT ABOUT YOUR ACTIONS, YOU CAN DM ME. DM ME THAT YOU WANT TO GO TO THE KITCHEN OR TO THE BEDROOMS N STUFF. SO IF YOU WANTED TO GO TO THE LAUNDRY ROOM, YOU DM ME ABOUT GOING TO THE LAUNDRY ROOM. DURING NIGHT PHASE THOUGH, ALL YOUR ACTIONS ARE AUTOMATICALLY COVERT BECAUSE... ITS DARK. AND YOU GUYS CAN'T SEE MUCH. WHAT CAN I SAY.   

   3 - VOTING HAPPENS WHEN A DEAD BODY IS DISCOVERED. AT THE BEGINNING OF EACH DAY PHASE, TM AWE WILL PERFORM A ROLL CALL, AND THEN THE MISSING PERSON WILL BE SEARCHED FOR. DURING THIS PERIOD, YOU CAN SEARCH AROUND THE MANSION. THE SEARCH PHASE ENDS WHENEVER THE DEAD BODY IS REPORTED- YOU CAN FIND IT, AND THEN NOT REPORT IT IF YOU WANT TO KEEP SEARCHING.   

   4 - WE ARE INSTALLING A SLOWMODE, AROUND 2 MINUTES AND 30 SECONDS. AROUND BEING THE KEYWORD- THINK OF IT AS THE SANDWICHPOSTING RULE. WHILE THERE IS A CLEAR, DEFINITE 'YOU'RE FREE TO GO AHEAD!' THRESHOLD, IT WILL BE TOLERATED IF YOU AREN'T BLATANTLY VIOLATING IT (I.E. POSTING IMMEDIATELY AFTER). THIS IS TO ALLOW OTHERS TO GET THEIR POINT ACROSS AND ALLOW YOU TO ARTICULATE YOUR POINTS PROPERLY, AND ALLOW ME TO HAND OUT DMS WITHOUT BLATANTLY VIOLATING LOGIC BECAUSE SOMEONE DECIDED TO TAKE THE LEFT TURN.
