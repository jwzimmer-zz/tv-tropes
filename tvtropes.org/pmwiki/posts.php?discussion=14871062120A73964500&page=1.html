How Will You Spend The Day As YOUR Avatar? (Yet Another Seconds Game)

Go To
  • Forums
  • Forum Games

Because, clearly, three Seconds Games were not enough.

Same rules apply as the other three Seconds Games: ("Make the post to establish the timestamp, then check to see the seconds time of your post is. Then, edit your post to state what you got and your reaction.") Please keep this main rule in mind when posting here.

In this game, though, you're being your own avatar instead of someone else's.

This game will use a similar seconds list to the "As The Above Avatar" game:

00 - Going through the drive-thru at a fast food restaurant
01 - Attending a kid's birthday party
02 - Going on an outing with the avatar's best friend/significant other (if applicable)
03 - Running a marathon
04 - At a comic/anime/video game convention
05 - Clothes shopping
06 - Swimming
07 - Visiting your family
08 - Sick with the flu
09 - Meeting the actual character you've become
10 - Going to your real-life school
11 - Visiting your avatar's fans
12 - Trying to apply for a job
13 - As part of a "Freaky Friday" Flip between yourself and your avatar
14 - As a two-inch tall version of your avatar
15 - As a fifty-foot tall version of your avatar
16 - As a Gender Flipped version of your avatar
17 - As a Living Toy version of your avatar
18 - Getting groceries
19 - Morphing back and forth between your normal self and the avatar every five minutes
20 - At the movie theater
21 - Taking a bath/shower
22 - Being your avatar without being aware of it
23 - Eating cookies with the avatar's series' Big Bad (or the series' hero if the avatar is the Big Bad or otherwise a villain)
24 - Baking a cake
25 - Drinking alcoholic beverages at a bar
26 - As a four-year-old version of your avatar
27 - In Mario Kart
28 - At the beach
29 - Stuck in a broken elevator with a complete stranger
30 - Skydiving
31 - Filling in for the local weatherman
32 - Mowing the grass
33 - Being the quarterback for an NFL football team
34 - Saving a princess
35 - Only being hypnotized into thinking you are your avatar
36 - Being your avatar both physically and mentally
37 - Surfing TvTropes
38 - Helping homeless people
39 - Stuck on an island in the middle of the ocean
40 - Stuck inside Rule 34/fetish art of the avatar (Hope you don't get unlucky with this one)
41 - In the world of your least favorite animated TV show
42 - Playing basketball
43 - Trying to convince everyone you meet who you really are
44 - Time-traveled to the American Revolution in 1776
45 - Trick-or-treating
46 - Babysitting a child
47 - Stuck in the last video game you played
48 - Saving Christmas
49 - Having only until the end of the day return to normal before you are transformed into your avatar permanently
50 - In the world of the last live-action sitcom you watched
51 - Throwing the first pitch at a baseball game
52 - Stuck on the side of the road in the middle of nowhere
53 - In Super Smash Bros.
54 - Slowly turning into the avatar over the course of the day
55 - Slowly turning back into your normal self over the course of the day
56 - Being the lead singer for your favorite band
57 - In the universe of the last animated TV show you watched
58 - Being a guest star on The New Scooby-Doo Movies like Batman and Robin
59 - Stuck outside of your house naked

For example, I got 17, so I would be a living toy version of my avatar. This may or may not be a fun experience...
