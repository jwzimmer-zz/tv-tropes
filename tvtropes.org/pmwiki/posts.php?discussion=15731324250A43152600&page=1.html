Since Alivia's Total Drama Simulator kinda just died, I decided to do the simulator of the show that it was based on, Survivor. We will cycle the simulators every season with starting off with the Borneo Simulator since the custom template isn't out yet. Every 8 seasons, we will have an all-star season where all of the winners of the past seasons will duke it out. This is similar to the Big Brother Simulator and the Hunger Games Simulator

Rules:

  • Due to limited spots, you may only nominate one character and a reserve in case we haven't gotten all 16 or 20 spots filled in 30 hours. Reserves will be rolled at random.
  • You may assign your characters to a tribe.
  • Stats are randomized unless with the exception if you put stats with your submission.
  • Do not nominate controversial characters or people. (Ex. Hitler, Donald Trump)
    • On a similar note, Do not nominate castaways with NSFW pictures.
  • You can nominate a group castaway, just not huge populations like countries, planets,& universes due to realism and own standards.
  • The current host may choose the names of the tribes for the season including the merged tribe. Some seasons even might have 3 or 4 starting tribes.
  • The role for the runner is optional due for it combining it with the hosting role but sometimes we might need a person to run or even screen.
  • We will have a themed season every 5 seasons with a Strawpoll made of the suggestions that will close after 24 hours.
  • The rules of this thread are similar to both of the simulator threads I listed above.
  • We're not ready to have lore in our simulator yet and we don’t want it to be Darker and Edgier. So be refrain from making lore in your host posts.

We also have a Discord Server which is located here.

This season's tribe names are the Stardust Tribe and the Moonlight Tribe! With the merged tribe being the Sunbeam Tribe! There are 16 spots this season.

  • RESERVE:Cerebella◊ (Stardust Tribe)
