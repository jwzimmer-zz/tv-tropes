EPISODE SIX

Here is a recap of who is still in the Survivor Simulator.

The Stardust Tribe Alliance.

The Moonlight Tribe Alliances, unchanged since the start of the game.

REWARD CHALLENGE

For this Challenge, each of the Tribes need to practice their eyes. They need to send one member after another into a barrack to search for certain items. The win condition is obvious; the first tribe with all of their items back wins. We all know how this is going to go, with a trained detective and several other types of people who tend to notice things all on one team. Starlight Tribe never stood a chance, but Moonlight Tribe does seem to be slacking a bit; high on confidence, perhaps?

IMMUNITY CHALLENGE

During the Immunity Challenge, each Tribe must have all of its members complete an obstacle course, which seems very underwhelming considering the various other Challenges they have overcome. Both Tribes seem to be doing well, with both of them going at nearly the exact same speed. Moonlight Tribe realises too late that while they have four more people needing to complete the course, Stardust Tribe is on its last member. Alas, no matter how fast they speed, Their win streak ends here.

EVENTS

At the Stardust Tribe, much rejoicing is had; they are finally free of having to rid themselves of another member every day. The remaining four members begin to open up to each other about themselves, and it turns out that the Dissolsaber and Kirby are actually rather good friends,◊ bringing the leader and former leader of Stardust Tribe further together.

Meanwhile, at the Moonlight Tribe, a certain feeling makes its way into the camp, following their first defeat in a while. Axe, Zig and Eastwood realise that they are not as popular as the other alliance, so they try to earn the trust and compassion of the unaligned member, Sylvia. However, she pays minimal attention to two of them. Eastwood, however, manages to leave a profound impression on her.

TRIBAL COUNCIL

Moonlight Tribe has only voted to remove a Tribe member once so far, so having to do it again feels odd for them. However, with Eastwood's words working on Sylvia, that aliance feels fairly safe. The first six votes are drawn... three to Zig, three to Likulau. There remains one vote, the deciding vote, and it says... Zig.◊

EPISODE SEVEN

The two Tribes... wait, what?

The Tribes are merged! Say hello to Sunbeam Tribe!

Alliances, including former Tribes!

CHALLENGE

There will be only one Challenge today, in order to give the new Tribe more time to interact. This challenge involves the Tribe members holding their breath underwater, then the top three participants need to release buoys along a ladder. The first participant to release all the buoys will win! During the first part of the Challenge, many Tribe members rise up early, each gasping for air. Eventually, it goes down to Axe, a naturally hardy person, Kirby, who absorbed half of the atmosphere before submerging, and the Dissolsaber, who doesn't have lungs in the first place. Kirby and the Dissolsaber look at each other, nod, and then the Dissolsaber emerges from the water. Axe and Kirby start releasing their buoys, but Axe fails at the last two, leaving Kirby the winner.

EVENTS

The Tribe begins friendly conversation for the first time, although to call it friendly would be... generous. The former Stardust Tribe is rather angry and the old Moonlight Tribe, with good reason given their lack of numbers. This leads to the Stardust Alliance, the one without Neptune, to realise that they need all the friends that they can find. So, they weaken that alliance to dedicate more time to the whole Tribe Alliance, including Neptune.◊

TRIBAL COUNCIL

The first Sunbeam council has come together. Everyone knows who they want to get rid of, but unfortunately that little pink dictator has Immunity. So, in their fist ever act of unity, the old Moonlight Tribe votes against his top lieutenant, the Dissolsaber◊, in order to have a better chance at outing Kirby next time

EPISODE NINE

Sunbeam Tribe.

Alliances.

CHALLENGES

For the Reward Challenge, it is a test of dexterity, as each Tribe member needs to navigate a "web course" to retrive 16 medalions from its legs. However, if they fall off the course, they are out. One by one, participants end up plummeting off the course. However, there is one person who is light and dexterous enough to avoid this fate; Kirby, of course.◊

The Immunity Challenge is a tactical one, in which the participants are on a grid, while having to remove tiles from that grid one by one, until only one person can still move. Due to aggresive tactics against a certain pink Tribe member, nearly everyone seems to be cooperating and analysing how their opponents move. However, by the end, the only remaining participant is Moonlight Cookie. It's very nice to not have Kirby win for a change, don't you all think?

EPISODE TWELVE

Sunbeam Tribe

Alliances

CHALLENGES

For the last Reward Challenge, the whole Tribe is thrown into a mud pit and told to cover themselves in as much mud as possible. Then, the first one to scrape all the mud off their person will win! Each particpant tries really hard to scrape all off the dirt, however there is one person who would be the natural choice to succeed; Samwise, being a hobbit, simply doesn't have as much mud to scrape, and thus wins with great ease.

For the Immunity Challenge, the castaways are told a story about Bornean folklore. Then they run into the scary dark woods to find scary masks with scary questions. The first one to get back having answered the questions correctl wins immunity! It seems that these participants have a great memory for stories like this, so it really comes down to who can find the masks first. And it looks like Gamechanger has... tripped over a tree root, allowing Neptune to win Immunity, and be guaranteed to make it to the finale.◊

EVENTS

Back at the camp, tensions have mostly deflated. The former Tribes are back on speaking terms, which was a bad idea in Samwise and Moonlight Cookie's case. They are back to bickering and arguing... Such fun...◊

Getting near to the end now, all of these people have come far together, so this is sure to be a tough vote. Or maybe not, seeing as how quickly the former Moonlight Tribe have put in their votes. And it seems to be... Moonlight Cookie will not make it to the finale. Better luck next time, eh?◊
