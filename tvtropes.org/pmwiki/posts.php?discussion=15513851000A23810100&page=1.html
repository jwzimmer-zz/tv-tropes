In your post, respond to the above post's trope name with a brief description of what it might be. Then, add your own terrible name for a trope (don't use a wikilink please) for the next post to describe.

For descriptions, you need not follow any of the rules about tropes, as long as there is some argument that it fits the trope name. Our Giant Space Fleas From Nowhere Are Different could be "A video game that uses more than one boss" for example. The more pointless, self-evident, and/or pointlessly specific, the better.

For names, there are no hard rules, but they should be parody names, meaning, for example:

  • Obtuse snowclones and crappy puns on existing tropes.
  • Random, meaningless, and/or highly obscure quotes or character names.
  • Mashing together troper vocabulary without regard for meaning.

Good luck!

Middle Child Hates Tomato Sauce And Loves Disco

This is the greatest PLANNNNNN!
InTheMirror
May 7th 2019 at 6:02:25 PM

Really a terrible trope with a terrible name. No snowclone? No trope namer? No clear-concise-witty effort? I'm disappointed.

That said, it's exactly what is says on the tin. It is someone who is legally a child despite being old by human standards because in society on their planet, children become adults in their 600th year. Their species is also known for cannibalism, autocannibalism and excessive complaining. Their limbs have to be shaped in various letters of Latin alphabet, Z, X, CH and SH being the most common, but Z is the universal favourite. The 573th Birthday is usually called THE NAMEDAY because that is the day everyone can choose their name, which everyone celebrates by complaining about cannibalism and eating their little finger.

There are at least 4 known example from various media.

Middle Child Hates Tomato Sauce And Loves Disco:

This character as device trope has 5 requirements:

  • must be middle child (2nd child of three siblings, 3rd child of five, 4th child of seven, etc.); it's usually a son among daughters or a daughter among sons
  • tomato soup is always their trademark hated food, no exception
  • loves disco — both music and dancing
  • usually drinks from an early age, starts doing drugs really soon, too
  • will never sit on chairs, only ever sits on the ground or on the couch
