Welcome to .EXE! This is my first time hosting a forum game like this, so pardon me if I make a couple of slip-ups. :p

.EXE is basically a combination of an RTD and a mafia game.

  • At the beginning of the game, anywhere between 1-3 people are chosen to be The Virus. At night, they will choose one (1) person to infect and kill by PMing me.note If there is more than one Virus, they must talk to one another and decide on who to kill. They can only kill one person per night. What happens next will be decided by a roll of the dice:
    • If the Virus rolls higher than the person they're attempting to infect, the victim will die.
    • If the rolls are equal, the victim becomes assimilated into the Virus.
    • If the Virus rolls lower than the intended victim, the infection fails.

  • During the day, the crew members will investigate the area and discuss who could be the virus. This is where the RTD aspect comes in—thanks to the destructive effects of the virus, whether you find evidence or not will be determined by a roll of the dice, as seen here:

1: Epic Fail - the action was not performed, and backfired horribly
2: Fail - nothing happened
3: Partial Success - the exact degree of success is established by the GM
4: Success - the action was carried out adequately
5: Epic Success - the action succeeded in the most positive way possible
6: Overshot - though the result was achieved, the action backfired horribly due to disproportionately large effort

  • At any point during the day phase (barring Day 1 for obvious reasons), you may choose to vote for someone to be banished from the complex. Whoever gets the most votes will be removed from the game, and their role will be revealed.

  • The Virus wins if the number of surviving members are equal or greater than the number of innocents. The innocents win by voting off all members of the Virus.

note For the first round, this will be pretty barebones—just innocents and viruses, nothing more. If it becomes popular, I'll spice things up by adding some new roles for both sides, and possibly some third parties.

Rules

They're fairly similar to Awe's own Mafia game, as are a lot of things about this game, but I'll put them here too just for simplicity's sake.

1. Try to stylize your votes like "/vote ThisPerson", or "/vote nobody" if you choose to abstain. Votes can be freely changed until the end of the day phase.
2. If you sign up, then for all intents and purposes, you must participate until you die. If, for whatever reason, something comes up that prevents you from being an active participant, PM me and I will remove you from the round. If you don't post for 48+ hours without giving me a reason for your absence, you will be removed from the round.
3. No cheating. If you are caught cheating, you will be removed from the current round and banned from the next one.
4. Dead people can't talk in this thread. You are still free to spectate, however.
5. Editing posts is strongly discouraged, as it can be used to hide important information. You can just as easily make a second post correcting anything that was wrong in the first one.
6. Directly quoting anything I PM you, in or out of this thread, without my permission will fall under the "cheating" rule. The first offense will result in a warning, the second removal and a ban.
7. Stalling. Try not to do it. If nobody is killed or lynched over the duration of 2 days and 2 nights total, I will give the victory to whichever side caused a death last (innocents if the last death was a lynching, Viruses if the last death was an infection).
8. Viruses can be as simple or as verbose in their decision to kill someone as they want. You can just tell me "I'll kill X in the living room", or you can write me a paragraph describing the crime in excruciating detail, or anywhere in between.
9. Aside from deadlines, day phases end when a majority vote is reached, and night phases end when a person dies. Easy enough.
10. If the pace of the game is too quick, I will install a slowmode.
11. As per Awe's suggestion, you may choose to play as more than one character. However, the two characters will function as one, so that if one dies, so does the other. 12. Here's the map. Everyone starts in the Lounge and is free to roam the building as they choose.
