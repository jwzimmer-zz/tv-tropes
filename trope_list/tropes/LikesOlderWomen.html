Likes Older Women

Go To

Edit Locked

Wynne: Zevran, I am old enough to be your grandmother.
Zevran: You say that like it's a bad thing.
— Dragon Age: Origins
Advertisement:

A character who proudly and often loudly proclaims their preference for an older female romantic partner. The exact reasons can be anything from liking a more intellectual woman to simply wanting someone with a bigger cup size. This preference is most obvious in Shōnen and seinen, which otherwise tend to concentrate on women of a younger age bracket.

In comedy, this character is inevitably set up with a somewhat childish female character who doesn't feed into this taste at all, and is told so. Since a character that Likes Older Women isn't actually interested in younger women, this is sometimes used to avoid Squick.

Compare Teacher/Student Romance, MayDecember Romance, Mrs. Robinson, Precocious Crush, and Stacy's Mom. Contrast Really 700 Years Old. Also, please note that due to this trope being related to the Christmas Cake trope, such a person is sometimes referred to as a Cake Eater, thus explaining the dozens of references to cake on this page. Likes Older Men is this trope's Distaff Counterpart.

The fact that she's 10-15 years older than him is never an issue to him or in the context of the show, though it is touched on in the sequel, where despite several years of domestic bliss, she's reluctant to marry him, fearing that he'll dump her for a younger woman so as to have children (it doesn't help matters that (a) this is what her husband did, and (b) that Owen himself dumped his previous same-age girlfriend because she didn't want kids). It takes a lot of work for him to assure her otherwise.
  • Oh My Lady: The young K-Pop Idol Singer falls for the over-30 divorced mom.
  • The Practice: Eugene's son Kendall (who's eleven) is shown as attracted to Lucy, who's around a decade older than him. He even asks Lucy on a date. She politely declines and explains she's too old for him.
  • In Smallville, it is a one episode thing, but they had a little Flight of Romance so it is worth a mention. Warrior Angel, who is actually only 12 years old, is obviously attracted to the then-about 23 Chloe Sullivan.
  • The Vampire Diaries:
    • Jeremy Gilbert. His three love interests have been Matt's older sister Vicki, his older sister Elena's best friend Bonnie, and Anna, a vampire who is hundreds of years old.
    • Damon also seems to have no qualms about sleeping with women who are either actually older like Rebekah, Katherine, or Sage, or physically older like Kelly, Andie, or Bree.
  • What's Up Fox?: Nine years separate Byung Hee at 33 from her boyfriend Chul Soo, 24, whom she used to help babysit. He announces he's liked her since practically puberty.
  • Why Women Kill: Tommy, who's just eighteen, pursues Simone, who is in her forties at least, with a daughter not much older than him.
  • World on Fire: Nancy's censor Schmidt (who's at least twenty years younger) it turns out is attracted to her.

    Music 
  • The reggeaton song "Mayor Qué Yo" (Older than Me) by Wisin and Yandel has the aforementioned artists courting a considerably older, blonde woman with, um, considerable assets, stating that they don't care that she's older than them. She's still fair game.
  • Ricardo Arjona's song "Señora de las Cuatro Décadas" (Lady of the Four Decades) is written from the POV of someone who Likes Older Women who tells his much older girlfriend to not try to make herself look younger than she is, since she's perfect for him already.
  • In the music video of Toby Keith's "You Shouldn't Kiss Me Like This" an eighteen year old boy has a vivid daydream of him and his older female employer falling in love and making love with one another. Unfortunately or fortunately for him, her real personality is vastly different from his fantasy of her, and she seems to barely recognize his existence.
  • Emmanuel's song "Bella Señora" (Beautiful Lady) is a subtler example, as the lyrics don't openly state that the man is one of these but has some verses about the "lady"'s skin not being "porcelain-like" like in the past.
  • Elton John's "Teacher I Need You."
  • Rod Stewart, "Maggie May."
  • Ronnie McDowell's "Older Women" has the narrator suggesting a love for older women since they have the experience to "please a man".
  • Garth Brooks' "That Summer" has a teenaged boy who works on an older widowed woman's farm and ends up having a sexual encounter with her.
  • Atlanta's "Atlanta Burned Again Last Night" has a similar plotline to "That Summer", between a 17-year-old boy and a woman in her 30s.
  • The Bare Naked Ladies song "Maybe Katie" is about how the singer doesn't care about the fact that his girlfriend is older than he is.
  • "Summer: The First Time" by Bobby Goldsboro.
    Just because her youth is fading,
    Doesn't mean she's not worth dating.
  • Hailing from Brazil comes "Panela Velha" ("old pot"), with the immortal chorus "it doesn't matter if she's older! Old pots make the best food!"
  • Rick Wakeman, Progressive Rock keyboardist of Yes, The Strawbs and several solo projects, once made this joke to his audience while in his 60s: "When I was younger, I used to have a thing for older women... Not anymore!"
  • Obviously the trope-naming Stacy's Mom by Fountains of Wayne, and according to Word of God, was inspired by a childhood friend's crush on the writer's grandmother.
  • Zak Waters reminisces about his former babysitter in "Penelope" singing "Yeah I was nine and she was seventeen", "I wonder if she would remember me/ She's 26 I'm turning 18", and "Oh Penelope, where are you now? I'm old enough to take you out."

  • Cherubino in The Marriage of Figaro, though more because of his Anything That Moves philosophy than anything else.

    Video Games 
  • In Assassin's Creed III in the first Sequence of the game the player can have a conversation with Benjamin Franklin in which the latter explains the "benefits" of courting an older woman.
  • Takumi from Battle Moon Wars openly admit his preference in his conversation with Aozaki Touko, which kinda explain why he is unfazed by a cast of three five galge games around him.
  • Toby in Catherine has a crush on Erica, an old friend of the main character, who is 32. However, in the True Lovers ending, he learns that she is a post-op transgender woman.
  • In Dragon Age: Origins, Zevran seems to like hitting on Wynne even more than hitting on the PC as every conversation he has with her involves either sex, her breasts, or both. He might just be messing with her, but knowing Zevran, he probably wouldn't object if Wynne responded positively to his advances.
  • In Fire Emblem, there are a few examples:
    • In The Binding Blade, Roy has 6 possible wives: one is Cecilia, a visibly older woman who was Roy's teacher in combat and tactics.
    • In Path of Radiance, 19-year-old Boyd implies to Titania, who is 41 or so, that he always had a crush on her. And when Titania is about to shoot him down, Boyd blurts out that he was sure that Titania was younger than HIS mother (who is also the mother of 24-year-old Oscar).
    • In Genealogy of the Holy War, every one of Dew's potential wives is several years older than him except for Sylvia (she's said to be 14 in the games and 16 in the Oosawa manga) or Raquesis (said to be 16 in the mangas).
    • Fire Emblem Awakening:
      • Ricken is the youngest member of the Shepherds, meaning that almost every one of his potential wives is several years older than he is.
      • In his C-rank support conversation with Nowi, Gaius states that he likes older women because they have a certain degree of maturity. He's lying.
      • While Donnel is slightly older than Ricken, his youngest potential love interest (Lissa) is still older than he is.
      • Flavia is stated to be old enough to be Chrom's mother. The Player Character, who is approximately the same age as Chrom, can romance her.
    • Fire Emblem Fates has the Ricken expy Hayato, who's also among the youngest members of the army. This means almost all his prospect girlfriends (with the exception of Sakura, Mozu and maybe a young-looking Avatar) are his seniors. Also, a male Avatar can romance Reina, who is implied to be near his mother's age.
    • Fire Emblem: Three Houses:
      • Byleth is presumed to be in their early 20s by the start of the game, and is presumed to be around the same age as many of their students, who they can marry, but Byleth can also marry several members of the Church of Seiros. Shamir and Catherine are around five years older than male Byleth, Manuela is nearly double his age, and Rhea looks visually older than Byleth and is revealed to have lived for over a millennium. Additionally, Rhea is also a Gay Option for female Byleth.
      • Dimitri can marry all of his female classmates in the Blue Lions house, of which, only Annette is his age, while Ingrid and Mercedes are older than him. He can also marry female Byleth (if she S Supports him at the end of the game), who is around four years older than him, and Catherine, who is older than Byleth as mentioned above.
      • While all of Balthus's potential wives are significantly younger than him, he's particularly open about his crush on Claude's mother, Tiana von Riegan, as revealed in his support chain with Claude, and also swoons over Judith (who looks visually older than him) if he's recruited to the Verdant Wind route. He also implies that his best friend and Hilda's brother Holst also had a crush on Tiana.
  • Trevor in Grand Theft Auto V, though being roughly in his late-40's/early 50's, develops a romantic relationship with Patricia Madrazo, the wife of one of the antagonists, who is roughly a decade older than him. Despite sharing his feelings, she eventually returns to her husband.
  • In The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, the elderly Sheikah character Mellie tells Link that she knows he is interested in older women, but she's already married so he should just give it up. This is actually a ploy to make Link uncomfortable enough to stop following her during a Stalking Mission.
  • An option in Mass Effect 2 to play Shepard as one, featuring Samara as the cake, who is several hundred years old (even accounting for her race's life expectancy, she's old enough to have three daughters, one of whom can pass off as her) as opposed to Shepard, who, as of Mass Effect 2, is either 29 or 31 (depending on whether you count the two years (s)he was dead)... although (s)he gets turned down because Samara is married to her job.
  • Otacon in the Metal Gear Solid series, Played for Drama. It was less "likes older women" and perhaps more like "can't say no to older women."
  • Persona:
    • Persona 3:
      • Kenji Tomochika, one of your classmates, loudly proclaims his love for one of the teachers in Gekkoukan High School, and devises a Zany Scheme to get her to fall in love with him in the male protagonist's route. It's Played for Laughs at first, but as Kenji's Social Link progresses, it becomes increasingly clear that he is at best heavily in denial as to the reality of the situation. It becomes even harsher in the female protagonist's route, which introduces Kenji's Unlucky Childhood Friend Rio Iwasaki: she's had feelings for him for ages, but he's too caught up in his attraction to his teacher to notice.
      • The female protagonist is the object of a crush from Ken Amada, which you can pursue.
      • The male protagonist can also pursue a relationship with an older woman he meets over an online game. She turns out to be his homeroom teacher at the end of the game.
    • Persona 5: For the 16-year-old Player Character Joker, all but three of nine potential romance options are older than he is. Four of them are women in their twenties, one even being his homeroom teacher. Makoto and Haru are both a year older, being third-year students. And of the remaining three that aren't older, two of them are the same age (Ann and Hifumi), leaving Futaba as the only potential romantic partner that is explicitly younger than Joker. In fact, towards the end of Chihaya's Confidant, she'll outright ask Joker if he's into older women, to which he can respond "Hell yeah I am."
  • Sonic the Hedgehog:
    • To a degree, Knuckles the Echidna, who's 16, has in canon been shown to have a soft spot for Rouge the Bat, who's 18.
    • There's Vector the Crocodile, who's 20, and has a crush on Vanilla the Rabbit (Cream's mother), who's at least several years, if not a decade his senior.
  • Tales Series:
    • In Tales of the Abyss Luke counts as he's 7 due to being a replica and has a love interest in the 16 years old Tear..
    • In Tales of Symphonia: Dawn of the New World, there is evidence that Lloyd has become one of these, if Colette is not chosen. At one point in the game, Lloyd even admits that he is more fond of older woman referencing the time when he actually proposed to Paul's mother Lilia.
    • In Tales of Graces, Hubert, who is around 17-18 years old, has a pretty obvious attraction toward resident Cloudcuckoolander, Pascal, who's 22.
    • Jude from Tales of Xillia, has once said Milla is attractive and commented on how Presa was "cute." When Alvin jokingly asks if Jude likes older women, Jude responds with an embarrassed chuckle and says "I don't know. Maybe."

    Visual Novels 
  • CLANNAD's canonical plot goes with Tomoya falling in love and marrying an older girl. Well, Nagisa is only one year older, but it is remarked upon as being unusual. Another route can have him fall for Misae, his landlady, who seems to be in her mid 20's.
  • In Crescendo the player has the choice of boning either his older non-blood related maternal sister Ayame or the smexy aloof, Christmas Cake School Nurse Kaori.
  • In Jewel Knights Crusaders your hero is currently in high school and there are 2 candidates that fulfill this trope. One is a hot nurse who is also currently attending college, another is a childhood friend who the hero frequently call Onee-san.
  • Maji de Watashi ni Koi Shinasai!:
    • Our protagonist here admittedly stated that he prefers older women during one the main routes. Overall he has 2 candidates to fill this trope in the original game one of which is his homeroom teacher
    • In addition, he gets about 5 more potential older partners in the expanded sequel Majikoi S. 2 of the 5 were more or less extras, though.
    • The trend continues in the A series with the Maids, who are actually older than Yamato, and Takae Tachibana, who was advertised for A5.
  • In True Love Junai Monogatari, two of the 18-year-old Player Character's prospect love interests are women in their early-to-mid 20's: the nurse Misako and his homeroom teacher Yumi.
  • In Spirit Hunter: NG, high-schooler Seiji is head-over-heels for Akira's aunt — who's in her thirties — and dismisses the idea of being attracted to girls his age since he prefers his women 'refined'.

  • This is the go-to joke for how The442oons portrays Wayne Rooney, and for good reason.

    Western Animation 
  • Aang from Avatar: The Last Airbender has a crush on Katara, who is physically two or three years older than him, even though he's chronologically 112 years old. As it turns out in later installments in the series, They Do. It runs in the family as in the Sequel Series, The Legend of Korra, his eight-year-old grandson, Meelo, has to be pulled away by his sisters from flirting with a girl who looks like she's twelve or so.
  • Parodied in Codename: Kids Next Door with King Sandy. He's into older women... though being about 7 years old, that means said women (like Numbuh Three) are 10.
  • On Daria, Upchuck by no means limits himself to any one kind of girl, but when he realizes the supposed high-schooler he was flirting with is actually Brittany's stepmom he suddenly becomes a lot more interested.
    Upchuck: An older woman. A younger, willing man. Do I dare to dream? Please, don't be gentle. (notices Brittany's stepmom has walked away) Huh?
  • Gargoyles:
    • Tom and Katharine.
    • Macbeth on a technicality, when he falls in love with Dominique Destine who is really Demona in her daytime human appearance. It Makes Sense in Context.
  • Generator Rex has Rex who has a crush on Dr. Holiday, who is at least a decade older than him.
  • Gravity Falls has two with a 3 year age gap between Dipper and Wendy (12-15) and Li'l Gideon and Mabel (9-12).
  • J'onn J'onzz was seen with a middle-aged woman, disguised as a middle aged man in the finale of Justice League Unlimited. Given the brief look between them before he returned to his original form, it seemed he'd already revealed his identity to her and she was okay with it. Given that he was Really 700 Years Old and had been married before, its no wonder he'd go with someone more mature, at least by earth standards.
  • In The Loud House, Clyde (who's 11) has a crush on Lincoln's oldest sister, Lori, who's 17 (making her at least six years older than him). Also, in one episode, Clyde (like every other male character, including Lincoln himself) develops a crush on Ms. DiMartino, an attractive female substitute teacher.
  • Skwisgaar of Metalocalypse has a preference for 60+ year old female partners. And overweight ones too. G-MILFs, in his own words. In addition to the MILFs and Soon-to-be-MILFs.
  • Spike is this for Rarity in My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic. Admittedly, this example is somewhat muddied in that Spike, despite being a baby dragon, is actually not much younger than the adult main cast and is really only considered such due to dragon's centuries-long lifespan. He does consistently behave less mature than the rest of the cast, though.
  • Phineas and Ferb: Ferb towards Vanessa, who is most likely 4-5 years older than him. As of Act Your Age, the timeskip episode where our cast will head to college, Ferb has been dating Vanessa for a while now. Due to certain bits of Word of God regarding Phineas' future relationships, this might also apply to him as well since it is widely suspected that Isabella is older than he is.
  • The Simpsons: Most of Bart Simpson's love interests are noticeably older than he is. There's also plenty of romantic subtext between him and Edna Krabappel, as well as one episode with a child therapist that was played out just a little too straight.
  • In The Spectacular Spider Man, sixteen-year-old Peter Parker very publically proclaims his interest in twenty-year-old secretary Betty Brant, and plays the Dogged Nice Guy to pressure her to be his date for the High-School Dance. He is thwarted by his Aunt May, who approaches Betty for a frank talk about their relative ages. Betty then turns Peter down.
  • Young Justice
    • Wally, who has flirted/commented on the attractiveness of several women older than him, most notably Black Canary. He eventually winds up with Artemis, though.
    • Dick is a subtler example: the tie-in comic shows that by season two he's had relationships with Zatanna, Rocket and Bette Kane. All are older than him, though only by a year or two. He eventually settles down with Batgirl.
    • Garfield in the third season is currently dating Queen Perdita, who is two years older.
