French Maid Outfit

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

Ooh la la.
Advertisement:

The French Maid Outfit is a form of fetish, a type of Fanservice or both, made (or perhaps maid?) to call to mind the French Maid character type (or, if in Japanese media such as Anime and Manga, the Meido character type). They are a favorite Halloween costume and often appear in sexual roleplaying. High-heeled shoes or boots and fishnet or black, back-seamed stockings are standard parts of this outfit, which may sometimes include fancy leather or lace gloves as well. As the page image shows, the dress always has a short skirt to show off the wearer's legs.

The Meido variant, while still very much a form of fanservice, tends to be more conservatively cut than the Western costume it was based on. The fantasy comes from the subservience and Moe-ness of the wearer rather than more blatant sexuality.

Advertisement:

For the characters, rather than the costumes, see French Maid and Meido.

Compare to Mini Dress Of Power and Sexy Santa Dress.

    Advertisement 
  • One Bud Light commercial had a woman phone her husband at work, "Guess who's going to be wearing a French maid outfit after work?" Cut to outside their apartment, where they both have the outfit on.
    "Oh, you meant...yeah"
  • Another commercial for Dannon La Creme brand yogurt has a man and a French maid; then their kids come in and we discover they were role-playing. The kids were Squicked.
  • A Verizon commercial for its NHL streaming services has a wife dressed up as a French maid preparing for a fantastic night with her husband. When her husband comes bursting into the bedroom in hockey gear, his excuse is, "You said 'fantasy'!"

    Anime and Manga 
  • Axis Powers Hetalia:
    • The French maid is just one of the examples of phrases used by the author in one strip to explain how the Anglo-Francais rivalry is expressed in their language (as in, in French, adding that 'anglais' means the item is dull, vulgar and tasteless, and in English, 'French' as a prefix means something is racy). However, the only real maid drawn is the English chambermaid - the 'French maid' is France in a French maid costume.
    • During the Hetalia Bloodbath 2010, Hong Kong was forced to wear one of these.
    • One April Fools' 2011 sketch shows Japan wearing one of these too. And managing to look completely badass while doing so.
    • The infamous instance where Russia forced Lithuania to dress like this. And then whipped him while he was working (at least, in Lithuania's flashback). There's a little basis for this, maid-inspired outfits were a common thing in the Soviet Union, such as girls' school uniforms. This is also shown with Russia's sister Belarus, who wears a maid-inspired Elegant Gothic Lolita dress Russia gave her even today, but her's is much more modest (though she's still quite the Ms. Fanservice)
  • Mine in Fruits Basket wears one (made by Ayame presumably) as her uniform for working in the shop...but she wears it when she's not working too.
  • Maid costumes are one of the many strange "punishment games" Mion inflicts on the other club members in Higurashi: When They Cry. Dr. Irie gets a little too excited about seeing Satoko in one.
  • In Kingdom Hearts: 358/2 Days, Roxas disguises himself in this type of outfit while sneaking into the White Rabbit's home (looking for food to restore Luxord's original size). However, the Rabbit sees through the disguise and puts him on trial.
  • In K-On! on a few occasions.
    • Especially their infamous first live performance. Azusa hates the part of herself that's started to think of it as normal.
    • Ritsu thought they should do a Maid Cafe for their first year festival, as Mio is really suited to the look. "The adorable power of cute compels you!"
  • The Negima!? second anime has Chachamaru often appearing in a maid outfit most of the time she is on screen, except during school hours.
  • The "Tanks for the Memories!" episode of Pokémon sees the gang helping out at a Maid Cafe because the joint was temporarily understaffed (Brock was training one of the maids working there). This results in Dawn, Ash (again!) and even some of their mons dressing up in these outfits.
  • In the Martial Arts Dining storyarc of Ranma ½, Akane and Soun dress as French maids to infiltrate the Mansion Ranma is staying at.
  • Two characters in SHUFFLE! end up in maid costumes: Nerine and Primula, the latter including Cat Girl accessories, when they're helping Rin out cleaning his new appartment.
  • Himeko wears a maid outfit during the school festival in Sket Dance, since her class is doing, unsurprisingly, a Maid Cafe. The costume makes a reappearance in a later chapter, in which Himeko agrees to do anything Bossun says as an apology for breaking his hand. This includes dressing up as a maid, calling him 'master' and hand feeding him. Of course, Hilarity Ensues.

    Comic Books 
  • In an old X-Men comic, Hellfire Club waitresses wore French Maid outfits.
  • According to at least one Silver Age Superman story, the Daily Planet requires its top female reporters to dress like this and act as waitresses at staff parties.
  • Spider-Man:
    • During the Spider-Man/Human Torch miniseries, the Torch and She-Hulk meet at his apartment to go to a costume party. Johnny dresses up as Spidey, while Jennifer shows up in a trenchcoat concealing one of these outfits. "Johnny-sense tingling..."
    • Mentioned as a gag during the Sins Remembered arc. Peter's looking for the kids from Sins Past in France. His thought process is basically, "This table is spotless" → "I've got to get one of those French maids...or maybe just Mary Jane in a French maid outfit. Why is that outfit so popular with guys? Must be the feather duster."
  • Worn mostly inexplicably by Deadpool in issue 20 of Way's run. He was on an assassin's hitlist at the time, so maybe he thought he needed a disguise?
  • There is a picture by J. Scott Campbell featuring Caitlin Fairchild of Gen¹³ in a French maid uniform, feather duster and all.
  • The Goddamned Maidman of Empowered is a subversion, being essentially Batman if Bats dressed as a French maid instead of a bat.

    Literature 
  • David Wingrove's Chung Kuo has many of these; in every case a Chinese maid to one of the T'ang lords or the princes.
  • In Sarah Caudwell's The Shortest Way to Hades, Selena and Julia are invited to a party by a grateful client, who has utterly misunderstood the nature of their relationship - as has his, uh, friend, who answers the door in "a black dress, black stockings and a little white pinafore."
    "In brief, an old-fashioned parlour maid."

  • In Ghost of a Dream, Melody comes on to Happy by reminding him of the French Maid Outfit she'd spotted in a shop window the other day. Happy adamantly objects, as he's already told her he won't wear the thing.

  • A hilarious inversion took place on The Big Bang Theory when Howard got Sheldon to wear one.
  • On Boy Meets World, Topanga in a French maid outfit is used for a brief sight gag.
  • Buffy the Vampire Slayer. In "Dead Things" Warren uses a Mind Control device on his ex-girlfriend, making her wait on them while wearing a sex-shop French maid outfit.
  • Doctor Who:
    • "Rise of the Cybermen"/"The Age of Steel": Rose spends a good chunk of the story dressed similarly to this after going undercover as a waitress at a fancy party.
    • "The Eleventh Hour": When we meet her as an adult, Amy Pond is dressed as a Fair Cop. When the Doctor questions her disguise, Amy tells him it was "this or a French maid".
  • In the episode "Gold Fever" of The Dukes of Hazzard, Boss Hogg has Daisy dress up as a French maid (even though she's a waitress) in order to impress a rich Texan.
  • In an episode of Frasier, Bulldog has one of his girlfriends wear this outfit to prepare for her role as a maid-on a radio show.
    • The fetish is also mentioned in an episode where Daphne startles Niles by speaking in an American accent. Once he's over the shock he asks her if she can do "a saucy French maid."
  • For a school event, the students in the Japanese version of Hana-Kimi decide to have a Maid Cafe. With all the guys dressing up in maid costumes to gain more female customers.
  • On the first season of The Joe Schmo Show, a bit of Fan Disservice involved Hutch, one of the guys, being dressed up in one of these as a penalty outfit for losing a challenge.
  • Definite Dad Service in the British Saturday morning kids' show Ministry Of Mayhem, with Holly Willoughby, a maid's outfit, and cream cakes being splattered about.
  • Luckily, we're only told that Hettie of NCIS: Los Angeles has a French maid outfit.
  • In Japanese drama Shōkōjo Seira, Seira is forced to wear one of these. It serves the purpose to increase her moeness.
  • In the season eight premiere of Smallville, Lois uses one as a disguise.

  • In Elsword, the Ice Burner costume set "Royal Maid/Servant" puts the girl characters in maid outfits (and the males in a tuxedo).
  • GalaxyAngel has this happen twice in the second trilogy, but both cases are route-specific (and it's not even the same route). The first game sees this happen as Cross Dressing for Kazuya Shiranami in what eventually becomes Natsume Izayoi's route (courtesy of Melba) while a cursed fruit during the third game lands Anise Azeat in one during her route.
    • Anise is the one who consumed said fruit in her route; in Natsume's it's Kazuya who consumes it (Lily neutralizes Kazuya's case immediately to protect Kazuya's secret from the first game, but Anise's case isn't neutralized until a HALO malfunction).

  • A French maid costume is unlockable for female characters in Guitar Hero: World Tour.
  • Nidalee the Bestial Huntress from League of Legends has a French Maid skin (which also turns her cougar form into a black panther).
  • One of the unlockable costumes in Lollipop Chainsaw is a French maid uniform.
  • Persona:
    • Persona 3:
      • The Main Character's ever-original classmates decide to do a Maid Cafe for their School Festival. Yukari dreads it from the depths of her spirit because it means wearing a maid uniform; Junpei and the rest of the male schoolmates look forward to this cherished event. Alas, a typhoon rips through Iwatodai and the School Festival is canceled, to the endless grief of said schoolmates (and the Main Character is also asked if he mourns the loss.) In the Persona 3 FES release, the surveillance camera skits show Yukari in her dorm room; she had indeed procured an authentic maid uniform, not a costume, and gripes loudly about how everyone wanted to see her in it. Eventually, she gives in to curiosity and wears it, and pantomimes serving a costumer. When Fuuka catches her, Yukari cheerfully ropes her into the game and dresses her in a spare uniform.
      • There's also a series of "Maid Uniform" costumes for the female members of the party to wear during the Dark Hour, as rewards for Elizabeth's requests. Aigis will find it odd if you ask her to wear one, but Yukari and Mitsuru will chew you out considerably for it. And of course, you can get all three of them in the party at the same time, wearing those outfits. In the portable remake the female main character can wear one as well, so you can defeat the forces of evil with a party of maids. Additionally, if she wears the costume while dating Akihiko or Shinjiro, they have...interesting reactions.
    • Persona 5: The servers at the maid cafe you can visit in Akihabara all wear the standard frilly black and white maid outfits, as does NPC Sadayo Kawakami in her second job as a "health delivery girl."
  • Piratez: One of the few ways for the protagonists to get into the Endless Mansion is to wear these.
  • Some Rabbids wear them for comedic effect.
  • The Sims:
    • If your Sims hire a maid, she's dressed like this. Male maids wear funny pastel shirts open at the neck, possibly intended to be sexy. Or Camp Gay.
    • Kaylynn Langerak is the French maid in The Sims 2. She's having an affair with Daniel Pleasant. This may or may not be due to her costume.
    • In the original The Sims, there were third-party mods where you could create NPC's with French-maid costumes of varying degrees of risqueness.
  • In Super Paper Mario, the first time the player sees Mimi (outside of the first scene in Castle Bleck, where she only appears in the forms of other characters), she is dressed in a french maid outfit and claiming to be working as a maid for Merlee. Once the Final Boss is defeated and most of Count Bleck's minions did Heel Face Turns, Mimi can be found in the mansion again, this time as a legitimate maid.
  • Iroha from the Samurai Shodown series is a Ninja Maid who literally dresses like a cross between a French maid and a kunoichi.
  • From the Dead or Alive series, Ayane, Helena, Hitomi, Honoka, Kasumi, Kokoro, Lei Fang, Mai, Marie, Mila, Momiji, Naotora, Nyotengu and Tina have this as a costume in Dead or Alive 5: Last Round.
  • From Fire Emblem:
    • Fire Emblem Fates Felicia and Flora wear French Maid outfits, as their classes are specifically "Maid".
    • Fire Emblem: Three Houses released "Servant Attire" for every student character in its third wave of DLC. For the boys, it was a butler's uniform. For the girls, it was maid this. In addition, an entire battalion of maids is able to be purchased about halfway through the game on any storyline if the DLC has been added.

  • In El Goonish Shive, during the explanation of Sarah's simulated time stop Catalina is depicted as wearing this at one point.

  • French Maid TV. A series of self-help videos by... women in French Maid costumes?
  • CHAINSAW MAID, animated by Takena Nagao, is a Stop Motion short featuring an attractive maid saving her family from horrible zombies. One part fetish, one part horror, but 100% awesome.
  • Gaia Online has a "Meido" set, consisting of headbands, two types of aprons, and poofy dresses, in several colours. The possibilities are endless. Interestingly, there's also an older set of "Maid" items, which are a bit more French Maid-style.

    Western Animation 
  • The Jetsons: When Jane goes shopping for a robot, in the first episode ("Rosey the Robot"), one of the models is a French maid robot in a metallic French maid outfit.
  • Francine of American Dad! wears one in "A Smith in the Hand". A blonde stripper wears a similar outfit to Francine's in "Stan Knows Best".
  • Hello Nurse wore this costume in Animaniacs in order to teach the Warner siblings table manners. Given that she's the Trope Namer for the Trope she's named after, she really didn't need any other reason.
  • Timmy Turner from The Fairly OddParents was forced to dress like this; one in "Power Pals!" and the other "Home Wrecker" (complete with fishnet stockings). Wanda donned such a disguise in "Channel Chasers" to match Cosmo's Alfred-esque appearance.
  • Boog of The Bully was forced to wear a maid outfit by Fanboy and Chum Chum in the episode "Chimp Chomp Chumps".
  • Discord wears one at the end of the My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic episode "Princess Twilight Sparkle". This is notably not played for fanservice, as Discord is a giant Mix-and-Match Critter (and male).
  • SpongeBob SquarePants wore a maid outfit somewhere during the episode "Can You Spare a Dime" where Squidward had to stay at his house. The fact that it was completely backless and that he was wearing nothing but briefs definitely counts as Getting Crap Past the Radar.

    Real Life 
  • Maid Cafes, similar to those found in Japan, have been tried a few times in North America, with varying degrees of success.
  • In a massive case of Fan Disservice, Alice Cooper on the Top Gear show recalled having Keith Moon stay at his house while he was out of town - he came home to find Moon wearing a French Maid outfit. Cooper's wife: "Who is that guy?!"
