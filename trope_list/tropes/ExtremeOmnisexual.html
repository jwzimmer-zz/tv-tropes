Extreme Omnisexual

This is a character who would, could, or has bedded anything. By "anything" we mean absolutely anything, up to and including the bed itself.

The Extreme Omnisexual isn't necessarily limited to chasing after humans or humanoids; targets of their affections can also include other species, robots, or even animals and inanimate objects.

This person is often a Lovable Sex Maniac, a depraved villain or is just really, really desperate.

If they will sleep with anyone within their sexual orientation, it's Anything That Moves. See also Really Gets Around for the usually-straight version. The polar opposite of this trope would be Asexuality. Compare Extreme Omnivore (the dietary counterpart).

    Tabletop Games 
  • Dungeons & Dragons:

  • Warhammer 40,000:

When it comes to sex, there's no coupling that they will not try—not just because it's encouraged, but because it may be the only way to actually stimulate their burned-out senses and desires. Needless to say, the fluff avoids dwelling too deeply on their "habits".

While their craftworld cousin eschew such extremes; those who leave as outcasts and corsairs often fall back into the ancient ways. Even the craftworld kin are not totally immune to this; after all, when your lifespan is measured in millennia, things like gender, age, familial ties, and even species tend to lose importance.
