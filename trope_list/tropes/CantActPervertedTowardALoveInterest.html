Can't Act Perverted Toward a Love Interest

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Laconic
  • PlayingWith
  • Create New

One sign that a couple is developing affections for one other can be, paradoxically, that they are unwilling to perform actions with or regarding each other which would be considered perverted or crude humor when done with anyone else. A guy known for making lewd sexual gestures or kissing complete strangers will all of a sudden shape up if the circumstances call for such activities around his Love Interest. His moral decency won't allow for it. There may be irony in that said interest actually finds said lewdness charming in the first place, in a variant of We Want Our Jerk Back.

Advertisement:

This trope does not necessarily involve romantic relationships; sometimes it's just a matter of personal respect to someone who, earlier on in the series, was just considered too faceless to have to worry about their feelings. Note that if the two do have to get involved in such a gesture, it tends to mark an ending to Unresolved Sexual Tension.

This trope dates back to the Petrarchan tradition. The general cultural idea was that sexual competition forced men to "clean up their act" to show legitimate affection. It is also, conversely, where the concept of Playing Hard to Get comes from.

See also Ladykiller in Love, My Girl Is Not a Slut, Nature Adores a Virgin, Chivalrous Pervert, and OOC Is Serious Business.

    Anime & Manga 
  • Gender Inverted in Aquarion Evol. Zessica initially is very flirty around Amata mostly because it amuses her that doing so sets off his flight power, and thinks nothing of his or anyone's reaction to her usual skimpy attire, but as she starts to develop genuine feelings for him, stops flirting and begins to become more and more self-conscious around him. Andy on the other hand is still perverted towards MIX and in fact never really acts perverted or interested in any other girl but MIX for that matter. He only stops and gets serious when she's captured and brainwashed late in the series and he realizes he blew his chance with her earlier.
  • Moe "Suzu" Suzuya in Asteroid in Love is the Audience Surrogate for its Yuri Fan readers, so it's not surprising that she often has perverted fantasies around the girls, including her crush Misa. However, she never fantasizes doing anything perverted in front of Misa. The only time she expresses some kind of perversion towards the Misa is in Chapter 29, when she tells Mira and Ao that one good thing about Misa moving out for college and Ao moving into her room is that she finally can do some Perverted Sniffing on Misa.
  • A dark version in Bokurano: Hatagai claims that the fact that he's never had sex with his girlfriend, Chizu's older sister is proof that he loves her. This, however, only serves to make him seem even more vile, by suggesting that only one woman is exempt from his deplorable treatment of all others, especially his gang-raping Chizu.

They end up with the arrangement of Sōsuke sleeping on the floor under the bed, and she sees no problems with sleeping in the same bed as Lemon. In fact, when Issei doesn't act like a horndog, it's a sign that there's something very, very wrong (which has been Played for Laughs and Played for Drama in equal measure during the series' run). That's not to say he doesn't have limits, though; while he fantasizes out loud with great enthusiasm, when it comes to actually doing anything, informed consent is something he will not compromise on.
  • Discussed in chapter 62 of Horimiya. Hori starts getting changed in the Student Council room while her Childhood Friend Sengoku present. He asks if she's okay with him being there, and she points out that she'd be perfectly fine bathing or even going to the bathroom with him present. When he asks if she'd be just as comfortable with Miyamura, she snaps at him, saying she could never do something like that. Bear in mind that Hori and Miyamura are already dating at this point, have an active sex life, and (unofficially) get engaged the very next chapter.
  • Inuyasha: Played with Miroku. He hits on every pretty girl, asking them "will you bear my children?" (When she points out that he hasn't, he does halfheartedly try to ask but she irritably cuts him off). Eventually, Miroku does get around to asking Sango his notorious question, and when he does, he means it as a marriage proposal.   • One of the signs in The Melancholy of Haruhi Suzumiya that Haruhi and Kyon are getting closer has to do with Haruhi's habit of nonchalantly stripping down for gym while male classmates are still in the room.      Real Life 
  • This is a common symptom of so-called "Nice Guy syndrome" (as described in the pop psychology book No More Mr. Nice Guy! and elsewhere) in which men feel ashamed of and suppress their own sexual desires, believing that no woman would want to be sexual with them. This becomes a Self-Fulfilling Prophecy when they either end up with women who really don't want to be sexual with them and choose them specifically because they don't display normal sexual feelings, or (best-case scenario) women who would love to be more sexual but believe it's the "Nice Guy" who doesn't want to.
  • Freud described this as the MadonnaWhore Complex, but it's mostly applied to women (thus the name).
