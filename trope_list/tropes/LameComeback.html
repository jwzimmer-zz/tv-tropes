Lame Comeback

Jesus Quintana: You ready to be fucked, man? ...Dios mío, man. Liam and me, we're gonna fuck you up.
The Dude: Yeah, well, you know, that's just, like, uh ... your opinion, man.
— The Big Lebowski

In a situation where the usual tropes call for a clever rejoinder, sometimes the absence of one can have the same effect (as far as the audience is concerned).

Related to Comeback Tomorrow, except here there is no comeback.

Advertisement:

Also see Dissimile, Metaphorgotten and Buffy Speak for different kinds of subverted verbal setups. See Your Mom, Big, Stupid Doodoo-Head, You Are Fat and No, You for straighter examples.

Takeru laughs at him and delivers a little "The Reason You Suck" Speech, telling him that he doesn't know the real meaning of "the Powers of Darkness" and will pay dearly for it. Ken immediately breaks into angrily calling Takeru "Maggot!" repeatedly, prompting Takeru to ask if that's all he can say.
