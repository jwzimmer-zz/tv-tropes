Role Swap AU

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

In a Role Swap Plot, characters experience an Alternate Reality Episode in which they briefly switch roles with someone else, or travel to an Alternate Dimension where their roles are different from that of their home universe.

But what if the roles aren't switched during the story? What if they were switched from the start, and this new reality is completely treated as the norm? Then you have this trope.

Advertisement:

Also known as "Role Reversal", a Role Swap AU is a common What If? fanfic storyline in which two or more characters switch their roles with each other, with the subsequent story exploring how plot events alter, diverge, or stay the same because of this swap.

Compare Fusion Fic, a similar Fan Fiction premise where a familiar story is retold using characters from a different fandom.

Fanwork Examples (sorted by source material):

    open/close all folders 

    Anime and Manga 
  • The Code Geass fanfic Code Geass: Reverse switches the roles of the Lamperogue siblings, with Nunnally becoming the protector for her younger, blind, and crippled brother Lelouch. Other swaps include between Suzaku and Kallen (with Suzaku being the half-Japanese freedom fighter and Kallen being a pure-Britannian soldier) and between Cornelia and Euphemia by their age.
  • Fairy Tail fanfic Alternate Tail Series uses this as the main premise, with the primary team consisting of Gajeel (switching with Natsu), Lyon (Gray), Mirajane (Erza), Levy (Lucy), and Pantherlily (Happy).
  • Fate Series fanfic, Inverse Fate, switches the servants of the Fourth and Fifth Holy Grail War.
  • The Kill la Kill fanfic Maim de Maim features an AU where Satsuki is the rebellious student investigating her father's death and Ryuko is the Student Council President at Honnouji Academy.
  • The Naruto fanfic Dead Garden has Sakura as the vessel for the Nine Tails instead of Naruto. Sakura sees her already low-confidence taking a hit as a result of learning this; meanwhile Naruto stays the same personality-wise, but ends up becoming a medical ninja to assist his mother.
  • The Tengen Toppa Gurren Lagann fanfiction Tengen Toppa Gurren Solvernia switches the roles of Nia and Simon, with Nia being the young orphan who follows Kamina to the surface with her Gunman Solvernia, while Simon is the discarded son of Lordgenome.
  • Reversal Of Fate is a Tsubasa -RESERVoir CHRoNiCLE- fanfic where Sakura and Syaoran's role in the story are swapped, with Syaoran being the one who lost his memory, and Sakura going on a quest to retrieve them.
Advertisement:

    Film - Animated 
  • Disney Reverse tales: Jasmine has Aladdin as the Prince and Jasmine as the street rat. In addition, Abu and Rajah swap roles, Cassim is now Sultan, and Jafar is replaced with an Original Character named Jaara.
  • The How to Train Your Dragon fanfic Souls of Dragons features Astrid as the one who was taken away by dragons at the age of three, while Valka remains on Berk. The events of the story are an alternate telling of the second film, with Astrid being a Feral Child due to being raised by dragons. As an aside, Word of God states Hiccup's sister took Astrid's place for the events of the first movie.
  • The Punk of Podunk is a Zootopia fanfic where Nick Wilde is the rookie cop, while Judy Hopps is the troubled delinquent he butts head with on his first major duty. The plot of the fic has almost nothing to do with the actual canon story line, however, as it took place 6 years before the canon timeline, and takes place in a completely different precinct.

    Literature 
  • The Sacred and the Profane is a Good Omens fic in which Crowley is an Angel and Aziraphale is a demon.
  • Harry Potter:
    • Another Prisoner Another Professor puts a spin on Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban where Lupin is the escaped convict, and Sirius is the the new DADA teacher for Harry's third year.
    • The Reveal that the prophesied "saviour" destined to defeat Voldemort could apply to Neville as well as Harry has spawned a number of fics where Neville is the Boy-Who-Lived, and Harry as just a normal wizard caught up in the war.

    Video Games 
  • Danganronpa
    • 'and back to despair again' switches the cast of Danganronpa 1 and 2, but doesn't go with a direct character-to-character switch. Nekomaru Nida is the protagonist of the Killing School Life, Mondo Oowada is the protagonist of the Killing School Trip, Aoi Asahina was the Future Foundation spy, and Peko Pekoyama is the original Mastermind.
    • Similarily, Danganronpa Class Swap also switches the position between Class 78 and 77. Here, Mahiru is the protagonist of the Killing School Life.
    • Danganronpa V3: Killing Harmony fanfic I'd Trade My Life For Yours has Shuichi taking the fall for Rantaro's death in place of Kaede, leaving the guilt-ridden pianist as the protagonist for the rest of story.
  • The Devil Survivor fanfic series "Following the shepherd" features an AU that swaps most of the major characters' roles - but most significantly, Abel is the one granted eternal life and memory instead of his brother, while Cain is the one to inherit the potential to become King of Bel.
  • Persona 5 Adult Confidant AU is a Persona 5 webcomic and and character concepts involving swapping around the ages of the core cast and adult confidants, having the largely passive supporting cast into the protagonists of the story.
  • In Iris Hawthorne: Ace Attorney, Phoenix Wright was killed by Dalia Hawthorne to obtain the poison-holding pendant, and she framed her sister Iris for her crime. After being found innocent, Iris chose to study law and became a defense lawyer, taking the place of protagonist for the series.
    • Mia Fey is the one that is poisoned by Dahlia, while Diego Armando becomes the mentor for rookie attorney Iris and dies during her second case.
  • The Undertale AU "Underswap" takes this a step further by having nearly the entire cast swapped with someone who's close to them. Amid the swapping:
    • Chara is the main character, and Frisk is the one who fell down before them.
    • Temmie is now the main antagonist, while Flowey has a village made for him.
    • Asgore is the caretaker of the Ruins, while Toriel is now the queen.
    • Mettaton is now the ghost in the Ruins, while Napstablook is the robot entertainer.
    • Sans is now the one who tries working hard, and Papyrus is the slacker.
    • The restaurant in Snowdin is now owned by Muffet, with Grillby running a bake sale in Hotland.
    • Alphys is now the Captain of the Royal Guard, while Undyne is now the Royal Scientist.
    • Asriel is now the one who admires the Royal Guard, while Temmie's true form is now Monster Kid.

    Web Comics 
  • Homestuck:
    • A common AU is "bloodswap," where the blood colors of the trolls are swapped so they have different societal roles as per the Fantastic Caste System. There's even a blog dedicated to giving advice on accurately making bloodswap AUs.
    • A similar concept is "kidswap," where the upbringings for the kids are swapped, either putting them under different guardians or swapping the roles of the guardians as well. In these fics, the kids will swap surnames to indicate their new upbringing, such as Dave Strider and John Egbert swapping to become Dave Egbert and John Strider.

    Western Animation 
  • Legend of Katara is an Avatar: The Last Airbender fanfic where Aang is freed over a decade earlier from the iceberg and soon dying from the aftereffects of being frozen for years. Katara ends up becoming the next Avatar in the cycle, with Aang serving as her spirit guide and airbending teacher.
  • Scarlet Lady is a Miraculous Ladybug fan comic involving Alpha Bitch Chloe getting her hands on the Miraculous box, forcing her to become a hero while Marinette never learns about the box's existence.
  • Cat-Ra is a She-Ra and the Princesses of Power fanfic that has Catra finding the Sword of Protection and joining the Rebellion while Adora stays with the Horde.

Canon Examples

    Canon Examples 
Anime and Manga
  • There exists a Dragon Ball manga revolving around an alternate universe where Vegeta was sent to Earth instead of Goku.

Comic Books

  • The Spider-Gwen series is set in a world where Gwen Stacy is the one bitten by the radioactive spider. This leads her to becoming Spider-Woman, which later results in the death of Peter Parker, mirroring the events of The Night Gwen Stacy Died.

Theatre

  • In 2019 London's Bridge Theater debuted a production of William Shakespeare's A Midsummer Night's Dream which swapped the roles of Titania (played here by Gwendoline Christie) and Oberon, with Titania causing Oberon to fall for Bottom.

Alternative Title(s): Role Reversal AU
