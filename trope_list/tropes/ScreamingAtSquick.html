Screaming at Squick

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

Bulma: Hey, I'm just gonna throw this out there, but you're really cute.
Trunks: W-well you know, my mom always said I was a cute kid...
Bulma: Oooh, a momma's boy, huh? I'll be your mommy. (winks)
Trunks: [internally] AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHH!!!!!!!
— Dragon Ball Z Abridged
Advertisement:

A comedy trope where a character experiences something Squicky, and that character is so horrified by this, he/she lets out a bloodcurdling scream. It doesn't matter what the thing is, as long as it's clearly gross and disturbing to that character. Something like Bob drools over a decades-old picture of a hot, young woman, only to be told that it's a picture of his grandmother.

Happens pretty often in MST.

Online, there are more YouTube videos of this reaction to shock images than one can count, not to mention all the image macros that can convey this trope.

Sometimes the thing can even be Faux Horrific.

Often this can overlap with The Scream, but for some reason this rarely overlaps with Screams Like a Little Girl.

A Sub-Trope of Brain Bleach and Comical Overreacting. Compare My God, What Have I Done?, Suckiness Is Painful.

    Comic Books 
  • Farmboy James reacts this way to Concrete showing him his "owl eyes" in No Sweat.
  • In Hawkeye vs. Deadpool Kate Bishop meets◊ the latter for the first time on Halloween and compliments him on "the best Freddy Krueger makeup I've ever seen" only to be informed by Clint that it's Deadpool's real face. She gives Deadpool a thumbs up and then turns around and silently screams at Clint.
  • Spider-Girl has an issue where May travels back in time and encounters her father when he was still a teenage super-hero. When Peter makes a flirtatious comment towards her, May's 2nd person narration ends up screaming at the idea.
    Spider-Man: We big league super-types often take abuse - though rarely from anyone as cute as you.
    Narration: I-Is he trying to come on to you?!
    Beat
    Narration: EEEYYUUUU!

Trunks is fully aware of it but doesn't want to change the past by revealing anything out loud, so he forces himself to keep up a strained smile while internally screaming in horror.

Kagami prevents her as she attempts this, but Mondo manages to disrobe all the way while everyone else is focused on Konata. Cue this trope from Tsukasa while the other girls merely fall speechless (barring Konata, of course, who cheers him on. And no, he doesn't actually streak, which averts further use of this trope).
  • In episode 14 of Sword Art Online Abridged, the last thing Kazuto Kirigaya says to Suguha before she (as Leafa) logs out of Alfheim Online is his in-game name of Kirito. Once Suguha finishes logging out, she screams up a storm of outrage (that her "cock-sampler" of a cousin is in her game) before screaming in disgust (because she said he had a sweet ass).
  • In chapter 24 of Harry Potter And The Rune Stone Path, Victor Krum utters a blood-curdling scream upon seeing a morbidly obese version of himself covered in dirt and smears of food and condiments. After frantically destroying the runic construct, he runs out of the arena looking for either alcohol or an "Obliviate" spell.

    Literature 
  • Jim, the main character of Mogworld, is a zombie who suffers a lot of damage during the course of the story. As bits fall off, he gets patched up with whatever is handy. At one point, he loses his eyes underwater and winds up getting them replaced with octopus eyes. It becomes a Running Gag through the rest of the book that whenever someone new meets him, they exclaim, "OH GOD YOUR EYES BLAAARRGGHGHH". Usually in mid-sentence.
