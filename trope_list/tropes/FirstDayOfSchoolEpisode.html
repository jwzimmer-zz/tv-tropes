First Day of School Episode

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Laconic
  • VideoExamples
  • Create New

"First day of school! First day of school! Wake up, wake up, come on! First day of school!"
— Nemo, Finding Nemo

Logically, everyone who has been to school has had a first day, and this is when that happens.

Sometimes, it's their first day at a particular school and they're a New Transfer Student, while other times, it's a young child and it's their absolute first day of school.

If it's a child, they may be nervous, or reluctant to go to school, but will generally learn An Aesop about how school is important and/or fun. They may not even want to leave afterwards. It's complete opposite is the "Last Day of School" Plot, which revolves around a character's last day of school.

     Anime & Manga 
  • One chapter of Bunny Drop starts with six-year old Rin getting ready for her first day of school. Her new guardian Daikichi has to get used to suddenly having a school-aged kid under his wing.
  • Miss Kobayashi's Dragon Maid: The first half of episode 4 is focused on Kanna's, where she meets her girlfriend Riko Saikawa.
  • Teasing Master Takagi-san: The second act of Episode 12 is called "First Day of School".
  • With the Light: Chapter 4 is focused on Hikaru's first day of preschool.

     Animation 
  • Masha and The Bear: Episode 11 is called "First Day of School".

     Comic Books 
  • In Robin, Tim's first day at Brentwood and then Louis E. Grieve Memorial high schools are shown where he's being shown around the campus by other students. The third time this looks like it's going to happen at John Wayne High in Bludhaven Tim leaves before his first day is up because he doesn't have the patience for his new classmate's ridiculous questions about the gang war his girlfriend died in, especially since his father was just murdered as well.
  • Steven Universe: Too Cool for School focuses on a story where Steven attends school with Connie. Note that, in the main series, Steven has always been homeschooled.

     Fan Works 
  • There is a whole tag, aptly called "First Day of School", on Archive of Our Own dedicated to this type of story.
  • The Octonauts fanfiction Junior Officers has a chapter titled "Back to School", which is this combined with a Whole Episode Flashback. It focuses on Peso's first day of fourth grade.
  • The K-On! Palcomix story On This Day has a scene of Ritsu and Mio's adopted daughter's first day of school.
    Ritsu and Mio's Daughter: Noo... I wanna stay with you... I don't wanna go...
    Mio: There there... be brave for mommy...

  • Finding Nemo begins with a clownfish named Nemo having his first day of school and getting kidnapped by a scuba diver after he takes a dare to touch a boat.
  • Inside Out has Riley, an 11-year-old girl, switch schools after her move as a minor part of the plot.

     Live-Action TV 
  • Bear in the Big Blue House: The episode "First Day at Mouse School" focuses on Tutter's first day of school.
  • The first episode of the docu-series Kindergarten begins with the kids starting kindergarten.
  • Full House: "The First Day Of School".
  • The first episode of Ned's Declassified School Survival Guide focuses on this by introducing most of the characters, Moze attempting to take an elective away from Ned and Cookie to make more girl friends while Ned tries to thwart her attempts, and Cookie nearly overheating himself due to him wearing a large winter coat in the summer.
  • Schitt's Creek features 28-year-old Alexis Rose going back to high school after it's revealed she never graduated from her posh boarding school. In the episode, she sticks out not only for her age but her fancy clothes and breezy attitude. She alienates her teacher, but she does make some friends with a couple of teenage girls.
  • Sesame Street:
    • One episode had Cookie Monster's first day of school, with Kermit interviewing him about how he feels.
    • One episode focused on Abby Cadabby the three-year-old fairy attending a school with a bunch of fairy-tale characters. In a later episode, Baby Bear started attending the same school.
    • In one episode, Elmo had his first day at preschool.
  • The Wonder Years opens with Kevin, Winnie and Paul starting middle school; a later season opener has the two boys starting Grade 8.

  • The prologues of The Legend of Heroes: Trails of Cold Steel I and III are set on the first day of school for both old Class VII and new Class VII respectively. Both also start off with all of them falling on a Trap Door.

  • Blue's Clues: "Blue Takes You to School", in which Blue and Joe take Periwinkle to his first day of preschool.
  • The Busy World of Richard Scarry has "Sally's First Day at School".

  • Daniel Tiger's Neighborhood:
    • "Daniel Visits School", which is before the actual day happens.
    • "Daniel Goes to School", where the day actually happens.
    • "Jodi's First Day at School", which introduces the new titular character and Daniel takes on the duties of "First Day Friend" to help Jodi feel comfortable at her new school.
  • Dora the Explorer: "First Day of School".
  • Doc McStuffins: "First Day Of Med School".
  • Franklin has "Franklin Goes to School", based off the book with the same name.
  • Jojos Circus has "Easy as Pie". Coincidentally, it's the series' first episode all together.
  • The first episode of Milly, Molly, entitled "Monday", is set about two years before the events of the rest of the series and focuses on the children's first day of school and the beginning of Milly and Molly's friendship.
  • Milo Murphy's Law: "First Impressions" has Milo's and Melissa's first day of kindergarten.
  • A flashback in the My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic episode "The Cutie Mark Chronicles" tells about Twilight's first day at Celestia's School for Gifted Unicorns.
  • The Nina Needs to Go! episode "To Preschool", Nina has her first day at preschool.
  • OK K.O.! Let's Be Heroes: A planned episode was about Fink getting enrolled in KO's class at Lakewood Public School.
  • Phineas and Ferb has "Doof 101". Notable for also being the only episode of the show's run to take place in school.
  • Rugrats had a few First Day of Daycare Episodes.
    • In "The Big House", Tommy gets dropped off at a daycare which nobody likes due to it being very strict. He meets a few toddlers who have been there before, all of whom have nicknames.
    • In "Dayscare", Chuckie and Kimi get dropped off at a daycare and Chuckie is nervous, due to mishearing "daycare" as "day-scare".
    • In "Educating Angelica", Angelica starts preschool and has to learn how to share.
    • In "Preschool Daze", Angelica goes to a new preschool and gets into competition with Susie.
  • The Simpsons:
    • In "Lisa's Sax", a Flash Back shows Bart's first days of kindergarten at Springfield Elementary.
    • In "Bart the Genius", Bart is mistaken for a genius and transferred to a new school, his first day there taking up one scene.
  • Spot the Dog has "Spot Goes To School''.
  • Steven Universe Future: "Little Homeschool" has an uncorrupted Gem named Cherry Quartz attending her first day of "school" with the guide of Steven.
  • Timothy Goes to School:
    • "Timothy Goes To School", based on the book with same name.
    • "Mama, Don't Go!" is this for newcomer Juanita.
  • Wonder Pets!: "Off to School": The team gets a call from a puffer fish who is about to attend his first day of preschool. Ming-Ming is quick to hang a lampshade on the fact that this is the least serious distress call they've ever received, but Linny says they should help him anyway.
  • Wow! Wow! Wubbzy!: "Who Needs School?".
