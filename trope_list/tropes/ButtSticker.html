Butt Sticker

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • ImageLinks
  • Create New

Naveen has seen things he wishes he couldn't have.
Advertisement:

A cartoon trope in which a huge or fat character — either accidentally or intentionally — sits on a smaller character. The bigger character then stands up, revealing the smaller character being Squashed Flat and stuck to the bigger character's backside. The bigger character may or may not be aware of what just happened. This is particularly common with animal characters who are elephants, hippos, and rhinoceroses.

Compare Ass Kicks You and Sleep Squashing. Not to be confused with Blush Sticker.

    Anime & Manga 
  • In the anime Rosario + Vampire the character Kokoa throws her Loyal Animal Companion Ko to the ground, shortly before being knocked over by several students running past and landing on Ko. Upon getting up, she is embarrassed to find that Ko has become a Butt Sticker.

    Asian Animation 
  • In Pleasant Goat and Big Big Wolf: Mighty Little Defenders episode 8, Huiertai accidentally lands on Wolffy, who ends up Squashed Flat on his bottom.

    Comic Books 
  • At the end of Archie Comics' Sonic Quest miniseries, Robotnik parachutes into his penthouse office and ends up flattening Snively. Snively remembers the incident in a Call-Back early in the Sonic Blast special.

    Comic Strips 
  • Mother Goose and Grimm: A very large friend of Mother Goose's visits and is disappointed not to see Grimm, and is told that she even sat in his favorite chair. As she stands, we see Grimm embedded in her.
  • The Far Side: "Whoa, Frank, guess what yooouuuu sat in!" says one elephant to another with a pith-helmeted explorer squished on his ass.

    Films — Animation 
  • At the end of the short film Gopher Broke, this is the fate of the eponymous gopher.
  • The Princess and the Frog: Though it's not done in the actual movie, but in the 82nd Academy Awards ceremony. When Prince Naveen was talking about being nominated for Best Animated feature, Louis squashes him and thinks they won the award. Naveen tells him it's just a nomination; then Louis turns to show us Naveen on his bottom.
  • Tarzan & Jane: After Tantor finishes his first story, he finally realizes he's been sitting on Terk.
  • In the opening scene of The Triplets of Belleville, one of the obese ladies visiting the show has her husband stuck on her butt.
  • Winnie the Pooh (2011): At the beginning of "The Backson Song", the Backson drops on Owl and starts dancing, then turns around to show Owl flattened on his backside.
  • The Lion King (1994): At the end of "I Just Can't Wait To Be King", Zazu ends up under the bottom of a sitting rhinoceros.

    Jokes 
  • Humorously inverted in a Russian joke: A hippo is swimming down the river with a frog sitting on its head. A crocodile asks the frog, "Hello frog, how are you?" The frog answers, "I've been in a bad mood all day, and something heavy is stuck to my butt."

    Theater 
  • At the Discworld Convention 2010 Maskerade, the winner portrayed Dame Violetta Gigli, the great opera singer from the backstory of Maskerade, demanding to know where her co-star is for the duet. She performs without him, and as she takes her bows we see a flattened figure in evening dress on her back. (The book itself mentions that she once flattened a tenor, but not that he was stuck to her afterwards.)

    Web Animation 
  • In episode 2 of Hanazuki: Full of Treasures, the titular character accidentally does this to Blue Hemka when she falls backward from stepping on a sharp moonrock.

    Web Comics 
  • Bronze Skin Inc : Messy Miles in Chapter Five after Charlotte sits on him.
  • In this Chainsawsuit strip, a Butt Sticker (on Cthulhu, of all people) is mistaken for a cutie mark.

    Western Animation 
  • Animaniacs:
    • "The Boids" with the director, Alfred Hitchcock, sitting on the Goodfeathers. Then they're shown stuck to his butt.
    • "Pitter Patter of Little Feet" During the night when Brain tries to escape, he falls on the floor. And when the hippos go to look for him, Flavio slips and Brain is found on his butt.
  • Back at the Barnyard:
    • "The Good, the Bad, and the Snotty": After all the animals rolled out of the barn all glued together. Bessy complains about Peck being glued to her butt.
    • "Pigmalion", Pig accidentally sits on Pip on the farmer's couch, and when he stands back up, the mouse is flat as a sticker on his butt.
  • The Bonkers episode "Witless for the Prosecution" had Jitters A. Dog get flattened by and stuck to Tanya Trunk's big butt.
  • In the CatDog episode "Vexed of Kin", there is a gag where CatDog's dad gets stuck to their mother's butt after she sits on him. For better context, their dad is a frog and their mother is a giant hairy Sasquatch-like monster.
  • Classic Disney Shorts:
    • "Tiger Trouble": an elephant lands on a tiger. In a variation, only the tiger's stripes are stuck to the elephant's butt.
    • "The Shindig" has a gag where Mickey Mouse is flattened by and stuck to the rear end of Patricia Pigg.
  • Cow and Chicken, it was a running gag in the earlier episodes with Cow unintentionally crushing her brother with her large posterior. Another example is from "Cow's Magic Blanket", where Red Guy does this to Earl.
  • Eek! The Cat Eek accidentally does this to Sharkie in "Eek Bin Ein Berliner".
  • Family Guy, "Mr. and Mrs. Stewie": Lois goes over to Quagmire's house to ask Peter to come home. When Peter gets out of bed... Quagmire is stuck in his backside.
  • Johnny Bravo episode "Johnny and the Beanstalk"; when Johnny jumps down from the top of the beanstalk, the giant falls down too and lands on him. Johnny Bravo tells him a knock-knock joke to tell him to get off.
  • Kid vs. Kat, at the end of "Bootsville's Most Wanted", Mrs. Munson falls out of Buck Diamond's car and onto Kat. She stands up to find the alien feline plasted on her rear.
  • Kung Fu Panda: Legends of Awesomeness:
    • "Hometown Hero" during Mantis' and Hao Ming wedding, Dosu tried to expose Mantis of not being the Dragon Warrior. But Po sits on him to try to keep him quiet.
    • "Scorpion Sting" when a possessed Monkey and Po collide with each other during "Sneak Attack Training" Po gets up and turns around shouting for Monkey; unaware that he's stuck to his backside.
  • The Legend of Tarzan, "Tarzan and the City of Opar": After Terk gets Tantor unstuck from a doorway in Opar, he crashes into a wall and crushes Terk.
  • Loonatics Unleashed, "Secrets of the Guardian Strike Sword": Danger Duck and Slam Tasmanian jump off a space train and Slam lands on Ophiuchus Sam. Then Slam gets up and Sam is stuck to his back.
  • Looney Tunes, Rabbit Punch: Bugs Bunny lifts the Champ over his head, but can't hold him up for long and is crushed. When the Champ sits up, Bugs is flattened on his back.
  • Pinky and the Brain, "Around the World in 80 Narfs": Pinky and Brain got sat on by a fat cowboy while they were sitting on a horse.
  • Rocko's Modern Life:
    • "Bye Bye Birdie": When Heffer sits on the couch, he is unaware that he sat on Filburt's bird. They scream when Rocko and Heffer find it stuck to Heffer's butt.
    • "Rocko's Happy Sack": While Rocko is shopping he gets crushed by the Hippo Lady's butt and gets stuck there.
  • SpongeBob SquarePants, "Kracked Krabs": When SpongeBob gives a quarter with a string to a very large employee, he pulls it back along with employee and he lands on SpongeBob. He then turns around revealing SpongeBob stuck to his backside.
  • In a George and Junior cartoon "Red Hot Rangers", Junior falls on and flattens George when the two bears fall off a tree.
  • The Twisted Tales of Felix the Cat, this happens to Felix when a Hippo lady sits on him in "Now Playing Felix".
  • Timon & Pumbaa, "Kenya Be My Friend": While Pumbaa is searching for a new friend, he sits on a meerkat named Monty.
  • Tiny Toon Adventures:
    • "How I Spent My Vacation": While Hamton's family is playing "Spot The Car", Hamton's cousin Stinky is hopping up and down on Plucky.
    • "Toon TV" during the music video Do You Love Me" by the Contours, Buster gets crushed by an elephant "doing the twist".
  • Tom & Jerry Kids:
    • "Circus Antics" after Tundo the elephant introduces his family to Jerry, he asks where Tom is. And it is revealed that he's stuck to his butt.
    • "Swallow the Swallow" after Bernie the Swallow tossed Clyde off of his birdhouse, Clyde falls and lands on Kyle.
    • "Two Stepping Tom" when the elephant singer is hanging on a beam he falls on Tom.
