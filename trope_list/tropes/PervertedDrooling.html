Perverted Drooling

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

The multiple-character Shirtless Scene was one panel above.
Advertisement:

Humans drool in anticipation of food, when asleep, and for other reasons. For this trope, a character drools because they are acting/thinking in a perverted manner. Sometimes accompanied by leering. If a character does this while they are doing Attempted Rape of another character it helps to make them even more depraved.

Compare Something Else Also Rises, for when arousal is depicted through a visual metaphor. Tongue hanging out and Wolf Whistle are optional. Sister Trope to Nosebleed.

  • Axis Powers Hetalia
    • France sometimes does this when he's being his usual lecherous self. It's one of the ways he shows attraction to the other nations.
    • Hungary's a Yaoi Fangirl who will coo at the sight of two little boys getting affectionate, drool at her husband getting molested, and also has a thing for bara (she was upset when something "rosy" did not involve manly men making out.

  • Bleach:
    • Chizuru sometimes does this when in the presence of another female.
    • The Vandenreich brings two new ones.
      • Giselle Gewelle has the ability to turn people into her zombified slaves by getting her blood on them and her interaction her female zombie and former friend, Bambietta, includes said zombie begging her for something unspecified while drooling and getting slapped around like some kind of fetish porn only make it worse. The fact that she's outright accused at one point of "smelling like semen" does nothing to help her case.

The first girl he uses it on literally drools over him and the fact that she called him disgusting before that can give the implication that the Power Perversion Potential is more than just subtext.
  • Master Roshi from the Dragon Ball franchise is known to drool in the presence of a sexy lady, Bulma also sometimes drooled in the earlier part of the anime when she saw a man she found attractive.
  • In the Full Metal Panic! manga there's a Camp Gay substitute swim teacher who takes an immediate interest in Sousuke and his abilities — an interest which apparently involves blushing, drooling, and having thoughts which are way too suggestive to not be taken as Hot for Student.
  • Kara no Kyoukai: Shirazumi does to Shiki, drooling all over her after ripping open her kimono with his teeth.
  • Koharu of Koharu No Hibi sometimes does this in regards to her Yandere crush on Akira.
  • Captain Continental prepares to rape Jonathan in Legend of the Blue Wolves and appears to wipe some drool from his face.
  • Tsukuyomi drools, blushes, and pants during her duel with Setsuna in Mahou Sensei Negima!.

  • In Maid-Sama! Yukimura is kissed by Usui and Misaki reacts by looking shell shocked and drooling a bit suggesting she is Yaoi Fangirl.
  • Saikawa does this several times in chapter 3 of Miss Kobayashi's Dragon Maid: Kanna's Daily Life in response to Kanna being cute. Her sister tells her to close her mouth before she gets her clothes dirty.
  • Naruto: In Konoha Gakuen Den, "Orochimaruko" is portrayed as a transvestite ephebophile who's after Sasuke, which, in turn, ends up Squicking most of the cast. Strangely enough, with the exception of Sasuke himself who drools at the sight.

  • In Perfect Blue Me-Mania tries to rape Mima and drools while doing so.
  • In Rosario + Vampire Routier does this as she's attacking Fong Fong with a chainsaw.
  • Shimoneta:
    • In the show's OP, Ayame is seen drooling while using a pair of panties to clean a metal bar, by pretending to give it a hand-job.
    • Anna does whenever she's lusting after Tanukichi, usually with a Luminescent Blush. Also seen both times that she facesits him.
  • Queen's Blade: Hide & Seek: Elina is shown doing this twice:
    • It happens at the beginning of the first chapter while she's dreaming about reuniting with Leina. But, when she awakes, she realizes she's drooling and snuggling with her duvet and tears it to shreds, out of indignation.
    • The second instance is in chapter 20, which shows Elina drooling while having an Imagine Spot of cuddling with her elder sister Claudette; complete with hearts and bishie sparkling.
  • In To Love-Ru, Momo does this often when thinking of Rito. Often accompanied by an Imagine Spot.
  • Chizuru of Yuru-Yuri is as much of a Covert Pervert as her twin sister Chitose. Unlike Chitose who nosebleeds over her fantasies, though, Chizuru do this.

    • Invoked by Sandra (in the last panel of strip #102, Feb. 08, 2014 — mildly NSFW), when she fantasizes about being in a sexual sandwich between Pierre and Gary; she's shown drooling and grinning lecherously.

    Western Animation 
  • Animaniacs:
    • Yakko and Wakko sometimes drool in the presence of a pretty girl often while panting like dogs.
    • Minerva Mink seems to enjoy all the attention after all... except when she tries to get something normal, like shopping for groceries or filing her taxes, but can't because every male around her is panting and howling and drooling.
      • Of course, the only males who don't have this reaction are those she's actually quite attracted to. Which causes her to react similarly to her drooling, gawking male admirers, for great comic effect.
  • Subverted in the Valentine's Day episode of Grossology when Paige appears to be drooling over her crush, but it turns out it was caused by her Valentine's card being laced with a pheromone that causes uncontrollable drooling.
  • Kaeloo:
    • In the Babysitting Episode, Mr. Cat does this when he tries to rape Kaeloo. The saliva instantly disappears once she escapes.
    • In the Season 3 premiere,note A 26 minute special Mr. Cat briefly has a daydream where he is being massaged by numerous clones of Kaeloo, while blushing and drooling.
  • The Legend of Korra: Bolin does this in "Rebel Spirit" in response to seeing Ginger do her "poses".
  • The Simpsons: Homer Simpson usually drools for food, but occasionally he's seen drooling at a sexy woman in the earlier episodes. In one episode he is taking the babysitter home when he sees a gummi stuck to her rear and reaches for it. When she turns and sees Homer reaching for her butt and drooling, she assumes the worst and sues him for sexual harassment.
  • In a Stressed Eric episode, Eric's boss hires a very hot girl to a high-powered role she seems quite unqualified for, and repeats the phrase "just some excess saliva" every time he thinks of her.
  • In Teen Titans Starfire and even Raven do this complete with releasing heart marks in the presence of Aqualad. Starfire also does this when she spots a hot guy she thinks she's engaged to be married to.
  • ThunderCats (2011): In one episode, Tygra openly gapes at the sight of Cheetara using her Super Speed, which Panthro Lampshades with a smirk and a chiding:
    Panthro: Close that mouth, you're drooling.
  • Tiny Toon Adventures: A guy with a jackhammer does this when he sees Fifi Le Fume in the episode "Aroma Amore". As soon as he realizes she's a skunk however he freaks out. Monty also does this upon seeing Mitzi in "Prom-ise Her Anything".
  • In Totally Spies!, Clover tends to do this when she sees a hot guy and goes into boy crazy mode.
