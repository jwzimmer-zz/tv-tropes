A Catgirl is a Little Bit Beastly character that has mostly human traits (hair, skin and body type) but she has some physical features like a cat, usually the ears, tail, eyes, and sometimes claws or even a natural Fur Bikini.

What makes a cat girl different from a anthropomorphic catwoman or a Funny Animal cat is that she only looks superficially feline. The Catgirl is stereotypically female, though Catboys do exist. (These are usually found in Yaoi, and as a general rule tend to be Uke archetypes.) Cat girls oftentimes follow feline Animal Stereotypes associated with cats, specifically female ones. Cat girls often tend to be sexualized and/or have added Moe traits to better appeal to the audience.

Most cat girls have biological cat features but characters who don't have those features can have a cat-eared hairband, a fake cat tail, Cute Little Fangs, fake cat paws, long, claw-like nails etc., or any combination of the aforementioned traits and a few cat-like mannerisms like Cat Smile and Faux Paw count as an invoked form of this trope.

Subtrope of Little Bit Beastly and Unusual Ears.

See also Beast Man, for an individual (fully) anthropomorphic cat person and Cat Folk, for an anthropomorphic cat species. For when a character sprouts cat ears and/or tail out of thin air, see Sprouting Ears. Compare Cute Monster Girl.
