Parental Sexuality Squick

Advertisement:

Do you have parents? Are your parents human? Do you have one or more siblings who are not your twin/triplet/etc?

If you have answered "yes" to any of these questions, then it's entirely likely that your parents have had sex.note Unless you were a test tube baby... and, even then, IVF is generally only done after couples have been trying to conceive naturally for a sufficient period of time, or are a same-sex couple lacking the opposing parts (and even then, they probably have sex, even if not the same kind). The alternative is that you are a Son of God, and statistically, that's even less likely. Also, as Dogma points out, a virgin birth doesn't necessarily mean the parents didn't have sex later. Listen closely: Your parents put Tab A into Slot B to bring you into the world.

Disgusting, isn't it?

Advertisement:

In fiction, it's common enough to see a child react with curiosity to their parents in a Primal Scene. As they get older, however, the very thought that their parents might be getting it on (or even their parents kissing in front of them, for the G-rated version) is enough to send them searching for some Brain Bleach.

Compare I Banged Your Mom, Affection-Hating Kid (though most affection that kids gag at in media is SFW), Old People Are Nonsexual (the belief that aging should kill sexuality). Contrast The Talk, which can be used to prevent this.
