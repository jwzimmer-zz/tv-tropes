Flirtatious Smack on the Ass

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Laconic
  • VideoExamples
  • Create New

COLDHANDCOLDHANDCOLDHAND!
Bob walks by Alice when Alice suddenly hops in place — she felt something grab her butt for a moment and Bob just smirks to himself.
Advertisement:

The mid-air light-spank is a customary ritual of flirtation between two (or more) mammals of mating probability since fish first crawled from the ocean onto dry land. How the person who got smacked on the ass reacts differs, either with violence or embarrassed indifference. For stronger males who do this, especially with smaller females the butt-smacked female could be shown visibly rubbing her bottom after the slap whether or not they enjoyed the action.

This can be subject to some Values Dissonance when coming from older works, as nowadays it can be a form of sexual harassment.

This trope isn't just relegated to an open-palm. Variations like pinching and finger-brushing are included. Unlike Someone's Touching My Butt, the smack presented in this trope is usually done completely on purpose, though the tropes could be confused by the person who gets the smackin'.

Advertisement:

Not to be confused with Shot in the Ass, Ass Pull, and Ass Shove. Compare Kinky Spanking for more than one swat. See also Pain to the Ass.

Like Nightwing, her Ms. Fanservice credentials tend to be focused on her backside getting attention, so it makes some odd sense.

Or an attempt to establish dominance, depending on the people involved. In the US, this is known as a "Good Game." With increasing numbers of women serving in the armed forces, the acceptability of this practice has begun to come under scrutiny, for reasons mentioned above already.
