A character reuses another character's Catch Phrase, possibly as an Ironic or Meaningful Echo. Often has a tendency to be mangled.

If the catch phrase is a Verbal Tic, this is Got Me Doing It.

If the catch phrase is less "borrowed" than "stolen out of the other character's mouth", it's Hey, That's My Line!. See also One-Liner Echo, Phrase Catcher and Share Phrase.
