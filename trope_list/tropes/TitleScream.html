Title Scream

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Laconic

  • Create New

"SUPER! SMAAAAAAAAAAAASH! BROTHEEEEEEEERRRRS!!"
— Super Smash Bros. 64 announcer

On the opening segment of a show or the title screen of a video game, the work's title is shouted very loudly.

This only applies to audio invocations of the work's title; when a work's title is written with exclamation points, it is an example of Excited Show Title!

A Sub-Trope of Title Drop. Compare Excited Show Title! (see above) and Metal Scream.
