Accidental Kiss

Cue fangirl Squee!

"How do you accidentally kiss someone? Did she slip on a rug, and your lips broke her fall?"
— Topanga Lawrence, Boy Meets World
Advertisement:

Nothing can be simultaneously more embarrassing and more exciting than the Accidental Kiss, especially if it's also a First Kiss or Sacred First Kiss. Tends to occur in three forms:

  1. In a fit of celebration, someone high on excitement unleashes their passion by grabbing and kissing the nearest set of lips before they realize what they're doing. Unplanned Smooch of Victory.
  2. After a Crash-Into Hello or something resembling, the colliders' lips always have a strange tendency to connect. Rarely, if ever, are the painful implications of teeth colliding against teeth at high speeds addressed.
  3. Then there are the foreigners (including aliens) who kiss someone without realizing what it means in the other participant's culture/planet.

This trope actually occurs in Real Life, only not to the same extent as it is shown in fiction. Can turn into a Gay Moment. Compare and contrast Platonic Kissing, which is on purpose but doesn't have any romantic intent.

  • Johnny and Yosaku share an explicitly implied off-panel Accidental Kiss in One Piece episode 23 while climbing around on Luffy's ship, to the disgust of both.
  • Sunako and Kyohei kiss accidentally by flying towards each other during a heated ping pong game in The Wallflower and connecting in the middle. This is so traumatic for Sunako that she suffers Easy Amnesia as a result.
  • Ichigo, in the first chapter of Tokyo Mew Mew, slips and falls on Aoyama and worries that it might have been a waste of a Sacred First Kiss. She doesn't seem to remember it the next time a perverted enemy kisses her in the middle of the street, though.

Oddly, it did show up later as part of the screen shots shown after his apparent demise during the Wave Country Arc. It also reappeared in screenshots during the show's fifth opening which ran for 24 episodes.

  • A mishap with a playground slide in Penguin Revolution causes Yukari to collide with Ryo and results in an accidental Sacred First Kiss.

  • In Lovely Complex, Koizumi gets her Sacred First Kiss when Otani passes out from his fever, falling forward on her lips in the process.

  • Karin-dou 4koma: While teaching Ran and Tamaryu how to ice-skate, Shizuki daydreams about a stumble causing her to accidentally kiss Tamaryu. Ran immediately stumbles into Tamaryu and does just that.

    Western Animation 
  • In the Walter Lantz Swing Symphonies classic Abou Ben Boogie this happens twice with two men who try to kiss the beautiful Miss X, but their lips are intercepted by a female camel who seems to be attracted to human men. The four legged animal speaks with the voice of an old woman even if she doesn't look quite elderly. Unlike a lot of female cartoon animals her body doesn't seem quite feminine and looks a bit goofy. One man goes to kiss Miss X and the camel sticks her head out of a window from outside to lock lips with him for a long smooch. Hearts appear in the air when their lips seperate. Another runs to embrace Miss X and finds he is making out with the camel. It seems that she is a good kisser because both men enjoy pressing their lips against hers and smile after having done so. That is until they open their eyes and realize they made out with an animal!
  • The Adventures of Jimmy Neutron: Boy Genius: Green-Skinned Space Babe April kissed Jimmy in a similar manner (on her planet, it's a trust ritual).
  • Alvin and Brittany in the episode "Kiss Conspiracy﻿" of ALVINNN!!! and the Chipmunks.
  • The main character of The Amazing World of Gumball has had at least five accidental kisses:
    • In "The Pressure" he accidentally made out with adopted brother Darwin when they were trying to kiss Penny and Masami, respectively. Neither realized who they actually kissed and they both liked it.
    • "The Kiss" had him become traumatized after accidentally kissing his grandma on the lips. He spends the whole episode trying forget and succeeds—which just makes it happen again.
    • A scene in "The Skull" (which was cut out of the American broadcast) mentions that Gumball once kissed Sussie while high on anesthetics from an appendectomy.
    • During "The Storm", Gumball pretends to be Carmen's boyfriend to make Alan jealous and she tells him to kiss her. He vocally refuses, but a basketball knocks him over and causes him to "kiss◊" her, loosely speaking—Carmen herself described it as more like a headbutt. Seeing as Carmen is a living cactus, it was physically painful for Gumball more than it was emotionally painful.
  • In American Dragon: Jake Long, Spud and Trixie kiss in a dark closet mistaking each other for their respective crushes.
    • Averted at the last second in the opening sequence; Jake is so caught up in a daydream involving Rose that he nearly kisses the mop he had been using to clean the floor.
  • Avatar: The Last Airbender has Toph pulling the first when she tries to give Sokka a kiss on the cheek for saving her from drowning. The thing is her Disability Superpower doesn't work in the the water and she was actually saved by Suki.
    Suki: Actually, it's me.
    Toph: (visibly embarrassed) Oh. well... you can go ahead and let me drown now.
  • Happens twice in Chip 'n Dale: Rescue Rangers:
    • In "Adventures in Squirrelsitting", Chip and Dale in drag get Wart and Mole to kiss.
    • At the end of "Gadget Goes Hawaiian", Gadget gets Chip and Dale to kiss.
  • In the Daffy Duck short "The Great Piggy Bank Robbery" Daffy dreams that he found his stolen piggy bank. So he grabs it and starts kissing it. In real life however he is in a pig pen kissing a plump yet cute little pig. Daffy plants kisses all over her face and a peck to the lips before he wakes up. The pig says with an elegant voice, "Shall we dance?" Then she gives him an open mouthed kiss to his lips. She proclaims to the audience "I love that duck!" and laughs. The disgusted Daffy runs away.
  • Danny Phantom: While Tucker and Sam are acting as parents to a flour sack for a school assignment, Tucker gets a little too into character and kisses Sam good-bye as he leaves. Both quickly agree, "That never happened." (Which, oddly enough, is a rare occurrence for this trope.)
  • Darkwing Duck: Darkwing, dreaming about Morgana, leans in to kiss her...and wakes up to find that he planted one on Launchpad instead. He then spits several times while Launchpad tries to make a joke to defuse the awkwardness.
  • A Running Gag in the final part of The Fairly Oddparents Wishology trilogy is Timmy attempting to kiss Trixie, only for a different Moment Killer to get between them and recieve the kiss instead.
  • In Gargoyles, stroking someone's hair was the gargoyle equivalent of kissing. When Elisa accidentally brushed Goliath's hair just before they were about to explore Manhattan, both Goliath and Hudson's expressions hint at how awkward it was.
  • On Jimmy Two-Shoes, Heloise chases Jimmy after he ruins the invention she just finished. She ends up bumping into and locking lips with Beezy, much to their disgust. To add insult to injury, Jimmy then breaks the fourth wall and shows the kiss again in slow motion.
  • Kaeloo: In Episode 83, Stumpy is kicked into the air by an angry sheep, and he lands on Eugly with their lips touching. Cue Quack Quack (who is really stupid in this episode) beating up Stumpy.
  • In My Little Pony: Friendship Is Magic, Spike very nearly smooches Applejack while daydreaming about saving Rarity in "A Dog and Pony Show".
  • In The Simpsons episode "The Way We Weren't", Milhouse accidentally kisses Homer.
    • In "Springfield Up", Moe accidentally kisses Comic Book Guy while trying to get his picture taken.
  • In Swing Shift Cinderella the Wolf rushes to kiss Cinderella AKA Red Hot Red but accidentally grabs her elderly Fairy Godmother and gives her a long smooch. While the Wolf is repelled by the old lady who has the hots for him he does seem to have enjoyed the kiss, but that changes once he opens his eyes and realizes his mistake! Unfortunately this only deepen's the Fairy's lust for the Wolf.
  • Tom and Jerry:
    • Dog Trouble starts this way when Tom, who was chasing Jerry, crashes into the sleeping bulldog and ends up locking lips as a visual gag. Needless to say, the dog is far from amused.
    • In Flirty Birdie, Jerry engineers one between a Disguised in Drag Tom and an amorous eagle courtesy of the elastic on Toms party blower beak. Tom is disgusted, but the eagle enjoys it so much he drops Jerry in his celebration.
  • Total Drama:
    • On Total Drama Island, it happens between Noah and Cody when they were sleeping, although it was more of an ear sucking. Cue massive Ho Yay.
    • And again in World Tour, between Bridgette and Alejandro. Alejandro uses it to manipulate Bridgette into trying to kiss him again, which then gets her eliminated (and in hot water with Geoff).
    • In All Stars, Courtney trips on a step and this ends up happening to her and Scott. However, both really enjoyed it, Scott openly saying so in the confessional.
  • The Ren & Stimpy Show: In the pilot short "Big House Blues", Ren dreams of caressing and kissing a beautiful lady. Once he wakes up and discovers he's actually kissing Stimpy, he freaks out and washes his mouth in the toilet. This scene is actually cut on Nick, Nicktoons, and Teennick reruns.
    Ren: What is the matter with you, man?! Have you no sense of hygiene?!
  • What's New, Scooby-Doo?: "Pompeii And Circumstance" has Shaggy and Scooby in a send-up of the Spaghetti Kiss scene from Lady and the Tramp. Yes, their lips meet. And they are repulsed by it.
  • The 2016 reboot of The Powerpuff Girls had the episode "Once Upon A Townsville," about a princess who winds up in the real world. Buttercup catches her and the princess mistakes her for her prince. She kisses Buttercup on the lips. Buttercup is repulsed; the princess is shocked and then angered when she wakes and finds out.
  • In the Popeye cartoon "For Better or Nurse," Popeye and Bluto want to woo hospital nurse Olive who tells them they really need to be hurt to enter a hospital. Excited by this prospect, the boys lean over to kiss Olive on the cheeks, but she ducks and...well, you get the idea of what happened.
