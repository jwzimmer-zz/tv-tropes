Mad Lib Anime Title

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

The manga, anime, and Japanese video game equivalent to Mad Lib Fantasy Title. For some reason these words and constructions are popular in the titles of Japanese works.

Often overlaps with Word Salad Title.

  • Role Called, with the role coming before the character name. This has its own subtrope:
    • The Adjective Noun Fred template (although this is really more of a quirk of the Japanese language than anything else)
  • Gratuitous English in general
  • "Chaos" (Chaos Wars, Metal Wolf Chaos, SNK vs. Capcom: SVC Chaos, Chaos;Head)
    • Advertisement:
  • "Metal" (Metal Gear, Full Metal Panic!, Fullmetal Alchemist, Metal Wolf Chaos)
  • "Blood" (common in vampire series, e.g. Blood-C, Blood Lad, Black Blood Brothers, Strike the Blood, Trinity Blood)
  • "Princess" (Murder Princess, Corpse Princess, Scrapped Princess, Princess Nine, Princess Princess (egregious!), Magical Princess Minky Momo, Petite Princess Yucie, Sister Princess, Princess Tutu, UFO Princess Valkyrie)
    • This became common enough that ADV Films used the Market-Based Title of UFO Ultramaiden Valkyrie because retailers allegedly felt that anime with "Princess" in the title didn't sell well.
  • "Complex" (Ghost in the Shell: Stand Alone Complex, Cosplay Complex, Lovely Complex)
  • "Mon", "Monster" (especially in Mons series, naturally; Digimon, Pokémon, Monster Rancher, Mon Colle Knights, ect.)
  • "Dragon" (Dragon Ball, Dragon Drive, Dragon Half, Dragon Quest, Dragon Crisis!)
  • "Children" (Fantastic Children, Zettai Karen Children, Final Fantasy VII: Advent Children)
  • "Super"
  • "Zettai/absolute" (Zettai Karen Children, Zettai Hero Project, Zettai Shoujo Seiiki Amnesian, Absolute Boyfriend)
    • Advertisement:
  • "Gakuen/school/academy" (Animal Academy Hakobune Hakusho, Psychic Academy, Doki Doki School Hours, Gakuen Heaven)
  • "Strawberry" (Strawberry Marshmallow, Strawberry 100%, Strawberry Panic!)
  • "Mahou/Magical" (very common for Magical Girl shows)
  • "Project" (Touhou Project, Zettai Hero Project, Idol Project)
  • Random Roman alphabet letters (Dragon Ball Z, Sailor Moon R, Slayers: Evolution-R, Familiar of Zero F, Mega Man X, Yu-Gi-Oh! GX, lots of other Oddly Named Sequels)
  • "Samurai" (Samurai Flamenco, Samurai Champloo, Samurai 7)
  • "Bakuretsu" (Bakuretsu Hunter, Bakuretsu Tenshi)
  • "Psychic/Psycho" (Psychic Academy, Psychic Force, Psycho Busters, Psycho-Pass, Mob Psycho 100, Psycho Soldier)
  • "Guilty" or "Gear" (twofer with Guilty Gear, plus Guilty Crown, Metal Gear, Symphogear, Air Gear)
  • "Tenshi/Angel" (Angel Beats!, Angel Densetsu, Angel Heart, Angel Links, Angel Sanctuary, Angel's Egg, Angel Tales... those are just the titles that start with "Angel")
    • Advertisement:
  • "Black" (Black Butler, Black Bird, Black Cat, Black★Rock Shooter, Black Clover, Black Lagoon, Black Jack, Black Bullet, Black God)

Alternative Title(s): Mad Libs Anime Title
