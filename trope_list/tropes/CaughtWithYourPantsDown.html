Caught with Your Pants Down

"We'll just tell your mother that we...ate it all."

"While the wee wife's away, a word on the subject of onanism: in this house, we don't masturbate. It's not a very pleasant thing to do. One hates to imagine young Chloe or Radclyffe scampering down the stairs of a Saturday morning, only to find you, double-bent on the sofa, pumping your fist!"
— Harvey Denton, The League of Gentlemen
Advertisement:

A subtrope of Cringe Comedy. A character, thinking they are alone, decides to indulge in a little self-pleasuring only to be interrupted by their partner, parent, sibling, best friend, The Vicar (for maximum laughs), whatever. Seriously people, next time, just lock the door.

Usually Played for Laughs. Occasionally Played for Drama, except in Christian cinema, where masturbation, porn, and occasionally the internet frequently cause the primary conflict. Sometimes it may overlap with Mistaken for Masturbating. For when it is not a solo situation, see Interrupted Intimacy.

Contrast with I'll Be in My Bunk, when a character leaves the room in an awkward and obvious fashion which makes it clear that they are headed out for A Date with Rosie Palms.
