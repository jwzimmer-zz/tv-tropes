Obey the hypno boobies!

"It's a level eighty female-only persuasion spell. We try not to over-use it."
— Lilah, Ctrl+Alt+Del
Advertisement:

How do you get something you want from another person? Logical arguments, diplomacy, trading, bribery, force, or — if you're a woman trying to persuade a man — the mere fact of having tatas.

Yes, because writers think All Men Are Perverts, women don't even need to pretend that getting sex is a likelihood or even a possibility. All they have to do is possess secondary sexual characteristics to get men to do their bidding. We mean, They're Men, They Can't Help It, right?

Manipulative seduction, of course, can happen in real life, but is rarely (if ever) this straightforward. This is another one that's fallen somewhat out of favor in The New '10s. Partly because it plays into sexist stereotypes. Partly just because it no longer plays well outside of certain kinds of comedy. At one point a common gag was to have a woman try this on a male character who would turn out to be gay. This shows up even less often.

Advertisement:
