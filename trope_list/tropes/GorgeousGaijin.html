Bubblegum's not the only thing that's popping.
 So, this next character is an Eaglelander, and her design should reflect that. Okay, blonde hair...blue eyes...175 cm (5 ft 9 in) tall... huge cans...
Advertisement:

Hmm. Is there some Fanservice potential here?

This is when the answer to that question is "yes." In short, this is the stereotypical height and bust of Western (though not necessarily American) women in an East Asian setting/work who's played for Fanservice.

The male equivalent shows up in Shoujo, josei and yaoi, where if there's an American guy around, he will be hot, blond, and have a great bod to go with his freakish height and lack of social norms (like kissing people goodbye and being shirtless in public).

Related to Phenotype Stereotype. See also Anime Chinese Girl, Everyone Looks Sexier If French, Gorgeous Greek, Sexy Scandinavian, Sensual Slavs, Latin Lover, Hot Gypsy Woman, and Europeans Are Kinky. Compare and contrast Mukokuseki. Subtrope of Foreign Fanservice.

    Anime & Manga 
  • Assassination Classroom: Irina Jelavić is a busty blonde from Serbia, and she's used her looks to turn heads as much as snap them. The nickname she's given by her students, "Bitch-sensei", is even based on how sexy she naturally comes off to others.note "Bitch" in this case is a loanword that means "slut" colloquially, but eventually turned into an Insult of Endearment from Class E.
  • Campione!: Erica Blandelli is Italian, blonde and blue-eyed, and while she's not exactly very tall, she has a very curvaceous body. When she transfers to Godou's class, her immediate declaration of "having already promised her future" to Godou quickly draws the ire of his male classmates, thinking him a lucky bastard for scoring such a beautiful girl.
  • Darker Than Black:
    • November 11 is tall, blond, blue-eyed, British. He is seen lounging around naked in one episode and again in a manga omake.
    • Brita is a blonde, blue-eyed American. She lacks concern for nudity, though because of sociopathy.
    • Madame Oreille is blonde and presumably American because she works for the CIA. She seems to spend an inordinate amount of time in swimsuits that show off her cleavage or getting a massage while covered in oil.
  • Detective Conan:
    • Jodie Starling is an American teacher and FBI agent. Aside from suggestive outfit, she's quite flirty.
  • Di Gi Charat: In the specials, the cast travels to the US and meets up with a full-blown 10-year-old Dejiko-fanboy, complete with blonde hair, Japanese with a weird accent ("De'jeeko'-chan!") and stars & stripes-colored Dejiko-cosplay.
  • Digimon Adventure 02: Wallace/Willis from The Movie is a blonde American with blue eyes (though they change to green during the course of the movie). Lampshaded in the dubbed version where Daisuke/Davis comments, "Yeah, yeah, back off there, blondie", after Wallace/Willis complements Miyako/Yolei.
  • Grenadier: Everyone assumes that Rushuna is foreigner based on her golden hair, her gun, and her massive pair of boobs. The hair actually winds up getting her into trouble on one occasion.
  • High School D×D:
    • Subverted with Rias. Her tall height, large chest, and brilliant scarlet hair lead people to assume she's Scandinavian. Her homeland is actually in her last name, Gremory.
  • Kenichi: The Mightiest Disciple: Rachel "Castor" Stanley. On top of being scantily clad and mightily endowed, she's also proven to be a gigantic Attention Whore who would do practically anything (especially strip) for a glance in her direction.
  • Kimi wa Petto:
    • Emma has the bust, but is red-haired instead of blonde and is the same height as her Japanese co-workers.
    • Hugh is blond, extremely tall, and a Chick Magnet.
  • Kuroko no Basuke: Alex Garcia is tall, blonde, blue-eyed, and busty, with a propensity for hanging out naked while at home, as well as kissing other girls and kids. She's also a former WNBA player and taught Taiga while he was growing up in the US.
  • The Kurosagi Corpse Delivery Service: Reina Gorn is a Hot Scientist American entomologist who is blonde, has an enormous bust, and wanders round Tokyo wearing a cowboy hat, bikini top, and tiny denim shorts.
  • Love Hina: Subverted. Keitaro returns from an academic trip abroad. Naru (after spending much of his trip worried about all the girls that he was bound to encounter) finds a blond hair on his clothes and takes him to task for philandering. As it turns out, it was a hair from a mysteriously blonde gorilla.
  • Lucky Star: Patricia "Patty" Martin is an American and has the second largest breast size of the entire cast.
  • Lupin III: The Pursuit of Harimao's Treasure: Diana has striking pale blue eyes, a pronounced British accent, a tall, nubile figure, and a beautiful pair of legs. Naturally, Lupin can't help himself when he sees her and spends almost as much of the film trying to win her affections as he does searching for the fabled treasure.
  • Lyrical Nanoha: Alisa is American and officially gets the second largest bust size amongst Nanoha, Fate, Hayate, Suzuka, and her after the Time Skip.
  • Maicchingu Machiko-sensei: Judy from episode 23 is a friend of Machiko and a beautiful blonde American girl. She even perform a cheerleader show.
  • Midori Days: Lucy Winradd is the prerequisite Foreign Exchange Student who is joining Seiji's class. Seiji immediately recognizes her as an American because of her blonde hair and Stars and Stripes patterned panties.
  • Neon Genesis Evangelion:
    • Asuka Langley Sohryu is a German redhead, The Ace, and not ashamed to show off her good looks (well, to Kaji, any other man gets called a pervert and runs a risk of being pummeled). The original show has several moments to show her off in things like Daisy Dukes and the artbooks had a field day with her. The Rebuild movies (in which she was slightly renamed as "Asuka Langley Shikinami") has her wearing a "test plugsuit" that was even more fanservicey than her regular outfit in a couple of scenes.
    • Rebuild of Evangelion 2.0: Mari Illustrious Makinami is half-Japanese, half-British (although this is All There in the Manual material) and her primary sources of fan service are being the most stacked of the Evangelion pilots and having No Sense of Personal Space. She's not above shaming Asuka in the chest department either.
  • Omamori Himari: Lizlet L. Chelsie is English and has the biggest bust of the main cast. This frequently gets lampshaded by the other girls of Yuuto's harem.
  • RahXephon: Cathy McMahon is tall, blonde, busty, freckled, boisterous, wears a cowboy hat with her uniform. She wears a very skimpy bikini in the Beach Episode.
  • Variable Geo: Erina Goldsmith is the lone American entrant competing in the VG tournament. She's blonde and busty, and she and her team parade around in Playboy Bunny costumes. She also keeps a lighter tucked conspicuously in her ample cleavage, which she uses as a makeshift flamethrower when she fights.
  • Yakitate!! Japan: Monica is a blonde and blue-eyed American girl. She wears an American flag bikini to distract her opponent in the baking duel. Her opponent responds to this by stripping down to his loincloth.
  • Yuria 100 Shiki: Lucy Mk II is a blonde blue-eyed American sexbot, modeled after generations of playmates, who behaves like a nymphomaniac.

    Films — Live-Action 
  • The Ring: When it was announced that there would be a Hollywood version of the film, the Japanese made a few joke comics about "American Sadako". In one of the comics, she would be blonde and have big boobs. It eventually spawned a full-blown meme where Sadako, the black-haired child-Samara, and the blonde and large-breasted American Samara were all separate characters.

    Video Games 
  • Advanced Variable Geo: Erina "Erinrin" Goldsmith is a busty blonde American bombshell, who runs her own casino and works in a bunny costume.
  • Darkstalkers:
    • Morrigan is Scottish (named in fact for a Celtic deity) and is literally sex-appeal personified in her capacity as a succubus — a female demon believed to have sex with sleeping men.
    • Felicia is an American in a Stripperiffic outfit, with the largest bust and hips of the female cast.
  • Dead or Alive:
    • Tina Armstrong is a tall, blonde and very well-endowed American professional wrestler and has a cowgirl-themed costume in all the games.
    • Helena Douglas is tall, blonde, French and oozes sex appeal.
    • Hitomi is half-German, buxom and even gets a German barmaid costume.
    • Christie is a statuesque, platinum-haired English woman with gaping cleavage. Besides Kasumi's clone, she is the first un-hacked character who has appeared nude.
    • Mila, introduced from the fifth game, is a female boxer and MMA fighter from Spain who bears a striking resemblance to Gina Carano. She can kick your ass and has muscles in all the right places.
  • Fatal Fury / The King of Fighters:
    • All of the western women sport the largest busts; Shermie (French, 92 cm), Elizabeth (French, 90 cm), King (French and blonde, 86 cm), Angel (Mexican, 92 cm), and Blue Mary (American and blonde, 86 then 88 cm).
    • In KOF Maximum Impact 1, Lien Neville (British, blonde, busty) is given the largest in-game chest, but no official measurements are provided.
    • When first introduced, Blue Mary filled this role in Fatal Fury 3 and Real Bout 1 when her bust was more emphasized than Mai's. In Mark of the Wolves, without any of them present, B. Jennet (British and blonde) is both Ms. Fanservice and this trope.
    • The guys aren't any slouches either: Series Mascot Terry Bogard is blond, blue-eyed, and one of the biggest Hunks in the series. His Midnight Bliss form in the SNK vs. Capcom series and his female form in SNK Heroines: Tag Team Frenzy appropriately translate his beefy prettyboy looks to a busty, leggy girl in cutoffs.
  • Kantai Collection: Pretty much every non-Japanese ship that isn't a destroyer. We'll save some time and only go over a few.
    • Iowa is tall, blonde, busty, blue-eyed, speaks in (actually pretty poor) English, is very upbeat and outgoing, and wears rather a revealing American flag-based outfit. Her design is based on pin-up girls found in US recruitment and propaganda posters, as well as in war bond drives. Her design is something of a stand-out case, as since she was the first US Navy ship to be added into the game, the creators felt they had to go big. Other USN non-DD/DE ships would follow the trend of being tall, blue-eyed, blonde, busty, curvy, or any four of the five.
    • Warspite, the game's first Royal Navy ship, is blonde and attractive, and has long legs (though her chest is average-sized), and generally has the appearance and personality of a young queen. like Iowa, she also frequently uses English, though hers is much better, to the point of sounding like a native Englishwoman.
    • The Regia Marina ships, Littorio and Roma, are both tall, have light brown and black hair respectively, have large chests, and are decked out in Italian flag-themed colors.note Though the former loses this upon remodeling.
  • Neptunia: Being the CPU of the foreign nation Leanbox, Vert is, of course, tall, blue-eyed, blonde-haired, and openly proud of having the biggest rack of the cast.
  • Osu! Tatakae! Ouendan: Anna Lindhurst is American, the tallest of all six cheer girls, has blonde hair, and the largest breasts.
  • Overwatch: Tracer is a spunky young British girl with a thick accent and proudly has the Union Jack emblazoned on the left shoulder of her flight jacket. She's also got stewardess legs and wears a skintight yellow tracksuit, but what she's really become famous for is her butt. Official art and plentiful fan art repeatedly place a strong emphasis on her shapely hindquarters.
  • Persona 5: Deconstructed. While Ann is only a quarter-white, the game still puts emphasis on her foreign traits (blonde hair and blue eyes), as well as her ability to speak fluent English. She's the biggest Dude Magnet among the girls and the most comfortable with her sex appeal, owing to her work as an aspiring model. However, she gets an unhealthy amount of Slut-Shaming for her looks, she draws all the wrong kind of attention from the gym coach Kamoshida, and people in general think of her as a Brainless Beauty and an Alpha Bitch, when she's actually rather lonely.
  • Rival Schools: Tiffany Lords is a blonde, blue-eyed American girl with large bouncing breasts. She wears a revealing, Stars and Stripes patterned cheerleading outfit and bright red boxing gloves.
  • Samurai Shodown: Charlotte Christine de Colde is French, tall, blonde and definitely looks like the sexier female compared to the other, cuter female Nakoruru.
  • Soul Series: English aristocrat Ivy is arguably the most fanservicey character in her home series, and combines a dominatrix-style costume that shows off her enormous triple-G bust with an icy, no-nonsense (though undoubtedly sexy) Evil Brit personality. She's also far taller than any other woman in the series, standing at an amazonian height of just under 6 foot.
  • Street Fighter:
    • British-bombshell Cammy was the first non-Asian female in the seminal series, and features the blonde hair and blue eyes associated with this trope, as well as a selection of thong-leotard costumes that always emphasise her toned butt.
    • Rose is Italian and is designed to be the series' mature, sexy woman. She stands at a statuesque 5'10'', with long, toned legs, an impressive bust, and permanently pouty lips.
    • Crimson Viper is American and has the largest chest among any female in the series.
  • Tomb Raider: Lara Croft is a British aristocrat, and with her daisy-duke shorts and proportionately huge breasts, she became among the first video game icons to be accepted as a mainstream sex symbol.
  • Virtua Fighter:
    • Sarah Bryant is American and was designed to be the sexier, icier western foil to cute-as-a-button Pai.

    Visual Novels 
  • Katawa Shoujo: Lilly Satou is half-Scottish, the tallest of all the girls, and has wavy blonde hair. Hisao comments on her foreign features, notably her large bust, long legs, and height (she's as tall as him) as all being attractive to him. Unusually, she's a reserved Yamato Nadeshiko, although she surprisingly takes the lead in the bedroom, in fitting the stereotype of Westerners being more sexually aggressive.
  • Rondo Duo: The Gratuitous English-spouting Riko has blonde hair, green eyes, and the biggest, bounciest bust in the cast.
