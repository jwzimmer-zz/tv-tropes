Should Have Thought of That Before X

Go To

Edit Locked

  • Main
  • Create New

Principal Skinner: Over here, Simpson! The detention room is dangerously overcrowded so you'll be spending your time in the cafeteria.
Student: Oxygen running out...
Principal Skinner: Yes, you should have thought of that before you made that paper airplane.
— The Simpsons, "Treehouse of Horror V", "Nightmare Cafeteria"

So a particular situation, severe or trivial, could whether rightly or wrongly be perceived as character A's fault, by character A or by another character, based on point [X]. Cue a Stock Phrase of one of two forms:

Advertisement:

"I should have thought of that before [X]." if said by character A.

OR

"You should have thought of that before [X]." if said by another character.

Compare to Didn't Think This Through.

Examples:

    open/close all folders 

    Anime and Manga 
  • Rosario + Vampire Capu2: In episode 7, Kurumu, Mizore, and Yukari get involved in a massive fight over Tsukune, causing damage to the surrounding neighborhood and risking a Broken Masquerade, and eventually forcing Tsukune to unleash Inner Moka to forcibly break them up. The next morning, Ruby arrives in the bus to drag them back to school; when Kurumu furiously objects, not wanting to go back to school just yet, Ruby just throws her into the bus, snapping, "Well, you should've thought of that before causing such a big ruckus in the human world!"
  • Yu-Gi-Oh!: During the Battle City arc, Insector Hagaaka "Weevil Underwood" convinces a kid (who he later double-crosses) to sabotage Jonouchi's deck before dueling him, giving Haiga a pretty serious advantage. Jonouchi manages to win anyway, and when he demands Haiga's two locator cards and Insect Queen card, Haiga suddenly decides to plead for mercy. Jonouchi's reply is pretty much, "you should have thought of that before cheating, stupid."

    Fan Works 
  • Harry Potter and the Prince of Slytherin: When Fred and George protest about having to make peace as a condition to be allowed to attend the Quidditch World Cup, Percy says they should've thought about that before ruining the party their mother "had slaved to prepare for" them.

    Films — Animated 
  • The Emperor's New Groove
    Yzma: It is no concern of mine whether or not your family has...what was it again?
    Peasant: Umm...food?
    Yzma: Ha! You should have thought of that before you became peasants!
    • And another one of the more trivial variety:
    Guard: For the last time, we did NOT order a giant trampoline!
    Salesman: You know, pal, you could have told me that before I set it up.
  • American Pop
    Palumbo: Don't you think it's time for a wedding?
    Zalmie: How can I ask her? I don't have a cent.
    Palumbo: You should have thought of that before you got her pregnant.

    Films — Live-Action 
  • The Dark Knight
    Maroni: This craziness... it's too much.
    Gordon: You should've thought about that before you let the clown out of the box.
  • Beethoven Lives Upstairs
    Ludwig Van Beethoven: Where are the pens! I need a pen!
    Schindler: You should have thought of that before you destroyed the last one!
  • Universal Soldier: The Return: The Intrepid Reporter, regarding the titular Super Soldiers annihilating the Red Shirt Army.
    "Maybe you should have thought it up before you built the fucking things!"''
  • Tower Heist: Mr. Simon fires Josh, Charlie and Enrique after Josh smashes the windows of Shaw's car. When Josh protests that the others didn't do anything, Mr. Simon says he should have thought about that before destorying a resident's property.

    Live-Action TV 
  • House episode "Hunting"
    Kalvin: This is none of your business!
    House: Well, you should have thought of that before you stalked me. Now I'm interested.
  • Heroes episode "Chapter Eighteen 'Parasite'"
    Isaac Mendez: How am I supposed to pretend this didn't happen?
    Candice Wilmer: Maybe you should have thought about that before you shot her. Twice.
  • Star Trek: Deep Space Nine
At the climax of "For The Uniform," Sisko declares his intent to render every Maquis colony in the Cardassian Demilitarized Zone uninhabitable to humans, following the Maquis' attack on the starship Malinche and their own use of biogenic weapons to poison Cardassian colonies. When Eddington points out that this would turn thousands of people who'd done nothing wrong into homeless refugees, Sisko rather darkly retorts "You should've thought about that before you attacked a Federation starship!"
  • Ugly Betty episode "A Tree Grows in Guadalajara"
    Alexis: [Hates the fact that Daniel is seeing her ex-girlfriend]
    Daniel: You're just jealous because I've got the one thing you can't have. Guess you should have thought of that before you gave away the family jewels, huh?
  • Dollhouse episode "Omega"
    Caroline: Hey! That *is* my brain! I want my brain back! I want back in my brain!
    Alpha: You should have thought of that before you vacated the premises.
  • In one disturbing example from Law & Order: Special Victims Unit, a man is framed for raping a teenage girl, and is subsequently abused in prison. He pleads with the officers for protection but they just tell him, "You should have thought about the pecking order before you raped that girl." He ends up getting killed.
  • In the Babylon 5 episode "Moments of Transition", Delenn and Warrior Caste leader Shakiri are in the Starfire Wheel together, as a contest of whose cause was the more righteous, at the end of the Minbari civil war. Shakiri (the one who initiated the war) is not faring very well, and showing his true colors as a result:
    Shakiri: Delenn... we can walk out together. Share the power. No one will speak against us.
    Delenn: NO!
    Shakiri: There are other ways!
    Delenn: Then you should have explored them before you tore our people apart!

    Theatre 
  • From Gilbert and Sullivan's The Pirates of Penzance:
    Sergeant of Police: It is most distressing to us to be the agents whereby our erring fellow-creatures are deprived of that liberty which is so dear to us all— but we should have thought of that before we joined the force.
  • Heathers:
    Veronica: Heather Chandler was the only person at school who could actually control Kurt and Ram, and she is dead.
    Heather Chandler: Should have thought of that before you killed me!

    Web Original 
  • The Joker Blogs, when the Joker gatecrashes Harleen's wedding:
    Mr Quinzel: I just don't want to have anything happen to my daughter.
    The Joker:...Probably should've thought of that before you gave her away.

    Western Animation 
  • Justice League gives us a delicious sarcastic subversion of this trope:
    Clayface, to outnumbered Batman: You didn't think I'd come here without reinforcements, did ya?
    Batman, completely deadpan: Wish I'd thought of that. *cue the entire Justice League coming in* Oh Wait! - I did.
  • The Simpsons:
    • An example that sort of toys with this. In "Lost Our Lisa", Lisa can't go to the Isis Exhibit because Marge has to take Bart to the hospital to get the novelty items he glued to his face removed, leading to this exchange:
      Lisa: Oh! It's the last day of the Isis exhibit!
      Bart: Well, you should have thought of that BEFORE I glued all this stuff to my face!
    • In "Fear Of Flying", when Homer gets banned from Moe's Tavern after pranking Moe by unscrewing a lid to a jar of sugar.
      Homer: Aw, come on, everybody. This bar is like a tavern to me.
      Moe: Sorry, Homer, you should have thought of that before you gave me the old sugar-me-do.
    • Parodied in "Mobile Homer", when Marge watches a Lifetime Movie of the Week about a woman losing her money after the death of her husband because he didn't have insurance.
      Woman: But how will I feed my family?
      Insurance Agent: You should have thought of that before you married a dead man.
    • In "Politically Inept, with Homer Simpson", Homer is denied entry into an airplane's bathroom and complains.
      Stewardess: You should have thought of that before you drank the fluids you need to live.
    • "Bart to the Future" has a Native American Chief predicting Bart will become a Future Loser who needs to mooch for a living. Homer eventually responds to Bart's attempt to make him give money by saying he should have thought about that before dropping out of the DeVry Institute.
    • "Orange Is The New Yellow": When Marge asks who's going to watch the kids while she's under arrest, Chief Wiggum says she should have thought about that before he and other cops showed up without warning.
    • In "The Old Man And The Lisa", when Lisa explains recycling to Mr. Burns:
    Lisa: You've never heard of recycling? It means to reuse things, to conserve our natural resources.
    Burns: Oh, so Mother Nature needs a favor? Well, maybe she should have thought of that when she was besetting us with droughts and floods and poison monkeys. Nature started the fight for survival, and now, she wants to quit because she's losing? Well, I say "hard cheese!"
    • The page quote is from the "Treehouse Of Horror V" story "Nightmare Cafeteria". Springfield Elementary's detention gets so overcrowded, prompting the exchange above, that prompting Principal Skinner and the teachers to start eating the children in detention.
  • Teen Titans.
    Robber: [Bats come swarming out of the shadows] Who's there? I don't want any trouble.
    Robin: You should have thought about that before you committed the crime!
  • In the South Park two-parter "Cartman's Mom Is a Dirty Slut" when Lianne is talking about abortion:
    Lianne: I mean, what right do I have bringing another child into this overpopulated world? Then again, I should have thought of that before having sex...

    Real Life 
  • When someone complains about the severity of their punishment or even about being punished in the first place, the authority figure pronouncing the punishment or carrying out the punishment will tell them they should have thought of that before committing any wrongdoing (i.e. a parent punishing a child, a judge sentencing a convict and so on).
